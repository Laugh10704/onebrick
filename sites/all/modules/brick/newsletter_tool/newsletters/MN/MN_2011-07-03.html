Happy Fourth of July!

We at One Brick want to wish everyone a happy and safe 4th of July. Summer is finally here so get out and enjoy it! Fortunately we've got some fun outdoor events coming up including the Drum Corps International setup on Saturday, July 16th and the Aquatennial Sand Castle competition on Sunday, July 17th. Hope to see you there!
