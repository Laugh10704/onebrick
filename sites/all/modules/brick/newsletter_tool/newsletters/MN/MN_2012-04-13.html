News and Announcements

Join us in celebrating National Volunteer Week!
National Volunteer week is April 15-21 and we've got a huge week of events lined up starting early tonight with the Vocal Essence Brits and Brass concert!

National Volunteer week started in 1974 as a way to recognize and celbrate the efforts of volunteers. Over the years it has grown into a nation wide effort to encourage people to get out into their communities and volunteer.

We're very excited for National Volunteer Week this year. Starting tonight we've got events almost every day until Sunday the 21st so come help us make this the most successful National Volunteer Week ever! There's something for everyone from music with Vocal Essence and the Minnesota Orchestra's Family Concert to preparing book packages for women in prison. You can make baby clothes at Bundles of Love or sort supplies at ARC's Value Village and Global Health Ministries. You can pack food at Feed My Starving Children or help restore the Birch Island Woods Conservation Area. The possiblities are endless! (ok, maybe those are all the possibilities, but they're some pretty great possibilities!)

As a special treat, we'll be hosting a Beer Bust on Saturday to kick things off! This will be a great time to get to get together and build up some excitement for the week. Plus, we'll be giving out Presidential Service Awards to show our appreciation for all the great work people did in 2011. See below for more details.

Kick off National Volunteer Week with a Beer Bust
As mentioned above, we're having a beer bust tomorrow, April 14th at 7pm at Shamrock's in St. Paul. For $20 you get all the beer you can drink (Coors Light, Summit EPA and Finnegan's) and live music by Free and Easy. Just tell them you're there with One Brick and you'll get in for no cover.

It's going to be a great time and we'll be giving out the President's Service Awards for 2011!

So come out, have fun, kick off National Volunteer Week with a bang and help support One Brick. You can't get a better deal than that!
