News and Announcements

Thank you!
I want to thank everyone for their incredibly generous support. So far we've raised over $18,000 to cover the shortfall from the deaulted predged donation. Unfortunately, we still haven't reached our $40,000 goal. Can you spare $2?

While One Brick operartes on a shoe string budget, we do have expenses. We don't charge our volunteers a membership fee and we don't charge the non-profits we support. If One Brick has made a positive impact in your life, please consider contributing to keep us going through this short term crisis while we continue to build on our long term sustainability plan.

Even if you can't contribute, take a look at this here's a dog playiing with a baby chicken as a thank you for reading this far and considering us.

Happy St. Patty's Day!
If you're still looking for something to do this St. Patty's day, we've got some great events lines up! On Saturday, we're going to go organize the St. Paul St. Patrick's day Parade and walk with the people in it!

Then on Sunday we'll be helping the Irish Music and Dance Association with admittance to their Day of Irish Dance over at Landmark Center. It's going to be a lot of fun, plus you get free admission to all of thier St. Pat's festivities!

Have a fun and safe St. Pattrick's Day everyone, hope to see you out there!
