Our Volunteers at Work

EM of the Month: Jill Pravatiner

Continuing our introduction of the people who help run One Brick, this month we're shining the spotlight on Jill Pravatiner.

Jill has been One Bricking since December of '09 when she helped usher at the VocalEssence holiday concert. She found out about One Brick when she was volunteering at CivicFest and met some One Bricker's there. She thought it sounded like an awesome way to do more volunteer work than what she was doing at the time.

After volunteering for a couple months Jill decided that she really liked one brick and wanted to get more involved with the group so in the January of 2010 she became an EC and followed that up by becoming an EM that same July. She sticks with it because "it's so ridiculously easy to find events that work for your schedule, get a chance to work with amazing people, and help relieve some of the stress on non-profits." 

Jill's favorite One Brick story is from helping out at the Skyway Golf Tournament this year. "We had the chance to talk to the person who designed our hole to find out more about it and then got to tell the judges all about the hole - and it won!  The designers were super excited and so were we."

When Jill's not One Bricking she volunteers with some of her other favorite non-profits, hangs out with friends, quilts, scrapbooks, and tries to make her way through her gigantic "to be read" pile of books.

So next time you see Jill at an event, say hi and ask her what book she's reading. You'll definitely be able to find her at Race up the Place next February. "Any event that involves 7 a.m., cowbells and pom poms, and really nice participants is pretty great in my book."
