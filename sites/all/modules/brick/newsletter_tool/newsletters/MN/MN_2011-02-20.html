News and Announcements

Welcome to the new Newsletter!

After a lot of thought, we'd like to introduce the new format for our newsletter. We feel this has a much smoother look and we're looking to try out some new things with it. All of the One Brick chapters throughout the country are moving to this format so there might be a bit of tweaking over the next few weeks as we finalize everything. We hope you like this as much as we do! If you have any questions or comments please send them to us.

Upcoming Events

One Brick Committees want you!!

Are you interested in how One Brick works behind-the-scenes?

Do you want to develop your skills, build your resume, and contribute your abilities to One Brick?

Would you like to get to know other One Brickers who are working to make all our volunteer opportunities happen?

If you answered YES to any of these questions, we would like to invite you to attend our Leadership Team Info Session/Meet & Greet event this Wednesday, February 23rd at Joe Senser's in Roseville!!  The event goes from 6:00-7:30 and we will be meeting in their party room for drinks and discussion!  There are leadership roles available, or you can simply join the committee that interests you most and bring in some fresh ideas.

Please RSVP if you are interested:

The following committees are currently in search of some bright minds:

  • Event Coordinators - We are always looking for fun, outgoing people who want to take that extra step and help run our volunteer events.  Your main responsibilities would include taking photos, planning the after-event happy hour, and making sure everyone is having fun!  Pretty easy stuff and it looks great on a resume.
  • Events – Help find events for our calendar! The Events committee works with local non-profits to develop events for One Brick, cultivates current relationships with current non-profits, and seeks out new partnerships. We are always looking for new volunteer opportunities and additional non-profits to work with!
  • Newsletter – Help write this newsletter! Anyone with writing skills, HTML knowledge, or just some generally good ideas is encouraged to contribute.
  • Social - Do you enjoy planning parties and happy hours?!  We are looking for help with planning some great upcoming social events for One Brick - concert events in the summer, volunteer appreciation events, happy hours, etc!
  • Marketing – Calling all social networking fiends! The Marketing committee manages One Brick’s social network media – Facebook, Twitter, blog, etc. – and provides additional marketing support (like ads and flyers) for special events.
  • Funds – Do you like to raise money? How about throwing parties? The Funds team plans and executes special events throughout the year to raise money for the organization, for example, Bowling Fundraisers, Trivia Nights, Wine Tasting events, St. Paul Saints Games and more! This committee also develops corporate sponsorships and other partnership opportunities.
