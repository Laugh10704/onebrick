Annual Survey Results
We've completed tallying up the results from our Annual Survey and wanted to share some of the highlights with you. With over 1000 people responding:

  • 80% plan to volunteer again
We are humbled by your encouragement and are re-doubling our efforts to make everyone feel welcome and comfortable as that was the only major area of concern.

Our Volunteers at Work

EC of the Month: Trisha Pederson
Continuing our introduction of the people who help run One Brick, this month we're shining the spotlight on Trisha Pederson

Trisha was heavily involved with Finnegan's and learned of One Brick through some of the events we've worked with them. When the Finnegan's Lasses retired she had some time on her hands and thought she'd give One Brick a shot. Trisha is our newest Event Coordinator and has been with us since she met with our chapter director Amie in September. While she's only been out to a couple events so far, she's been a big help behind the scenes such as organizing last Saturday's beer bust a Shamrock's.

Trisha chose One Brick because it "provides a unique way for people to volunteer without training or commitment and also allows for social interaction that people enjoy. Other organizations require much more time and most people are afraid to get locked in but One Brick lets you do as much or as little as you are comfortable with."

By day, Trisha works in the radiology department at the U. She loves spending time with family and friends and stays active going to sporting events, concerts, festivals, game nights, theater and no cover charge dance venues.

So next time you see Trisha at an event, say hi and ask her to tell you a fun story about some of the shenanigan's the Finnegan's Lasses got up to.
