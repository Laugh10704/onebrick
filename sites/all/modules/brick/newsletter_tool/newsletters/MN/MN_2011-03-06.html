New Schedule

With the beginning of March we are making one more change to the newsletter. To make sure we can fill the newsletter with interesting content and to keep from overwheling our readers we're going to start sending out the newsletter on the frist and third Sundays of the month.  Hopefully this new schedule will work out even better for everyone!

Upcoming Events

Join us for our St. Patty's Bowling Tournament!

It's time to celebrate the spirit of the Irish with a St. Patty's themed One Brick Bowling Tournament! Next Sunday, March 13th at 4 we will be meeting at Park Tavern in St. Louis Park.  For just $10 you will get 2 hours of bowling with some of the coolest people around.There will be a sweet prize for the highest score and some other prizes for things like most Irish! So come out, wear some green and have fun! Bowling always fills up fast so sign up today!
