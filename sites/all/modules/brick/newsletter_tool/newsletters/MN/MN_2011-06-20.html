Our Volunteers at Work

EM of the Month: Paul Jeyaraj

Continuing our introduction of the people who help run One Brick, this month we're shining the spotlight on Paul Jeyaraj.

Paul started with One Brick at a happy hour in March of 2008 just after the Minneapolis chapter started and has been one of our most active members averaging almost 3 events a month since then. He found One Brick doing a google search for volunteering and gave it a shot because it didn't require a commitment, there were lots of fun events on the calendar and he liked the social aspect.

He decided to become an event coordinator in May of 2009 because he wanted to give back and become more involved with the organization. Just this last April he stepped up to become an event manager.

Paul spends his days as an IT consultant. In his free time he plays on a kickball team, enjoys outdoor sports, going to concerts and traveling. His favorite event is the U.S. Bank sky way golf tournament. So next time you see him at an event, say hi!
