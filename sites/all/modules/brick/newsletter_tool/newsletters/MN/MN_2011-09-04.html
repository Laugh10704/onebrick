September 4th, 2011  

News and Announcements

Fall is Coming
Happy Labor Day everybody! Now that the craziness of summer is starting to wind down we're really excited about all the great events we've go coming up this fall. We'll be helping out with some races, helping set up Halloween displays at ARC, as well as all your old favorites. Best of all, we'll be going back to Global Health Ministries after our summer hiatus!

This October we're also going back down to New Orleans for a week of clean up and reconstruction. 6 years after Katrina there's still a lot of work to do down there with 10,000 families still struggling to rebuild.

Enjoy your last couple days of summer (even if it doesn't feel like it) and come back energized to continue all the great work you've been doing. We'll look forward to seeing you at an event and hearing about the great adventures you've had.

Twin Cities Helping Hands Lead the Nation
A recent Star Tribune article just rated the Twin Cities as having the most volunteers per capita of anywhere in the country and you all are helping contribute to that. You guys rock!

Event Recaps

Happy Hour and Saints Game
You guys all did a fantastic job with both our joint happy hour with Bundles of Love and at the Saints Game last month. We managed to raise $143 and took in hundreds of diapers, dozens of articles of clothing and several other assorted baby supplies. Your generosity is astounding and I hope you all had as much fun hanging out as I did.

We also had a fantastic turnout for the Saints Game. We may be done with these for the year, but we look forward to them again next year!
