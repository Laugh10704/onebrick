CitySolve Urban Race
Ready for something different this summer? Forget your errand-running and postpone your pub crawl; it's time to mix up your routine with CitySolve Urban Race.

One Brick is partnering with City Solve, and with our unique promo code (ONEBRICKMN), you can register for just $25 per person! (It’s normally $50, so this is a Groupon-sized deal!)

CitySolve is a unique urban adventure that takes a lot of brain, a bit of brawn, and a ton of fun! More than a scavenger hunt, but less physical than a 5k-road race, this unique hybrid combines trivia, from pop culture to world history, with a team-oriented adventure. Add in the occasional checkpoint challenge (think Double Dare without the slime) and you've got CitySolve Urban Race!

All you have to do is go to: https://2012minneapolisurbanrace.eventbrite.com/ and use our PROMO CODE: ONEBRICKMN. And to make things even better, a portion of each registration goes to benefit One Brick!

The event will be on Saturday, June 9th. For more information visit
www.citysolveurbanrace.com. Teams must be made up of two or more people. So what are you waiting for? Register your team today!
