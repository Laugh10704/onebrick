News and Announcements

Winter's Coming...
The weather is starting to turn colder out there. Now that the bustle of warmer weather is starting to wind down, check out some of the great event's we've got coming up! There's something for everyone, whether you want to learn/do some knitting, be an Elf for ARC, encourage some runners, host a party, or send out some holiday cards to children fighting diseases.

If that wasn't enough, we're throwing a big party a week from Saturday (November 19th) out at Shamrock's! There'll be a beer bust, wine tasting and some great people to hang out with. We'll look forward to seeing you there!
