Come out to a Saints Game with One Brick!

Summer is finally here! Come celebrate by watching  the Saints play the Wichita Wingnuts with One Brick on Friday, June 24th at Midway Stadium. Go to SaintsGroup.com and use the password ONEBRICK or call (651) 644-6659 to order your tickets.

For only $15 you get:

  1. A general admission ticket
  2. A hot dog
  3. A soda
  4. A Saints baseball cap
  5. Post-game fireworks!

Best of all, a portion of the proceeds go to benefit One Brick!

See you there!
