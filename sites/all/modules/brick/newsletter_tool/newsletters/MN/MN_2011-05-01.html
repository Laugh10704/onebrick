Event Recaps

Spill the wine was a huge success!

I was going to use this news letter to start getting excited for the great events we're lining up for the summer, but this weather has made me too sad. Instead, I'm going to look back to happier times:

Thank you to everyone who made it out to Spill the Wine for our happy hour with Drinks for a Cause. It was a fantastic night filled with fun people, tasty food and good wine. Over 50 people came out to spend the evening with us and with 20% of everyone's tab going to One Brick we raised $322.40 which maxed out our match of $200. With the match our grand total for the evening was $522.40! You guys rock!!

The night was such a huge success we'll definitely be doing another one later this year. I'll look forward to seeing you there!
