Meet our new partner, OutstandingDining.com

Meet our new partner: OutstandingDining.com! Outstanding Dining is a brand new online gift certificate venue that believes in supporting local restaurants, benefiting diners and giving back to the community. From start to finish, they are dedicated to offering only the best dining experiences with irresistible discounts. By carefully screening restaurants before featuring them, they take the risk out of trying somewhere new. By donating 10% off all sales to local nonprofits they take care of the community! The remaining portion of the sale is split between the restaurant and Outstanding Dining.com - 50/50. This system is intended to network with grass roots businesses; every day consumers; local charities and the individuals who depend on them - and give back to all of them. The reason we do this is simple: We believe a company is only as valuable as what they give to the community.

This week 10% of all sales will go to One Brick. So take a look at their list of great restraunts, get yourself a good deal and help support One Brick!

Check out Project Success

Do you like theater? Would you like to help expose middle and high schoolers to theater? Would you like to see a free show? Then check out Project Success! Project SUCCESS is a youth-development organization that works with students over a seven-year period, from middle school through high school, to help them develop life-skills that can transform their lives. Through in-school goal-setting workshops, theater experiences, and special programming/services, students learn to make informed choices and plan for meaningful futures.

Project SUCCESS promises all of its students the opportunity to attend live theater, whether they have transportation or not.  Volunteer drivers play a critical role in allowing us to fulfill this promise. 

Volunteer drivers pick up students and families at their homes and drop them off after the show.  In between, volunteers join with Project SUCCESS families to experience an evening of theater together.

One Bricker's who've been doing this program for years and they rave about it. Check it out for yourself!
