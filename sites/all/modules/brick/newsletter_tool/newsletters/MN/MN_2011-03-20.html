GoodSearch

Want an easy way to support One Brick? Use GoodSearch! GoodSearch will donate money to One Brick every time you use it to search the internet. Just go to www.goodsearch.com, type in "One Brick" under Who do you goodsearch for? (note: it will show SF, not MPLS!), scroll to the bottom of the page and download the toolbar...then SEARCH!!  :)  You might have to close the window and open it up again for the toolbar to appear.  It's super easy and you can watch our progress!
