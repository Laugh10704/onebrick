Join us for Climb for a Cure!

Join us on Saturday, February 4th to support the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation's Climb for a Cure. This will be a very special event for One Brick. Not only are we going to be volunteering to register people and help manage the race, we'll also have a team climbing!

Cystic fibrosis (CF) is a life shortening, genetic disease affecting approximately 30,000 children and young adults in the US. A defective gene causes the body to produce an abnormally thick, sticky substance in the lungs and digestive system that hinders the body’s ability to breathe and absorb nutrients. Currently, there is no cure.  The Cystic Fibrosis Foundation is aggressively pursing a cure for CF by efficiently investing in cutting edge, life saving science.

So come help us support this great cause in any way you can, either by volunteering, climbing or donating. 1280 steps. 50 floors. 1 cure.
