News and Announcements

Happy New Year!
2011 was a fantastic year for One Brick. We added 3 new chapters in Silicone Valley, Detroit and Los Angeles. We ran over 1200 events totaling more than 50,000 hours of community service. We were able to do all of this because of volunteers like YOU! You are fantastic and we can't thank you enough for your support.

My new year's resolution is to do at least one event per month in 2012 and to try out some new events to broaden my horizons and meet new people. If one of your resolutions was to give back to your community, get more involved in your community or just meet some new people, go to our event calendar and check out all of our great opportunities!

We hope you had a fun and safe new years and look forward to seeing you in 2012. Here's to another record breaking year for One Brick!
