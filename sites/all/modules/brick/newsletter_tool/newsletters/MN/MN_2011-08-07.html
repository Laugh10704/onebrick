News and Announcements

Joint Happy Hour at Spill the Wine

On Thursday, August 18th Drinks for a Cause will be putting on a joint Happy Hour with Bundles of Love at Spill the Wine in Minneapolis. The last happy hour we had in April was a huge success. A whole bunch of people made it out and everyone had a great time so we're really excited to be doing this again. Like last time, 20% of your bar tab will be donated by Spill the Wine and the proceeds will be split 50/50 between One Brick and Bundles of Love.

Bundles of Love is one of our favorite charities here at One Brick and we help them make baby clothes every month. They are a local non-profit that makes baby clothing and bedding for sick and needy newborn and premature babies.  They provide these items in a “bundle” to local hospitals, women’s shelters and other social service agencies, where the social workers give them to the babies who need them most.
One Brick is partnering with Bundles of Love for this happy hour because on Saturday, July 17th the Bundles of Love warehouse was flooded with over 2 feet of muddy water from the severe rains that hit New Brighton.  Fortunately they found out about it quickly and were able to save most of the clothing by washing it before the stains could set in, but almost all of their diapers, including a large shipment from Target that had just come in, were ruined. We're really happy to help them raise some money to help recover from this disaster. Unfortunately they've spent most of the month trying to clean things up from the flood and have fallen behind on their commitments to their partners so if you'd like to help them directly they could most use fitted crib sheets, gowns, hats, sleepers, preemie diapers, pacifiers and socks. We'll have a bin to collect anything people would like to bring.

So tell your friends, RSVP and get a little more info on the event!
