Paint the Town!
 
One Brick is excited to be partnering with City Year on March 31 to help paint the McClellan school  in Bronzeville. 
 
McClellan Elementary serves a student body of pre-K through 8th grade, and the school has been instrumental in developing a curriculum and providing a supporting environment for identified autistic students.
 
One Brick volunteers will join dozens of other City Year volunteers to help create a more inviting educational atmosphere for McClellan students by painting inspirational and motivational quotes as well as vibrant murals to liven up the building.
 
Help paint a smile on the faces of McClellan’s students!
