Red Rover, Red Rover, Send One Brick Right Over!

Come Help Reclaim the Skinner North Elementary Playground!

This weekend One Brick will be helping the Friends of Skinner North (FoSN, the parent-teacher organization) reduce the school's carbon footprint by planting special shade trees on the south side of the school. This will lower energy costs, make the classrooms (no AC) less uncomfortable for the kids, provide homes for birds/wildlife, and beautify what has been a very poor neighborhood.  FoSN has donated Fastagiatia trees and supplies, and One Brick will provide the volunteers!

Plan to get dirty - this event is rain or shine!  Though supplies will be available, feel free to bring your own work gloves, dandelion diggers, or other appropriate tools.

In addition to planting trees, volunteers will also weed and clean up broken glass.  Ultimately, we want to help reclaim the grass areas around the school as safe playgrounds!

Time to put on your party pants and have a ball!

Our good friends at Friends of the Parks need our help to make their Annual "Parks Ball" a success

The Parks Ball honors those in Chicago who have supported Friends of the Park's mission.  The gala will feature cocktails, entertainment, dinner and silent auction.  Support of the Parks Ball will enable FOTP to create a new children’s playground in the Chatham neighborhood in memory of slain police officer Thomas Wortham IV, and to take steps to implement The Last Four Miles Plan by acquiring 100 acres of land for a new lakefront park.

One Brick will be helping out with guest check-in, auction help, cashiering, auction check-out and more!
