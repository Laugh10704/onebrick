Summertime Is Here!

Summertime
Summertime
Sum...Sum... Summertime!
 
 
Happy Summer, One Brickers!  Although June 20 is the “official” start of summer (solstice, schmolstice…), we Chicagoans know that when the sidewalks are crowded with restaurants' outdoor seating, Moody’s pub opens its beer garden, and we start planning our commutes according to street festival road closures – summer is here.  (The hot Memorial Day weekend weather didn’t hurt either.)
 
While there are outdoor movies to see, neighborhood street festivals to visit, and Chicago 1st-place-in-American-League-Central White Sox games to see, our non-profit partners are as busy as ever.  We need your help! 
 
So, come on out, volunteer, make a new friend, and have some Summertime fun!  Paul Konerko promises he’ll hit his next home run - just for you - if you volunteer with One Brick in June.**
 
**Paul Konerko has no knowledge of this promise, but if he did, we bet he would totally agree to it because he's just that awesome.

Calling all Gleeks, Band Geeks, and Rock Stars!

The Intonation Workshop presents the 3rd Annual Stars of Tomorrow Youth Music Fest!  This day-long indoor/outdoor musical extravaganza is perfect for the whole family and features a stellar line-up of 15 young pop groups, sets from Future Hits and Psalm One, and lots of great activities for all who attend.
One Brick volunteers will help with final setup activities and then staff the first couple hours of the festival!  Festival duties may include:
  • Style Zone - Face painting, temporary tattoos, and helping band members with their rock star hair styling!
  • Instrument Petting Zoo - Staff the area where kids can try out instruments!
  • Front Gate -  Greet festival goers, collect tickets, and direct band members!
  • Band Hospitality - Staff the backstage green room for student bands!

Passport To Chicago

Passport To Chicago:  Explore Chicago,
Save Some Cash, Party AND Help One Brick!


Over the last ten years The Local Tourist has grown from a business directory of River North into a comprehensive resource that covers the neighborhoods of Chicago and beyond. To celebrate a decade online, The Local Tourist is inviting visitors and residents to tour Chicago like a Local Tourist with “Passport To Chicago”.
 
On June 3 you’ll enjoy exclusive events and deals at destinations all over Chicago, getting their pages stamped at each place you visit.  Each Passport comes complete with a CTA day pass and one free ride from Uber, the new car service. Those who purchase their passports in advance will also get an “I’m A Local Tourist” sling bag for free.  After exploring Chicago on June 3, enjoy exclusive access to the Official After Party at Public House.
 
Because there are so many places to go and things to do that there's no way to do them all in one day. That's why the Passport To Chicago is good for a whole year! From June 4, 2012 to May 31, 2013, these businesses are offering even more special discounts.
 
Here’s the BEST part - All of this exploring is also for a good cause.  The Local Tourist is donating a portion of every Passport to participating local charities and One Brick is one of the beneficiaries!
 
What is the price of this awesome year o’ local walk-abouting?  Only $35!  For more information and to get your Passport - visit http://passporttochicago.com.
