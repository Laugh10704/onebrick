One Brick's Fundraising Campaign Begins!

Support One Brick by Making a Donation!

This year, we will provide over 50,000 hours of help to hundreds of community organizations. We would not be able do it without your support. Thank you!

One Brick is a very efficient organization - our annual budget is just $150,000. We work hard to make sure your donation has the maximum impact possible. For example:

  • a $25 donation can equip and train an Event Coordinator or Event Manager
  • a $100 donation can support a chapter-level staff meeting
  • a $250 donation can pay for insurance protection for one chapter for a year
  • a $1000 donation can fund a new chapter's launch and all expenses for the first year

Please consider setting up a recurring donation. Your monthly gift of $10, $25 or $50 would provide a dependable revenue stream and allow us to focus more on creating volunteer events and less on fundraising.

To donate, link here:  http://chicago.onebrick.org/donate/

 

Holiday Season + One Brick = Awesome!
The Holidays Are Here - Get Ready to Volunteer!

After your turkey (or tofukey) on Thursday, rest up because One Brick's volunteering calendar is jammed pack with fun volunteer opportunities.  

For the week of November 28, our friends at Girls on the Run and Chicago Foundation for Education need your help with office tasks and mailings.  These after-work volunteering events are always fun and a good way to get to know One Brick with minimal commitment.

Later in the week, the Night Ministry will need your help packing hygiene kits and condom kits.  On Saturday, join us at the Greater Chicago Food Depository to help pack and sort food items to help Chicago's hungry.

As December continues, look out for lots of opportunities for holiday events like the Lincoln Park Zoo Lights Festival, ushering "A Christmas Carol," stuffing stockings for the homeless, and wrapping presents for kids in need.  

Have a wonderful Thanksgiving, everyone!



Happy Birthday, One Brick Chicago!
You Say It's Your Birthday....


On December 4, One Brick Chicago will turn seven years old!  Woo hoo!

Over the years, One Brick has helped hundreds of organizations in the Chicagoland area and donated thousands of hours of service.  It could not be done without you, our volunteers.  Thank you!

A special birthday shout out to all of our Event Coordinators and Event Managers, past and present.  These volunteers lead our events.  Without them, One Brick would not be able to help as many organizations that we do.  And without them, we wouldn't be celebrating our 7th birthday.  Thank you, EC/EMs! 

And everyone, happy birthday to us!
