Shuffle Up and (Learn How To) Deal!
Have you looked at the One Brick calendar longingly when a charity poker tournament is featured but decide you can't volunteer because you don't have training?  Have you played Texas Hold 'Em but do not feel confident in dealing at the various charity poker events One Brick has been asked to deal?  Have you just watched the 1998 Clive Owen magnum opus “Croupier” and decided that’s the life for you?

Well, have we got a deal for you! (pun intended)

On February 24, One Brick once again will be helping Horizons for Youth at its annual Texas Hold 'Em Poker Tournament. 

To get more volunteers involved, One Brick's Elite Dealer Squad (trademark pending) will be holding a training on February 16 after work. 

Come on out, socialize, learn the rules of Texas Hold 'Em, and deal a few hands.  Participants will be ready to help Horizons for Youth and other charity poker tournaments that One Brick assists throughout the year!
