Upcoming Events

Support Terrific Teachers with CFE!
Help the Chicago Foundation for Education prepare for its 23rd Annual Teachers as Leaders and Learners Workshop! The workshop, geared towards PreK-8th grade teachers, will feature 60 sessions on grant winning curricular projects and teaching strategies, a keynote address, Grant Writing 101, amd MORE!

One Brickers have been asked to help stuff 500 goody bags with tons of fun items for the teachers and to set-up tables/chairs and posting welcome/directional signs throughout the school.



Bonus for One Brickers and friends of One Brick! If you are a teacher, know any teachers, etc., pre-register to attend Saturday's workshop for the reduced rate of $15 ($5 off). Spread the word (and code.. OBGROUP)... this is an awesome event! For more information, visit http://www.CFEgrants.org.
