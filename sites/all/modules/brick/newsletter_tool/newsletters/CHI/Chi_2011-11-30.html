Join us for the Annual One Brick Holiday Hour!

Get the Mistletoe Ready! Jingle Those Bells!  Put on Your Party Pants!  

It's the Annual One Brick Holiday Happy Hour Extravaganza!

It's all the things you love about One Brick and the holidays rolled into one!  Ugly sweater contest!  Goody bag stuffing for Inspiration Cafe!  A Bake Sale!  And, of course, boozin' and eatin' and socializin'!

Join us for our end-of-the-year, festive cavalcade of holiday joy... the One Brick Annual Holiday Happy Hour Extravaganza!  Celebrate the holidays!  Catch up with your friends!  Meet new ones!  (New to One Brick?  What better way to find out more about us?)  Show off your ugly holiday sweater and have a chance to win prizes for your pain!

 

In addition to our usual flotilla of fun, this year, we're even going to put you to work (if you want) by making party favors/goody bags for the Inspiration Cafe holiday parties.  We also are going to be holding a bake sale (details below) - with all proceeds to benefit One Brick's Annual Campaign. 

 

What is the price of admission for this armada of merriment?  Just $10! 

We can't wait to see you there!  RSVP here:  http://chicago.onebrick.org/event/?eventid=10345

Busy Busy One Brick
The Volunteering Spirit of One Brick Is In Full Swing!

Wow!  Our calendar has been busy lately, and you have answered the call!  Check out the photos on our website from last week's Inspiration Cafe event, led by Oscar and Beth.  As usual, One Brickers provided a yummy and wholesome meal.  And look at all the fun volunteers had at the Hull House Coat Distribution project, led by Amy and Annette. 

As December continues, look out for more  opportunities for volunteering.  Remember, if an event is filled up, email the Event Manager directly and he/she will put you on a waiting list.  Often, spaces open up last minute.  

Have a great week, One Brick Chicago!  And remember to sign up for our Holiday Happy Hour on December 15!!
