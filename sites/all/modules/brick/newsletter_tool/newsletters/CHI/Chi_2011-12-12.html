Join us for the Annual One Brick Holiday Hour!

It's Almost Here!  Put on Your Party Pants!  

It's the Annual One Brick Holiday Happy Hour Extravaganza!

It's all the things you love about One Brick and the holidays rolled into one!  Ugly sweater contest!  Goody bag stuffing for Inspiration Cafe!  A Bake Sale!  Food Drive!  And, of course, boozin' and eatin' and socializin'!

Join us for our end-of-the-year, festive cavalcade of holiday joy... the One Brick Annual Holiday Happy Hour Extravaganza!  Celebrate the holidays!  Catch up with your friends!  Meet new ones!  Show off your ugly holiday sweater and have a chance to win prizes for your pain!

 

In addition to our usual flotilla of fun, this year, we're even going to put you to work (if you want) by making party favors/goody bags for the Inspiration Cafe holiday parties.  We also are going to be holding a bake sale - with all proceeds to benefit One Brick's Annual Campaign. 

AND we will be hosting a food/hygiene items drive for our friends at Care For Real.  We will be collecting donations of pasta, canned goods and personal hygiene items.  If you bring a donation for Care For Real, you will get your own super-cool thank you gift. 


What is the price of admission for this armada of merriment?  Just $10! 

We can't wait to see you there!  RSVP here:  http://chicago.onebrick.org/event/?eventid=10345

This Week in Volunteering

Howdy-ho, gentle readers!  This week is a busy one again - but we still have spaces for a few events. 

Tuesday, December 13 we will be at Safe, Humane Chicago.  Join Polly H, maker of yummy cinnamon cookies, and Tiffany F, sewer of lovely handbags, to help the humans that help awesome pooches.  RSVP here:  http://chicago.onebrick.org/event/?eventid=10341

On Saturday, December 17, we will be helping the kids of CircEsteem put on their Winter Circus!  You don't need to know how to juggle, but maybe the kids will teach you a few tricks!  RSVP here:  http://chicago.onebrick.org/event/?eventid=10340

Remember, if an event is filled up, email the Event Manager directly and he/she will put you on a waiting list.  Often, spaces open up last minute.  

Have a great week, One Brick Chicago!  See you at the Holiday Happy Hour on Thursday, December 15!!
