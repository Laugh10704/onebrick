MS Dinner of Champions!
MS Dinner of Champions was created in 1979 by Frank Gifford and Howard Cosell as an annual fundraising gala to benefit the National Multiple Sclerosis Society, Greater Illinois Chapter. This year's event enjoys strong support from Chicago’s corporate community and the friends of the Greater Illinois Chapter and is expected to raise more than $1,000,000 to support research, programs, and services for the 20,000 families in Illinois affected by MS.

One Brick Volunteers will be helping out in different areas to ensure the event run smoothly. For example roles will consist of greeters and directional assistants.

Semi-formal attire (colors: black, white, orange) or business attire (pant suit, jacket & tie or skirt & blouse) will be required for this event.  Shoes should be comfortable and appropriate for day & dress.  No gym shoes or jeans!

Champion among Champions, RSVP HERE!

Help out at Festiv-Ale for the CFF!
The 5th annual Festiv-Ale is a Celebration of Beer fundraiser for the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation's Greater Illinois Chapter.  Attendees will enjoy high-end, hand-crafted microbrew samples, Signature dish samplings from local restaurants, and entertainment!  There's also a VIP reception, silent auction, raffles, and more!

One Brick Volunteers will be helping with registration, the walk-up raffle & t-shirt sales, potentially pouring beer and being an aid to the brewers, and keeping the overall event tidy for guests.



Fired-up about the Festiv-Ale, RSVP HERE!
