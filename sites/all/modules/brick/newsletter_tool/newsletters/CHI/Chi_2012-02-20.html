Alright, we may have fudged on the words a little bit, but before Chicago can continue its quest for its next "Superbowl Shuffle" - there's a whole running season to get through!

The annual Shamrock Shuffle begins the running season in Chicago, and One Brick is going to help the Shuffle with preparations.  We'll be doing runner packet prep:  programming running chips, sorting bibs, stuffing packets.  With 40,000 runners registered, we are going to be busy!  For your hard work, we will promise you a good time, a chance to meet new friends, and pizza at the end of the shift, courtesy of Bank of America!

Inspire and Empower a Girl Today!
One Brick is very excited to be partnering with its newest non-profit, Lil’ S.I.S., primarily located on the South Side.  One of Lil’ S.I.S.’s biggest events is the Expo.

The Lil' S.I.S. Expo is an annual conference for teen girls, featuring forums on topics such as body image, college preparation, sexual health, spirituality, teen pregnancy, fitness, and financial well-being.  Girls of all races and socio-economic levels are invited to attend, and speakers from across the country volunteer their and expertise to this event. 

One Brick volunteers will be assisting with directing the young ladies to the various workshops, distributing goody bags, and any other task to help the event run smoothly. 

In keeping with the spirit and mission of the event, this opportunity is limited to female volunteers only.
