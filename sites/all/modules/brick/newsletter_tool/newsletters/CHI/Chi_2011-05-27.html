Upcoming Events

DASH FOR DETECTION

A 5k walk-run with the Pancreatic Cancer Foundation

The Rolfe Foundation in partnership with The Lustgarten Foundation is hosting a 5K walk-run to raise awareness and funds for early detection pancreatic cancer research.

Come help One brick support the cause by volunteering to work the Post Event Refreshments Table!

On the morning, during and after the event, we will be in charge of organizing and distributing refreshments and table crowd control (so that the "back of the pack" runners/walkers are able to enjoy the refreshments as well). This duty also includes cleanup, break down of boxes and collection of bags.

Are you Down to Dash, get the details here!
