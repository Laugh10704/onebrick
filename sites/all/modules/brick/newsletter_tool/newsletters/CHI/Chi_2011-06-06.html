All Hands On Deck-Missouri Disaster Relief Project

Bust out your inner handyman (or woman) and road trip to Morehouse, Missouri to help flood victims reclaim their homes and their lives!

Can't take a week-long trip to New Orleans? Plan to join One Brick for two days as we head to Morehouse, Missouri to help flood victims.

In May, 280 homes - two-thirds of all the homes in Morehouse - suffered flood damage.  Most residents in this small community did not have flood insurance because the town never floods, and they don't have the additional financial resources to repair their homes without help from agencies and volunteers like you. 

The weekend of June 17th, One Brick will be partnering up with All Hands Volunteers to help gut and muck out homes and generally assist in the relief effort they are providing in Morehouse.

Food and housing will be provided; volunteers are responsible for their own transportation. Carpool from Chicago will be available.

Spots are filling up fast!

Get The Movement Started With Girl World!

Girl Movement is an annual event sponsored by Girl World to that provides community girls and young women the opportunity to learn about a variety of professions, and encourage participants to think broadly and openly regarding their future career possibilities.

This ladies only event draws up to 150 girls for an entire day of activities with professional presenters from careers in education, science, medicine, law enforcement and the performing arts!

Girl World has asked that volunteers help with set-up,decorations, registration, taking pictures, serving food, clean up, and assisting event participants with room location.

Interested in helping the Girl Movement? Sign up here!

**Female volunteers only as this is a conference event for young women and girls. **

TREMENDOUS TOM!
