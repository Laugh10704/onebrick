Big Help in the Big Easy!

Help Rebuild New Orleans!!!

Once again, volunteers from each of One Brick's nine chapters will be convening in St. Bernard Parish from May 7th-15th. Join volunteers from around the country on another trip to take part in this incredible volunteer effort to rebuild the Gulf Coast.

One Brick has been providing a steady stream of volunteers to this area with trips to the region every year since 2005 hurricanes and will once again be working with the incredible nonprofit organization: The St. Bernard Project (www.stbernardproject.org).

Interested in lending a hand? Find more details here!
