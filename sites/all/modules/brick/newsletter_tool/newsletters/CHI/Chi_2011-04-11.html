National Volunteer Week!

Growing in scope each year since being established in 1974, National Volunteer Week is about inspiring, recognizing and encouraging people to seek out imaginative ways to engage in their communities.

At One Brick we use this week to take a pause, remember all the good work we've done together and make sure we thank all the wonderful people who volunteer with us.

If you haven't volunteered in a little while, this is a great opportunity to re-engage with your community and get out and volunteer. Check out the calendar here.

This year we have a special thank you for folks that volunteer during NVW. Our friends at bloomspot would like to "take you to the movies." No strings attached. Simply attend a One Brick volunteer event between Sunday April 10th and Saturday April 16th, and we'll send you a movie ticket.

Lastly, if you haven't become a member of bloomspot yet we'd like to encourage you to sign up. They are a great organization, which offers tremendous deals/opportunities. Plus, their "Community Circle" Program supports hundreds of community non-profits, including ONE BRICK.

On behalf of the board, our local chapters, teams and, most importantly, all of the 1200 non-profits we help, thank you!

One Brick in the Big Easy

Help Rebuild New Orleans!!!

The week of May 7th, volunteers from each of One Brick's nine chapters will once again be convening in St. Bernard Parish to take part in the incredible effort to rebuild the Gulf Coast.

Since 2005, One Brick has been providing a steady stream of volunteers to this area with trips to the region every year. We will once again be working with the incredible nonprofit organization: The St. Bernard Project (www.stbernardproject.org).

Getting to know your EM/EC's

In keeping with the spirit of National Volunteer Week, the newsletter is launching a new monthly Q & A article to highlight an EM or EC and get to know them better.

Our first Q&A was with Dave Z!

Dave is an Event Manager with One Brick and has done over 150 events since joining 2007. A frequent Project Rebuild volunteer, Dave was also a member of One Brick's previous NOLA team! Learn more below:

1. First/Favorite Event

Night Ministry Cookie packing, which was changed to Condom packing.

2. Reason for volunteering

Because my time is the best thing I have to give.

3. Your life needs...

A tall ship and a star to sail her by.

4. Best part of NOLA trip?

Meeting the family whose house we were working on and finding out their reason for staying in New Orleans.

5. Yellow or Orange?

Orange.

6. CTA or Car?

Car. Even when I CTA.

7. Twitter or Facebook?

Both.

8. What is the first thing you notice about people?

Whether or not they are wearing a hat. It used to be glasses, but I've been proven wrong on that point.

9. What was the last movie you went to see?

Predators

10.Parting words...

Wherever you go, there you are.
