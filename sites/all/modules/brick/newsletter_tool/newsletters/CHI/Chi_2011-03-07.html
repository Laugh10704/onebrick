"Beer" Ware the Ides of March

Spring's not here, but we've got beer!! It's that time of year- March Madness, a longing for Spring, St. Patty's Day, and Mardi Gras- but wouldn't you like a chance to give back AND have some fun at the same time!?

Come to Beer Tasting at Haymarket Brewery! www.haymarketbrewing.com

Here are the details:

For the tasting- entry will be $15, $10 with a donation of children's books (Pre-K-8th grade please!) for Galapagos Charter School on the West Side!

To try the beer, it is $1.50-$2.50 per 4oz pour- there will be lots of options, and an expert on hand to teach you about the beer you are trying!

Other FABULOUS activities will include a Beer Goggles Photo Contest and March themed trivia- prizes will also be March themed and AWESOME.

Calling all Kickballers!

First EVER One Brick Kickball Team!


Who: One Brickers!

What: Co-ed, Rec League (read: fun, not competitive) Kickball!

Where: Games (and any practices) will be at Waveland Park

When: First game is April 16th, games will be between 10am and 1pm on Saturdays for 6-7 weeks (depending on how awesome we are!)

Why: Have fun, get exercise, meet new people, support One Brick!

How much: $90 per person- includes a T-shirt and Team Picture, as well as specials at our Sponsor Bar (still TBD), helps to pay for refs, field upkeep, and league fees



Contact Andrea S. for more info: andreas@onebrick.org


**Info Session and In-Person Sign-Up March 24th, 7:30pm at The Stretch Bar and Grill (www.thestretchchicago.com)- bring cash/check to register!
