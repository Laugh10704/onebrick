Volunteering = Career Advancement
Ever wonder what makes a good job candidate a great one?

In a recent study conducted by LinkedIn, a leading social media career site, researchers discovered that 89% of those surveyed have volunteer experience that went unlisted from their personal career profiles.

For all the hard work that our One Brick Seattle volunteers do, why not flaunt it and see what it can do for your career!

Other News

One Brick Seattle returns to NOLA!
The One Brick New Orleans chapter is proud to host another week-long volunteer re-building effort at St. Bernard Project in New Orleans, Louisiana. Join One Brick Seattle volunteers as we travel to New Orleans for this service event from October 1st - 9th.

In a community devastated by hurricanes Katrina and Rita, St. Bernard Parish in New Orleans still depends on the support of volunteers to re-construct hurricane-devastated residential and commercial units, to help bring families back to their homes. 


To learn more about this event and travel arrangements, visit the One Brick Seattle event page and secure your spot at our Eventbrite site today!
