Join us for Happy Hour!

Please mark your calendars to join our next happy hour on Monday, March 14, from 6:00 to 8:00 pm.  This time, we’ll be gathering at the George & Dragon Pub in Fremont for an evening of food, libation, and fun!  If you’re new to One Brick, this is the perfect opportunity to introduce yourself, learn more about our organization, and meet others who also share an interest in helping out our community.  Feel free to bring some friends, too!

Word has it that free pool is offered on Mondays.  So why not stop in and challenge some One Brickers to a game of pool or two!

See you there!
