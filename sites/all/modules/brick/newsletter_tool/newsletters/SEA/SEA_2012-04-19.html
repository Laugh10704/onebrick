Open Events

This Saturday, April 21, One Brick will be out helping Garden for They Shall Walk and we're currently shy on RSVPs.

This is one of One Brick's newer events and They Shall Walk works to develop technology that allows paralyzed people to stand, walk and exercise.

Our work helps those volunteers use more of their time towards development instead of grounds keeping.

RSVP here for this National Volunteer Week event and we'll see you on Saturday!


On April 28, the Send Bicycles to Africa project also has several open RSVPs available for the taking. At this event, find yourself with tools in-hand working alongside your friends to disassemble donated bicycles for shipment to Africa.

This event supports the Ndumo Orphans project. RSVP here.

May calendar


Our May calendar is ready for your RSVPs! Take a look and start planning your spring so you can get while the getting is good.


Cool stuff next month includes:

A fundraiser for North Helpline which boasts fare provided by local restaurants, live music and crafts for sale. Help contribute to its success.

The 21st annual PONCHO Wine Auction for the Arts is happening on Friday, May 11. This event benefits non-profit arts organizations in the Seattle area.

Lastly, Hope-Link Bellevue teams up each year with the local post office for a food drive. Carriers collect food donations along their routes and One Brick volunteers will be there to help unload and sort the donations. Help the Bellevue postal food drive.

RSVP via the orange links above. Thank you for all that you do.
