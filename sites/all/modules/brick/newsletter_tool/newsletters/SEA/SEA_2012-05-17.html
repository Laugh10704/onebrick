Upcoming Events







Help Little Bit prepare for their annual horse show

Each year, the Little Bit Therapeutic Riding Center puts on a horse show for its riders.

June 3rd
One Brick will join Little Bit staff from 10 AM-1 PM to help ready the arena for the show. To help out with this event, dress for dirt and prepare for painting and clean-up efforts in Woodinville. Please RSVP here.

Little Bit is an organization that provides equine-assisted therapy for adults and children with disabilities. Their mission: To improve our clients' bodies, minds and spirits through our programs, and to be an inspiration and educational resource to the therapeutic riding profession, both regionally and nationally.
