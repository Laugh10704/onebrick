April 16th, 2011  

News and Announcements

THANK YOU, One Brick Seattle Volunteers!!

President Barack Obama has officially proclaimed this week -- from April 10 to April 16 -- as National Volunteer Week.  Last year, nearly 63 million Americans decided to make a difference in their communities by serving as volunteers.

Entirely volunteer-run, One Brick Seattle is fueled and sustained by you!  We simply couldn't make it happen without your dedication and commitment to public service.  On behalf of the non-profit organizations we serve, we truly appreciate all the time and energy that you choose to generously donate.

 

Upcoming Events

Happy Hour at Rob Roy

Please join us at our next happy hour on Monday, April 25, from 6:00 to 8:00 pm.  This time, we'll be gathering at Rob Roy in Belltown for another evening of food, drink, and merriment.

If you're new to One Brick, this is the perfect opportunity to introduce yourself, learn more about our organization, and socialize with others who also share an interest in helping out our local community.  Feel free to bring friends with you, too!

 

Looking Ahead to May

Our schedule is now set for May, with many exciting volunteer opportunities. 

To kick it off, on Sunday, May 1, is the Fight for Air 5K Run/Walk, an event supporting the American Lung Association's ongoing efforts to prevent asthma and other lung diseases.  That day, we'll be volunteering at the finish line, handing out water bottles to participants and assisting with event clean up.  Among other events, we'll be again helping out with food distribution at St. Mary's, preparing bicycles for shipment to South Africa, and repacking bulk donations at Food Lifeline.

So, check out next month's calendar!  And since many events fill up quickly, don't forget to sign up early for those you're interested in.
