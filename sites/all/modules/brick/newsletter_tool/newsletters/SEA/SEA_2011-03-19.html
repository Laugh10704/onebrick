Selfless Acts of Volunteerism

With the recent tragedy in Japan, the images and stories in the media serve as a subtle reminder of how a nation, or nations, band together in times of need. 

On a smaller scale, the work that One Brick has done on a national level - and locally in our Seattle community - is also important to note. The work of our volunteers is both selfless and essential to building a strong sense of community. By investing in the community as a continuous One Brick Seattle volunteer, together we can assist local non-profit organizations and community partners.

So thank you, One Brick Seattle volunteers, for all the work that you do and continue to do!
