News and Announcements

Give the Gift of Walking
At One Brick, we strive to make a difference in our community. Join fellow One Brick volunteers on Saturday, November 19th, from 10:00am-1:00pm, at the They Shall Walk Research Lab. While volunteer medical professionals, reseachers and nurses develop innovative ways in improve the quality of life and give the gift of walking, we will be preparing their community garden for the winter. Sign up for this wonderful event today!
