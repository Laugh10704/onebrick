One Brick's Annual Campaign is Coming to a Close!




Thank you to all of our hard-working volunteers and non-profit partners! In our 10 years, One Brick has grown from one chapter to eleven chapters strong!

To keep the momentum going, please consider giving a gift to One Brick today before our annual fundraising campaign comes to a close. We have a way to go to meet our goal of $45,000 so every little bit counts!

We've made it easy for you to submit your secure gift online as a one-time payment or reoccuring gift, so thank you in advance for making a difference "one brick" at a time!





 

Other News

'Tis the Season for Volunteering!

During the holiday season, Seattle-area non-profits experience an influx of volunteers, which help meet the overwhelming needs of families in our community.

In a recent Seattle Post-Intelligencer article, non-profit volunteer coordinators cite their appreciation for additional help during the holidays, "but they also hope more folks will consider volunteering year-round or at other times."

At One Brick Seattle, we are proud to keep a year-round volunteer presence in our community and we couldn't do it without your help! We hope that you will continue your involvement into the new year!
