Upcoming Events

North Helpline Needs You!
One Brick Seattle Volunteers!

Looking for a fun weekend activity this Saturday? Hurry now and sign up to volunteer at North Helpline this Saturday, August 5th, from 9:00am-1:00pm!

North Helpline is a non-profit organization that works to alleviate financial crisis for the community by providing nutritious food, hygiene items, baby supplies, eviction prevention, utility shutoff prevention, and move-in support.

At North Helpline, volunteers will assist customers, distribute and re-stock food items and help carry groceries to client vehicles. We look forward to seeing you there and don't forget to RSVP today!

How Can You Help?
At One Brick Seattle, we pride ourselves on all our great volunteers and non-profit partners. As a 100% volunteer-run organization, it's the dedication of volunteers like you that make the difference!

With your help, we can make an even bigger impact! One Brick Seattle is looking for additional volunteer staff interested in taken on a role with the organization. Much like volunteering, you can decide what works best for your schedule and your intererests. Perhaps you're a social media maven? Or maybe you are looking for a behind-the-scenes event planning role? Either way, we'd like to hear from you!

For more information, email seattle@onebrick.org or visit the One Brick Seattle website.
