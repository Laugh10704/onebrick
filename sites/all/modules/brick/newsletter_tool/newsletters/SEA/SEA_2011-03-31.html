Let's Help North Helpline This Weekend...

Started in 1989, North Helpline began with a few volunteers to help our community by providing basic human resources such as nutritious food, hygiene items, baby supplies, eviction prevention, utility shutoff prevention, and move-in support. North Helpline now serves over 2,500 unduplicated households each year and distributes over one million pounds of food.
 
One Brick volunteers were sorting food at North Helpline - January 22, 2011
 
One Brick Seattle is proud to have the opportunity to assist North Helpline in their effort to reduce the impact of hunger and poverty with dignity and respect in Greater North Seattle. Please come join us at the next North Helpline event on April 2.

Help Rebuild New Orleans!

By the end of August of this year, it will have been six years since the devastating Hurricane Katrina. Yet, many people can only yearn to "go back home." In an effort to help rebuild a home for Katrina's survivors, One Brick is launching its annual return trip to New Orleans. Volunteers from each of One Brick's nine chapters will be convening in St. Bernard Parish from May 7th-15th. Join volunteers from around the country to take part in this incredible volunteer effort!

For further details see our FAQ at: http://wiki.onebrick.org/index.php/NOLA_trips
