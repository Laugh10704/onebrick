News and Announcements

We're doing something new!

It's true. Don't look so surprised. There's a new organization in town and they're called South West Queen Anne Green Belt Forest Restoration. Now take a deep breath and a sip of water, because that was a mouthful.

So this new organization is harvesting the One Brick volunteer crop (that's you) and you can RSVP this very second through our calendar.

We hope you'll join us on this adventurous journey into the wild. We will be helping to clear invasive plant species from the 70 acre green belt on the Southwest side of Queen Anne (just like the name promises). Don't worry. We won't be tackling all of it, but this will be the beginning of an ongoing effort. We hope to see you there!
 

Showing Love

The only thing we love more than a good situation comedy is a well-attended happy hour.

Look, we've all met. We've all worked for some great local causes alongside each other. We at One Brick want you to know that we're not neutral about you. We are ready to take the next step in this relationship.

It may be sudden and unexpected, but it's true. We want to go to happy hour with you. But how do we entice you to share a beverage and a snack with us?

If you're just not that into us, that's okay, but we'd love to know why. Please send us your suggestions (or rejections) on all things happy hour; Location, libation or potential activities, we'll take it all.

If you must reject us, please let us down gently. Maybe you could write a nice haiku to quell the sting.
