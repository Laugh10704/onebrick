Tucking away the 2011 uglies
If you're like us, your ugly holiday
sweater just arrived back from the cleaners and you're busy wrapping it up lovingly in tissue paper. Tucked away safely in a drawer, it will patiently await next season's festivities.

Now what the heck are you going to do with all of these upcoming months that don't require sparkly garb?

What you can do is join One Brick out in the field on one of our volunteer gigs. You'll feel pretty darn awesome about yourself whether you succeed in giving up cookies for 2012 or not. That's a win in our eyes. Get your instant dose of awesome for 2012 right away. Sign up for a fun event through our calendar and we'll see you soon!

Upcoming Events

Garden to support wearable robots

They Shall Walk is a non-profit organization that is 100% run by volunteers. They've enlisted One Brick to help them find folks that can assist in maintaining the grounds of their research lab lot on Saturday, January 14.

With you and One Brick manning the yard, They Shall Walk researchers and medically-trained volunteers can focus on advancing the technology of their wearable robot called LIFESUIT which assists paralyzed people with walking, exercise and quality of life. Researchers will be able to spend more time researching in the lab instead of keeping up the lab grounds. We think that's cool. 

To lend your gardening skills and environmental knowledge, RSVP on the event page.


You should resolve to have more fun – One Brick happy hour

Trending in 2012 are newsletters encouraging you to forget about your new year’s resolutions followed by a cheesy call to action that promises to somehow enhance your life and possibly save the world.

Hey! Don’t judge us!

Without any of those (mostly) true claims, we simply want to make you aware that there will be some sweet drink and food specials going on at Mexico Cantina and Cocina in Pacific Place on Sunday, January 22nd . One Brick people will be there too. This is a no-pitch, happy hour-only hang out, but if you have questions about volunteer events, we will try not to talk with our mouths full.

Beer and wine are $3 a pop while happy hour is on and there are also specials on appetizers. The coolest kids will  RSVP via our calendar. Cheers!
