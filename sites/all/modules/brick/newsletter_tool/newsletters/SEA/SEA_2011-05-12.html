Beat the Bridge to Beat Diabetes

  Calling all One Brick Seattle volunteers!

Sign up for this weekend’s Beat the Bridge to Beat Diabetes event to support the Juvenille Diabetes Research Foundation. We want to make a huge impact with our partner at this event, so grab a friend or family member and sign up today to volunteer at University of Washington Husky Stadium this Sunday, May 15, 2011 from 10:00am – 12:30pm. One Brick volunteers will assist in cleaning up and taking down booths after the event. Thanks in advance for your help and be sure to spread the word if you aren’t available to sign-up!

Click here for full event details and to sign up on the One Brick Seattle website.
