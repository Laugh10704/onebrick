The heart of the matter
Typically we're fond of playful segues but this week, Seattle One Brick, we'll cut to the chase. You've helped One Brick with your time and maybe you've even opened your wallet for the One Brick cause already but here we are asking more of you.



There's no choice.

A year from now we want to see you at Food Lifeline wearing a One Brick name tag. A year from now we want to be welcoming new folks into the One Brick family with you by our side. A year from now we want to be writing playful newsletters about the experiences we've all shared. We've built a lot together and we're hoping that Seattle, along with all of One Brick's chapters, can give a little bit more so that we can all continue offer our time to the community.

So we ask that you donate, if you can, to help reach the $40k goal to keep One Brick in Seattle and eleven other cities across the country. Click here to donate.

Thank you so much from the bottom of our hearts for your continued support.
