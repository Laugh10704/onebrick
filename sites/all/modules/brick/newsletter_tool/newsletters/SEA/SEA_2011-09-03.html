One Brick - Seattle's First Trivia Night!

One Brick Seattle helps connect fantastic volunteers with stellar non-profit and community groups... enabling people to get involved, make an impact, meet new folks, and have loads of fun! Come join us on September 29th at 7:00 pm at Buckley's In Belltown for a fun-filled night of trivia competition.  
Your admission price benefits One Brick Seattle and includes:

     - Extended Happy Hour pricing until 9:30 pm

     - Individual entry into our trivia night contest

     - Chance to win fabulous prizes and One Brick swag!

Bring your family, friends, co-workers, neighbors, and community members... form a team of four to compete for prizes, including Flip video cameras for the first place team, plus wine and gift cards to Buckley's for other winners!

Tickets are on sale now at http://seattle.onebrick.org! Early bird pricing is $25 per person and goes directly toward helping One Brick Seattle offer even more volunteer opportunities in our community!

Let's Get Ready for the Seattle Marathon!

The Seattle Marathon isn't until November 27th, but there's a lot of work that goes into getting ready. They are expecting 17,000 participants for the marathon and half marathon events this year. Be a part of One Brick's effort to get those runners ready for the long haul by volunteering with the Seattle Marathon Association to prepare t-shirts for merchandise sales and unpack leftover bags from last year's race, plus any other tasks that the Seattle Marathon Association might need us to do.
