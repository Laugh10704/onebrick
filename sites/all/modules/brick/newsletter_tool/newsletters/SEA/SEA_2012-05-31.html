Happy 20th Anniversary, FareStart!

FareStart is celebrating 20 years of transforming lives! The generous support of the community has enabled new beginnings each day at FareStart. Over the past 20 years, FareStart has provided opportunities for nearly 6,000 people to transform their lives, while also serving over 5 million meals to disadvantaged men, women, and children.

The One Brick Seattle family of volunteers is proud to assist FareStart and to accelerate their mission. Join fellow volunteers on Saturday, June 9th, to assist with FareStart's special anniversary fundraising event. Click here to RSVP today!  

Upcoming Events

Help Conquer Cancer!



The Enbridge Ride to Conquer Cancer® is a unique, two-day cycling event from Vancouver, B.C., to Redmond, WA, benefiting the BC Cancer Foundation. Last year, over 2,900 cyclists participated and the foundation raised over $11 million dollars.

This year, One Brick Seattle volunteers will be helping cyclists at the finish line in Marymoor Park on Sunday, June 17th, from 12:30pm-4:30pm. Come out and conquer cancer with fellow volunteers. To RSVP for this event and to learn more, click here.
