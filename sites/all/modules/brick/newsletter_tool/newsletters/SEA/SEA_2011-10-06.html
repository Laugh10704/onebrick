News and Announcements

Warming up to fall

October is percolating! We kicked off the month with three hearty events on Saturday, Oct. 1 after ending September with our trivia fundraiser.

Thanks to you, events are filling up more quickly than our rain catcher. Get yourself to the calendar right away to sign up for new events if you haven't already!



Eastside Happy Hour
Keep in mind that our first Eastside happy hour is approaching on Wednesday, Oct. 12. This will be a fun, relaxed event at the Celtic Bayou in Redmond. Stop by, say hello, sample the Irish and Cajun fare and have a frosty beverage.
Please let Eastside friends and co-workers know about this event. We love new faces! Bridge-friendly Seattle dwellers are also welcome and encouraged to join.

Consider this:

Food Lifeline needs many volunteers this month to make their engine go!
Volunteers that sign up for this event will spend time with a large gathering of fellow One Brick folks packing up food for food banks around the area.

Please consider donating your time here, especially if you think you can make it into the Hairnet Hall of Fame. Here are a few contenders. Do you have what it takes?

Remember: You can't win if you don't play. Join us!
