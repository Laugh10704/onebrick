National Volunteer Week is Near!


One Brick Seattle family is pleased to take part in the upcoming National Volunteer Week from Sunday, April 15, through Saturday, April 21st!

In recognition of this service week and the many hard-working volunteers in the area, the Seattle Mariners and the Washington Commission for National & Community Service are co-sponsoring a special night as a thank you! The 4th Annual "Salute to Volunteers Night" will take place at 7:10pm on Tuesday, April 17, 2012, when the Seattle Mariners play the Cleveland Indians at Safeco Field. Highlights include:

  • Pre-game ceremony salute to volunteers;
  • Special in-field recognition for recipients of the 2012 Governor's Volunteer Service Award;
  • The first pitch will be thrown by the youngest recipient of the Governor's Volunteer Service Award '12; and
  • The April issue of the Mariners Magazine will feature an article on volunteerism. 
Discounted tickets (from $8 View Reserved tickets to $25 Field Level Seating tickets) will be available for purchase in advance of the game!
 
For more information on this event or for purchasing your discounted "Salute to Volunteers Night" tickets, visit the Seattle Mariners' website today!
