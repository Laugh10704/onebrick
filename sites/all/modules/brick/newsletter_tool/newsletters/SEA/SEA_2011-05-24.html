Upcoming Events

Helping Homeless Families to Rebuild Their Lives

The Baby Boutique needs our help!  This Saturday, May 28, from 10:00 am to 2:00 pm, we've been asked to organize and stock donated items for its clients.

As a program of Wellspring Family Services, the Baby Boutique provides free clothing, diapers, infant formula, and many other essential supplies to homeless families with children.  Each year, it serves the needs of over 3,000 children and their families in King County.  And its operation relies heavily on dedicated volunteers to sort through donations and organize items for distribution.

You can impact young lives and help to combat homelessness, just by volunteering a few hours at the Baby Boutique.  Click here to sign up for this event.

 

A Happy Hour for All Math Lovers

We are excited to partner with Explorations in Math, whose mission focuses on improving math learning in elementary schools.  At their Math Happy Hour on Thursday, June 2, from 6:00 to 8:30 pm, we'll be preparing materials for several summer math camps for kids.

Although not your traditional happy hour, you can still expect fun times and wine at this event.  No math expertise is required...just your enthusiasm toward helping young students to excel in math!  For more event details, click here.
 

 

Serving Hot Dogs for a Great Cause

Do you like fundraising for a great cause?  Do you like shopping for bargains?  If you answered yes to both, here's the perfect event for you!

Come and join other One Brick Seattle volunteers at the Grocery Outlet's grand opening celebration in Lake City on Saturday, June 18, from 10:00 am to 2:00 pm.  We'll be selling hot dogs, passing out balloons and prizes, and participating in other fun activities.  All proceeds raised from the hot dog sales will go directly to North Helpline, a non-profit organization that we regularly support by lending a hand at its food bank.

Together, let's ensure this event is a fundraising success!  Click here to sign up now.
