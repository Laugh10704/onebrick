Upcoming Events

Volunteers still needed this Saturday, Aug. 20

Howdy One Brick,

Spontaneous folks will be delighted to learn that there are still volunteer spaces available for both Saturday events.
 

Consider donating your time to help the Seattle Marathon Association by stuffing packets for their upcoming 10K.

Each runner needs a packet with a bib and timing chip and we'll be helping to make that happen for the following week's race.

On Aug. 20, meet One Brick in the Seattle Marathon Association storage area in South Seattle on 4731-B Colorado Ave. S just before 9:30 a.m.

RSVP here to participate in this effort, find additional details for the event and learn more about the Seattle Marathon Association.


August's Movie in the Park is happening this Saturday too!

As part of Jamfest, Hing Hay Park in the International District, will host an outdoor viewing of "Newsies".

Volunteers are still needed to help with set up and take down of the movie event as well as a sprinkle of other Jamfest-related needs.

One Brick will meet at the Wing Luke museum before 6 p.m. Check in by 5:30 p.m. to take part in a light dinner with fellow volunteers.

RSVP for Movie in the Park here and read more about Jamfest.

We hope to see you Saturday!
