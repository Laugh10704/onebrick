Fight for Air with the American Lung Association

Calling all One Brick Seattle volunteers! Sign up for this weekend’s Fight for Air 5k Run/Walk event to support the American Lung Association. We want to make a huge impact with our partner at this event, so grab a friend or family member and sign up today to volunteer at the Fight for Air 5k Run/Walk in Magnuson Park this Sunday, May 1, 2011 from 9:00am – 11:30am. One Brick volunteers will assist at the finish line and with tear down following the race.

Click here for full event details and to sign up on the One Brick Seattle website.

Thanks in advance for your help and be sure to spread the word if you aren’t available to sign-up! We look forward to adding to our existing One Brick Seattle volunteer family!

Improving Communities One Bike at a Time

 

 

 

One Brick Seattle is excited to work with the Bicycles for Humanity Seattle, an organization committed to sending unwanted or surplus bicycles to South African communities. Join us on Saturday, May 14, 2011, from 9:00am – 12:30pm, as we dismantle, pack, and prepare hundreds of bikes to be shipped to South African communities in need.

Click here for full event details and to sign up to assist at this event.
