News and Announcements

Sign up for Op4G to support One Brick!

One Brick has recently partnered with Op4G, an internet-based market research organization that pays its members for sharing their opinions about products and services.  Participating in Op4G is simple and absolutely free.  In return for completing online surveys, you earn cash for yourself AND One Brick.  Plus, just by signing up, you'll have a chance to win an iPad2!  It’s definitely a win-win situation!

Op4G initially offered to make a one-time $15,000 donation to One Brick if we recruited 500 people by March 31.  We’ve now exceeded that target!  Over 700 people have signed up so far!  As a special thank you, Op4G has sweetened the deal by offering to donate an ADDITIONAL $10,000 if we get a total of 1,000 people to sign up.

So don’t forget to register on the Op4G website today!  Encourage your friends, relatives, and co-workers to register, too!  Let’s meet that 1,000-mark goal!
