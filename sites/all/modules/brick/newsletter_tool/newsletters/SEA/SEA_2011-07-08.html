Volunteers Needed at the Kids Parade Tomorrow!

Redmond Derby Days celebrates its 71st anniversary in 2011! It's a summer tradition that came to life at the end of the Depression. Derby Days began as a drive to raise money for downtown holiday decorations and school athletic equipment. The Redmond Bicycle Derby was the outgrowth of a small town's community spirit and is now the nation's longest running bicycle race. It includes activities for young and old, such as the free Friday Night Live concert, Friday Night Poker Ride, Movies in the Park, Kids & Grand Parade, Microsoft Entertainment Stage, Bicycle Criterium, IMPACT Eco-Fair, Derby Days Dash 5K Race/Walk, Overlake Hospital Medical Center Kids Zone, Food/Beer/Wine Gardens, Funtastic Carnival, Fireworks Finale and the Redmond Fire Department Pancake Breakfast.

Volunteer Matching

Many companies offer programs that support your volunteer efforts, either by allowing time off or by matching funds to your volunteer time. If your company is one of these, lucky you, and lucky One Brick! One Brick is completely volunteer run which allows us to keep our operational costs much lower than other similar organizations. However, we are also completely volunteer supported and we rely on indvidual contributions to help keep us running year after year. Ask around at your company and see how you can take advantage of their volunteer match programs. Send any questions you may have to seattle@onebrick.org.
