Partners in Math

One Brick Seattle is honored to work with a diverse group of area non-profits and community-minded organizations. Whether it's working with a local food bank, or assisting at non-profit fundraisers, we are pleased to give back where we can. That's why we are excited to partner with the Explorations in Math organization, as they work to promote positive and fun math education in elementary school communities.

Explorations in Math is gearing up for their 1st Annual "Backyard Mini-MathFest" on Saturday, June 18th, from 10:00am-12:00noon. Co-Founder Katie Klein will be hosting the event and she is looking for volunteers that are interested in engaging families with interactive and fun math games. To sign-up directly with our partners at Explorations in Math, please email volunteer@eimath.org or call 206-325-0774!

Upcoming Events

Conquering Cancer!

Hey One Brick volunteers! 

Looking for a one-of-a-kind event to kick off your volunteeering this summer? Join the One Brick Seattle team as we assist at the Enbridge Ride to Conquer Cancer, a two day, 250km bike ride from Vancouver to Seattle. The event begins on Saturday, June 18th, but we are looking for a few good men and women to join the fight against cancer on Sunday, June 19th, from 12:30pm-4:30pm.

Enjoy the great outdoors at Marymoor Park in Seattle while supporting this great fundraising cause! All funds will benefit cancer research, treatment and education programs. Volunteers will assist with monitoring bike race transition areas, help load racers' gear and bikes, and assisting at the finish line.

To learn more about the event or to register online to volunteer, visit the One Brick Event Calendar today!
