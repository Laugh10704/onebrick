Enbridge Ride to Conquer Cancer
This event is this Sunday, June 17 at Marymoor Park and we’re in need of volunteers to assist the Bike Parking, Bike Loading and Gear Retrieval Teams. Please lend a hand to keep the One Brick presence strong and RSVP here.

The efforts of volunteers at this event help raise funds, all of which go to benefit cancer research, treatment and education programs.

The 2012 Enbridge Ride to Conquer Cancer is a two day, 250 km bike ride leaving Vancouver, BC on June 16th and arriving in Seattle/Redmond on June 17th.

We look forward to seeing you at this exciting event!
