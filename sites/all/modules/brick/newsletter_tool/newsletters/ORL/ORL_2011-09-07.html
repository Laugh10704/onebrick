September 7th, 2011  
News and Announcements

One Brick Plays Softball
This summer One Brick formed a softball team with the Orlando Sports and Social Club. Stanton, Kristin, Toby and Ross from our event management team played along with some of our other volunteers. Although the team won zero games, everyone had a great time making new friends, getting exercise and even drinking beer! There is another team starting soon so if you are interested in joning, you can contact Stanton at stantonb@onebrick.org. We are also helping the Special Olympics team practice with a monthly scrimmage game. The next game is October 8th and no experience is needed. Sign up here!

Volunteer Appreciation Day
Orlando One Brickers joined together at Wekiva Island for our Second Annual Volunteer Appreciation Day. We rented a cabana and cooked out on the grill while we celebrated a year of hard work and helping the community. One Brick is 100% volunteer run and without the help from our volunteers, none of this would be possible. Thank You Volunteers! We Appreciate You!

Day of Service 2011
On the 10 year anniversary of September 11, 2001, millions of people around the world will be participating in a Day of Service and Remembrance. It is easy and free to participate! All you need to do is observe the anniversary of 9/11 by performing good deeds, supporting charitable causes, volunteering and engaging in other acts of compassion. One Brick is proud to offer 2 opportunities to volunteer leading up to September 11. If you are not available for those events, you can find more at 911day.org or contact us at onebrickorlando@onebrick.org for help.

Event Recaps

Special Olympics County Games
In August we had the opportunity to provide volunteers to the Special Olympics of Seminole County during their bowling and power lifing games. One team of One Brickers met at AMF Altamonte Lanes while the other was at Gold's Gym for check-in, registration, awards, score keeping and over-all, having a good time. If you missed these events, don't worry we have more coming up with the Special Olympics! Check out our event calendar for dates and times.

Food and Clothing Drive
For our September Social, One Brickers joined together at Blue Jacket Park for a food and clothing drive to benefit the HOPE Foundation and Community Food and Outreach Center. We had delicious food donated by Einstein Bagels, Sweet! Cupcakes, Margarita's Grill of Avalon Park and China Garden of Winter Park. Channel 13 News came out to cover the event! Having a good time and helping the community is what we are all about! You can see a video here.
