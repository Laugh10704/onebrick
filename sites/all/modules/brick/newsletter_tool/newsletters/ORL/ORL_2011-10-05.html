September Events and One Brick Detroit
September was a volunteer packed month with our Clean Out Your Closet and Park It! food and clothing drive, our monthly trip to the Community Food and Outreach Center of Orlando, and Soapy Saturday at Clean the World. Check out our Facebook page for pictures!

On October 1st, a group of One Brickers from all over the contry took off to New Orleans to work with the St. Bernard Project. One Brick has been providing a steady stream of volunteers to this area with trips to the region every year since 2006.

The newest chapter of One Brick has started in Detroit this month and their October calendar is already packed with events! Detroit is the 10th chapter in the US to join the One Brick team!

Leadership Opportunites with One Brick
Did you know that One Brick Orlando is 100% volunteer run? From our chapter director, to event managers, to the person who is writing this newsletter, all are volunteers. We rely solely on the help and support of the community to recruit, train and provide quality volunteers to non-profit organizatioins throughout Central Florida.

Did you also know that volunteering can be good for your career and help build leadership skills? Recently, LinkedIn added a "Volunteer Experience & Causes" section to their profiles. You can include the causes you care about as well as your volunteer experience with One Brick.

One Brick is always looking for new event managers and coordinators and we are currently recruiting new members for our Leadership Team. Below is a list of open spots on our leadership team. If you are interested in becoming more involved with One Brick, contact Bruce at brucew@onebrick.org.

Marketing
Mission: To promote the Orlando One Brick Chapter to new and existing volunteers to increase volunteer participation and retention
Task: create marketing messages and campaigns; execute marketing delivery through email, social media, craigslist, advertising, and any other creative low cost means of attention, monitor results of marketing.

Fund Raising
Mission: Build a stable funding stream to support both existing operations and future.
Tasks: Arrange fundraising events, apply for and maintain grants, manage relationship with donors

Socials
Mission: Build a fun, social atmosphere and culture for One Brick locally to build relationships outside of volunteer events.
Tasks: Find and schedule fun social activities for whole chapter, host events and provide a welcoming atmosphere for new and returning volunteers.
