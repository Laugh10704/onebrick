“Food Drive Social” to be held at Baldwin Park
You are cordially invited to: FIRST FRIDAY at Orlando's Most Innovative Sports bar!

Friday, February 3rd 2012 6PM-10PM

Join One Brick and the Caddyshanks Family for a Food Drive Social benefiting the: Community Food & Outreach Center

The Community Food and Outreach Center helps those in need transform their lives by providing food, crisis care, opportunities for education and employment, and a hand up to families, individuals, and children who are fighting to break the cycle of poverty. Only one of many programs that the Center offers, The Emergency Food Box program is designed to meet the immediate hunger needs of those who visit the Outreach center’s campus while facing crisis.

Bring an unperishable food item to donate and you'll get a feel warm and fuzzy feeling inside...but also Caddyshanks is letting donators try their new Racing simulators for Free! Also, enjoy free appetizers and great drink specials and really good music..we tested these ourselves...worth the trip. Whether you donate or not, it's just a great time to get together, celebrate, and learn more about what's next at One Brick.

The First Friday & Food Drive Social event will be held February 3, 2012 at CaddyShanks Virtual Golf & Sports pub at 4882 New Broad Street, in the village center of Baldwin Park in Orlando from 6-10pm.

RSVP here: http://orlando.onebrick.org/event/?eventid=10792
