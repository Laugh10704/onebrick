Friendsgiving with One Brick
Happy Thanksgiving Weekend One Brickers!

This is wonderful time to give thanks and appreciate the blessing in our life, family, friends, food, love, having the opportunity to make a difference in people's lives, and many more.

Let's get together this coming week to celebrate with new and old friends and appreciate our time together as we look back on a meaningful year and look forward to many more opportunities in 2012. 

We'll be getting together for eats and drinks this coming Tuesday (11/29) evening at Urban Flats Downtown for our One Brick Social!

We are also hosting our Annual One Brick Toy Drive at Caddyshanks in Baldwin Park this Friday (12/2)!  Here's our chance to share our blessings with the kids in the community and make their holiday extra bright this year.

So you've got two great chances to celebrate Friendsgiving with One Brick. Grab some grub and collect some toys.  Also, we'll be having some special announcements exclusively for special events in the new year...early RSVP is a must so come out or you may miss out on the limited spots!

Visit our calendar to the left for event details and sign up today.

See you there!

Become a One Brick Event Manager!
Our volunteer Event Managers are the staff who make One Brick events happen! Anyone can join our team - all we ask is that you attend an Orientation to learn how One Brick works and then we'll discuss how you can help out. For more information, email us at orlando@onebrick.org.
 
Donate to One Brick!
One Brick has only one paid employee, our Executive Director. At the chapter level, One Brick is 100% volunteer-run. To help us keep making a difference in our community, you may may make a tax-deductible donation here.
