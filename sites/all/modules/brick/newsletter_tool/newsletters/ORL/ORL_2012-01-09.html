What do you get for 6 hours?

Being a One Brick Event Manager requires only 6 hours per month.

 

The single most common question asked by a volunteer being recruited for the event management team is: "How much time will I have to commit?"  After some serious number crunching (using both hands) we've arrived at a number: 6
 

Six hours per month is all it takes to be a One Brick Manager. Four hours for the event, one hour for the post-event social, and half an hour on either side for emails and reconciliation. This of course is a fungible value, and is wholly dependent upon the number of events you choose - but why sacrifice elegance and simplicity with a variable discussion?
 

Besides, discussing the time commitment is only half our recruiting effort. It should not be overlooked that there are rewards with becoming a One Brick Event Manager. Many would argue that the rewards outweigh any sense of obligation. Here's our top ten reasons why we like it so much: http://on.fb.me/ljMsuU
 

So, we're kicking off a recruiting campaign to replenish our ranks by addressing this very question.
 

So if you're up for joining the team and like the fun and challenge of leading awesome events, growing a cool organization, and meeting strikingly marvelous people then...
