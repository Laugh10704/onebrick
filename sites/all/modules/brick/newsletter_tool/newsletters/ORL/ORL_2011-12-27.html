Are you up for the Challenge?
One Brick Orlando presents
THE UCF CHALLENGE COURSE

January 14, 2012
1:30PM - 5PM


Come join us for our most extreme group social ever! We'll be teaming up to tackle the UCF Challenge Course for a day of action-pack, heart-pounding obstacles, exhilarating 45 foot high zip lining, outdoor fresh air and Florida Sun. 

We'll test our wits, our teamwork, and sense of humor.  There will be plenty of laughs because this is non-competitive and just for a good time.  Best of all, you get to do it with some really awesome people. Bring a couple pals and introduce them around, make some new friends, and do something bold.

There's something magical about getting around like-minded people interested in making a difference and enjoying life....especially in a venue like this. It'll be an all-day affair so be ready for an adventure.

Contact Eric Sugarman - esugar22000(at)yahoo(dot)com for payment details and questions. or Bruce Wang - brucew(at)onebrick(dot)org
