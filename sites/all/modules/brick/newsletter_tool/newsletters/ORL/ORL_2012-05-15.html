May 21: Pins, Pints and People
Bowling, Games and Book Drive - One Brick 2 Year Anniversary
 
Every event is a celebration with us, but this one is going to be exceptional!
 
Monday 21stMay 6.30PM – 10PM
 
Over this past year, One Brick Orlando has made a concrete difference in our local community. Since the launch of the Orlando Chapter in 2010, we have partnered with 64 organizations to host more than 150 events while being 100% volunteer-run. The team of 650 plus volunteers have contributed over 1700 volunteer hours to helping within the Orlando community as well as in other cities.
 
Led a team of passionate Event Managers and Event Coordinators, One Brick is committed to commitment-free volunteering in Orlando, and to creating social connections and encouraging others to have a great time while giving back to the community.
 
For our 2nd Anniversary celebration, we will be bowling, playing arcade games and shooting pool. There will be food and drinks, and lots of conversation and laughter. And since, for us, a celebration isn’t really a celebration unless we are giving back, this event is also book drive. So bring all the used books that you have around the house and no longer read (you know, like those books that are just propping up your old coffee table!) These books will go a long way in helping children and families enjoy the invaluable benefits of reading and bonding.
 
This is a great event for volunteers to meet the rest of the One Brick Orlando team. It’s also a wonderful opportunity for new members to learn and experience first-hand the unique social culture of One Brick, so bring a friend! Or two! Or three! Or four! 
 
The 2nd Annivesary celebration will be held on Monday 21stMay, 2012 at Firkin & Kegler which is located at 12850 Waterford Lakes Parkway, Orlando, FL 32828.
 
For details and to RSVP, please visit our event website: http://tinyurl.com/OB2yr
