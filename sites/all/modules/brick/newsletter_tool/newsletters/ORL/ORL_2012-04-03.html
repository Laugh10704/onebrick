How to Celebrate right this Easter Weekend
One volunteer, let's call her Selma, really did something inspiring.  She used her birthday as a reason to get her friends together and volunteer for a great cause.  So she choose to have her birthday celebration helping hard-hit familes last Christmas during our Christmas in the City event with One Brick and the HOPE Foundation. So instead of making it a day about her...she made it a day about helping.

Well, Selma inspired me to follow suit. So I'll be celebrating my birthday on April 7th, 2012 to give back because I've received so much from my community.  So I want to coordially invite you to celebrate with me and help out the local families by volunteering at the Community Food and Outreach Center this Saturday morning. 

We'll help out this wonderful organization that provides clean clothes, groceries, needed supplies, and life skill programs to families in tough transitions. Together, we'll roll up our sleeves, lend a needed hand, have a great time, and build our Orlando community one brick at a time.

Thanks for caring and making a difference.

Bruce W.
Chapter Director

One Brick Orlando
