Social Event: Beer Bus Trip ($25) - space limited

Last year we held a wildly successful "Wine Bus Road Trip". Now we are going to go one better and to a BEER BUS!...

Free Brewery Tours, Free or Reduced Priced Beer, Fun, Games, Laughter, .... It's not to be missed!

On Saturday March 10th, we are jumping on a bus and heading out for a bit of a pub crawl.  Please sign up early as space is limited.

10am   Mountain View Pickup (Google Building 46 - Map)
 8:30pm  Drop off in Mountain View 

Itinerary (tentative) will include stops at 21st Amendment (SF)  an, Marin Brewing Company (Lakspur) , Langunitas (Petaluma) , Jupiter Brewery  (Berkeley) and Polo Grounds.

Cost: $25 per person.
We are renting a "Google Bus". It is very comfortable and includes tables, toilet and wifi.
Food will be available at each of the stop. We will provide snacks, water etc on the bus.
$25 covered bus rental only. It does not include tasting fees.
Drinking beer is totally optional (but a lot of fun).
We are operating the bus "at cost" and we will hold a raffle (with beer and beer-related materials) to raise funds to support One Brick's volunteer work.

Sat, 3/10/2012 - 9AM-8:30PM - Social Event: Beer Bus Trip ($25) - space limited
 
 
 

Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)
Love to knit (or crochet) and like to socialize???  This is the perfect event for you.  Please join One Brick in helping make scarves and hats for seniors.  As this is our second Sip & Knit of the year, we'll decide which group we will support this year. 

Don't know how to knit or crochet?  This is the perfect opportunity to learn.  We will have a few experienced people who will teach us how to knit and/or crochet. Please feel free to bring your own needles, hooks, yarn etc.  However, these are not required.  We will try to have extra needles and hooks on hand.
 
Tue, 2/7/2012 - 6:30PM-9PM - Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)
 

Event Management Informational Meeting (San Jose)
You know how every time you go to an event, there's always somebody there, smiling, cracking dumb jokes, who makes you sign in at our event, and makes you wear the goofy nametag? Plant a Tree in San Jose
Since 1994, Our City Forest has planted over 22,000 trees in neighborhoods throughout Silicon Valley, and now it's One Brick's turn to help! We'll be planting in the Oak Grove Neighborhood of East San Jose.
 
Sat, 2/18/2012 - 9AM-12Noon - Plant a Tree in San Jose
 

Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers (Sunnyvale)
Sustainable agriculture is labor intensive. Full Circle Farm is a fledgling start up non-profit, we will be working to help the farm crew with tasks such as weeding, mulching, thinning and other light labor in the row crops and fruit orchard.
 
We will be outdoors the whole time on uneven, dirty and possibly muddy ground so please wear sun protection, bring water bottles, and wear clothes that will keep you cool or warm enough and sturdy shoes. This event is rain or shine.  You may get dirty but it is fun!
 
Sun, 2/19/2012 - 10AM-1PM - Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers (Sunnyvale)
 

Our City Forest New Nursery Work Day (San Jose)
Our City Forest's nursery is where we house all of our trees before they are planted in San Jose.  One Brick will help with irrigation, repotting, pruning, weeding, early tree care and tree stake/tool maintenance, all in preparation for upcoming plantings!
 
Sat, 2/25/2012 - 9AM-12Noon - Our City Forest New Nursery Work Day (San Jose)
 

Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen Helpers (San Jose)
Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen, a non-profit organization has been serving the working poor, families, homeless and seniors living in and around the downtown and East Side of San Jose for close to 30 years. What began as a simple first meal of fish and bread for 11 adults and 15 children has grown to nearly 500 meals per day, served with dignity. These no-cost hot meals are offered to our clients in a safe, caring, and friendly environment.
 
Volunteers at Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen may be asked to help with all aspects of the meals, including setting up our dining room for dinner, preparing the meal, serving and clean up.
 
Wed, 2/29/2012 - 3:15PM-6PM - Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen Helpers (San Jose)
