Event Recaps

Helping Hands at Sacred Heart (San Jose)

The cold weather on Saturday morning didn't deter our One Brick volunteers from showing up and eager to help at the Sacred Heart Helping Hands event. We were assigned to the clothes closet to sort through mountains of donated clothing for men, women and children. Everything from women's tops, bottoms, jackets, men's shirts and pants and children's outfits in a wide range of styles were hung on racks in the closet to prepare for when they next open it up. When all the sorting was done, we proceeded to Panera for lunch and interesting conversation. 

Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers (Sunnyvale)

After the winter storms had passed, One Brick farmers enjoyed a  gloriously sunny Sunday morning weeding & mulching in the orchards and turnip thinning in the fields.  We were also treated to a farm tour, fed the chickens so they wouldnt be tempted to eat mice (?!! who knew it?!!) and some of us bought some freshly picked produce from the farm store, before heading off for a well earned burger!
