Upcoming Events

Helping Hands at Sacred Heart (San Jose)
One Brick will help Sacred Heart prepare for another week of helping those in need. This may working in Louise's Pantry and Gracie's Clothes Closet. In the pantry, we will primarily make dried food bags of rice, beans, canned goods, cereals, toilet paper, etc. In the clothes closet, we will receive clothing donations, sort through donations, and hang clothes on racks. Some cleaning may also be involved.

Sat, 10/22/2011 - 9AM-12Noon - Helping Hands at Sacred Heart (San Jose)
 
 

Second Harvest Food Bank (San Carlos)
Help the Second Harvest Food Bank sort, inspect and repackage thousands of pounds of food, in just a short, 3 hour shift!

Sat, 10/22/2011 - 9AM-12Noon - Second Harvest Food Bank (San Carlos)
 

 

Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers
Sustainable agriculture is labor intensive. Full Circle Farm is a fledgling start up non-profit, we will be working to help the farm crew with tasks such as weeding, mulching, thinning and other light labor in the row crops and fruit orchard.  We will be outdoors the whole time on uneven, dirty and possibly muddy ground so please wear sun protection, bring water bottles, and wear clothes that will keep you cool or warm enough and sturdy shoes. This event is rain or shine.  You will get dirty but it is fun!

Sun, 10/23/2011 - 10AM-1PM - Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers (Sunnyvale)
 
 

Day of the Dead Family Art Day (Palo Alto)

Palo Alto's Day of the Dead celebration is free to the community! Guests enjoy hands-on art activities, performances, gallery activities, food, music and storytelling for the whole family. Drop by the Children’s Library and the Junior Museum and Zoo for activities throughout the day! One Brick will be helping "behind the scenes" at Community Celebration Family Fun Day at the Palo Alto Art Center. We will be keeping the art stations stocked with supplies and helping families make Day of the Dead themed art projects.

Sun, 10/30/2011 - 1PM-5PM - Day of the Dead Family Art Day (Palo Alto)
 

RAFT: Sorting for Schools (Sunnyvale)
One Brick will help Resource Area for Teaching (RAFT) sort community and corporate donations, assemble project packets for classroom use, and get a look at all the cool stuff they do with donations. RAFT is the MacGyver of non-profits! We will also have the opportunity to tour the warehouse. We will see how RAFT is helping our teachers and how our work will directly impact the teaching community.

Sat, 10/22/2011 - 9AM-12Noon - RAFT: Sorting for Schools (Sunnyvale)

Thu, 11/3/2011 - 6PM-8PM - RAFT: Sorting for Schools (Sunnyvale)
 
 


 

Event Recaps

Pumpkins in the Park (South Bay)

 One Brick volunteers helped out in the Discovery Meadow in downtown San Jose for Pumpkins in the Park.  We assisted with various booths and tables including information, donations, cutting apples for apple tasting and purchasing tickets for the bouncing houses.  Afterwards, we headed over to Peggy Sue's for root beer floats, fries, burgers and liquid refreshments.
 

Second Harvest Food Bank (San Jose)
This Saturday, we had a bunch of enthusiastic One Brickers, new and current, at the Second Harvest Food Bank all ready to pack pounds of food. We started off with our usual One Brick briefing and ice breaker where we talk about our dream vacation destinations. Paris anyone? Finally, the work came and it was all about sorting and packing green Granny Smith apples into bags and then into boxes when we ran out of bags. When all was done, we sorted and packed hundreds and hundreds of green apples for the community. All that work also made us hungry, so we made our way to a taqueria near by for more socializing.
