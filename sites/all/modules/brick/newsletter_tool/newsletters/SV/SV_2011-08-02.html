Social Event: Bus Trip to Sonoma Valley ($25)

Road Trip!

On Saturday Aug 6th, we are heading out to Sonoma Valley. Space is limited.

Note: To reserve a space please make sure you buy a ticket using Google Checkout on the event link. If you don't pay for a seat we will not be able to hold space for you on the bus.

Details (may change slightly as we pull things together):

Cost: $25 per person which covers the bus rental only. It does not include tasting fees.

8:30am - Pickup in Mountain View (Google Building 46)
9:30am -Pickup in San Francisco (8th and Market)
Ravenswood - Ravenswood is offering us a tour and tasting for $15 a person.  We will begin the day with a white wine, head out out into the vineyard and taste an estate zin, then we will the go inside for a barrel tasting and to see the fermentation area
Sebastiani  - The Presentation Tour and Tasting is offered for $15. It includes a short tour of Sebastiani Vineyards and Winery’s Hospitality Center and Winemaking Facility. Following the tour your guide will lead you through a tasting of Sebastiani’s public wine list.
Lunch in Sonoma - You may have a picnic at Sebastiani's picnic area or you may walk to one of the restaurants nearby in downtown Sonoma for lunch.
Nicholson Ranch - $15 includes tastings and a tour of the vineyards, fermentation facilities and caves.
5:30pm - Drop off in San Francisco
6:30pm - Drop off in Mountain View

Social Event: Bus Trip to Sonoma Valley ($25) - Sat, 8/6/2011
space limited

AT&T San Jose Jazz Festival (San Jose)

San Jose Salsa Festival (San Jose)
Alum Rock Village Business Association will stage the fourth annual salsa festival.  Each festival features several live Salsa bands, Salsa-dancing instruction and a dance contest, a Salsa-tasting contest featuring the salsas of six local Mexican restaurants, a special stage for child-oriented performances, children’s crafts and hands-on science-focused demonstrations, about 60 booths (community non-profits, information providers, great food vendors, merchandise vendors, etc.) and an opportunity for a tremendous celebration of music, food and family.  No admission is charged.  The al fresco dining area and the two stages offer shaded seating.  For their 2010 event, they drew about 15,000 guests from 46 different zip codes. 

Sat, 8/20/2011 - 11:45AM-3PM - San Jose Salsa Festival (San Jose)
 

End of Summer Sweep at Hidden Villa Hostel

A stay at Hidden Villa Hostel offers the quiet charm of country life with easy access to the city lights of Silicon Valley. Founded by Josephine and Frank Duveneck in 1937, this hostel is the oldest operating hostel in the United States. The main building, office and bathrooms, rebuilt in 2001, showcase environmentally sustainable technology and design.

One Brick will be helping the staff prepare for the autumn season by pulling furnishings out of storage and returning them to the hostel.

Wed, 8/24/2011 - 6PM-8:30PM  - End of Summer Sweep at Hidden Villa Hostel (Los Altos Hills)
