Event Management Informational Meeting

The limit on how many events we can put on, and how many community organizations we can serve, is the number of Event Managers and Coordinators we have to staff events.

Upcoming Events

Our City Forest New Nursery Work Day (San Jose)
Our City Forest's nursery is where we house all of our trees before they are planted in San Jose.  One Brick will help with irrigation, repotting, pruning, weeding, early tree care and tree stake/tool maintenance, all in preparation for upcoming plantings!

Sat, 5/26/2012 - 9AM-12Noon - Our City Forest New Nursery Work Day (San Jose)

 

Summer Sweep at Hidden Villa Hostel
A stay at Hidden Villa Hostel offers the quiet charm of country life with easy access to the city lights of Silicon Valley. Founded by Josephine and Frank Duveneck in 1937, this hostel is the oldest operating hostel in the United States. The main building, office and bathrooms, rebuilt in 2001, showcase environmentally sustainable technology and design. 

One Brick will be helping the staff prepare for the summer season by packing up the old and bringing in the new!

Wed, 5/30/2012 - 5:30PM-8:30PM - Summer Sweep at Hidden Villa Hostel (Los Altos Hills)

 

Veggielution Community Farming & Potluck Lunch
On each workday, they do tasks such as planting, weeding, composting, harvesting, and more. You don't have to have any previous gardening experience, they will show you what to do. If you do have gardening experience, they would love to learn from you! 

All volunteers are encouraged to take home some food, up to one grocery bag per person. Work In = Food Out. The rest of the produce is distributed through their various programs, or sold at their low cost Farmstand.

Sat, 6/2/2012 - 10AM-12:30PM - Veggielution Community Farming & Potluck Lunch (San Jose)

 

New Almaden Trail at Almaden Quicksilver
June 2 celebrates the American Hiking Society's National Trails Day.

Come help our expert County Parks Trail Crew with new trail construction, tread work and brushing. You’ll work along some of the most beautiful and pristine trails in Santa Clara Valley and you will also learn the art and science of professional trail work.

Sat, 6/2/2012 - 9AM-1PM - National Trails day - New Almaden Trail at Almaden Quicksilver (San Jose)

 

Repair a Bike for a Kid in Need (Mountain View)
Here's your chance to learn some new skills or to use your old ones while repairing bikes for deserving children!

Volunteer tasks can include cleaning bikes, repairing bikes, and/or tearing down bikes. You'll get to select a project bike that looks like a good match for your skills. If you're a new volunteer or are just looking to learn more about bike repair, you can ask to be paired up with an experienced volunteer - these folks are usually wearing a green name badge. Don't be afraid to ask for help if you get stuck or have any questions! Tools will be provided.

Sat, 6/9/2012 - 10AM-2PM - Repair a Bike for a Kid in Need (Mountain View)

Take Steps for Crohn's and Colitis (San Jose)
