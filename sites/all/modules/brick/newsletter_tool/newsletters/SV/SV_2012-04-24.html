Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen Helpers (San Jose)
Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen, a non-profit organization has been serving the working poor, families, homeless and seniors living in and around the downtown and East Side of San Jose for close to 30 years. What began as a simple first meal of fish and bread for 11 adults and 15 children has grown to nearly 500 meals per day, served with dignity. These no-cost hot meals are offered to their clients in a safe, caring, and friendly environment.

Wed, 4/25/2012 - 3:15PM-6PM - Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen Helpers (San Jose)

Event Recaps

San Jose Project All day event
One Brick volunteers participated in the first ever San Jose Project event where we spent a glorious Saturday cleaning bridges of trash and painting over graffiti.  A few local residents joined in too!  Parts of San Jose now look all the better for our work, and half our team won cosmetics prizes and vouchers at the end of the day.

Indulge Your Inner Artist Family Day
Volunteers were inspired by the art projects, and assisted children & their families for the event by demonstating ways to put together and decorate the projects.  Kids and volunteers used grocery bags, plastic bottles, wooden sticks, cardboard, twigs and tissue paper to create marvelous boats.  With the volunteers assistance, children sailed out of the sight with their handmade boats and joy.  Old magazines were turned into decorative flowers.  A boy came to our volunteer to learn how to make flower for his mom.  What a wonderful way to celebrate Earth Day held at the beautiful Lucy Evans Baylands Nature Interpretive Center in the Palo Alto Baylands!

Nicolosi Jazz Gala (Alum Rock)
Our volunteers sold raffle tickets and supervised the silent auction item area during the jazz concert for this first Alum Rock Jazz Band fundraiser at the Evergreen Valley Community College Theater in San Jose.  A couple of our volunteers won a few of the raffle prizes, which was a fun surprise.
