Upcoming Events

Helping Hands at Sacred Heart (San Jose)

One Brick will help Sacred Heart prepare for another week of helping those in need. This may working in Louise's Pantry and Gracie's Clothes Closet. In the pantry, we will primarily make dried food bags of rice, beans, canned goods, cereals, toilet paper, etc. In the clothes closet, we will receive clothing donations, sort through donations, and hang clothes on racks. Some cleaning may also be involved.

Sat, 6/11/2011 - 9AM-12Noon - Helping Hands at Sacred Heart (San Jose)

Sat, 6/18/2011 - 9AM-12Noon - Helping Hands at Sacred Heart (San Jose)
 


 
 

Silicon Valley Duck Race helpers (Los Gatos)

The Silicon Valley Duck Race is a coalition of local non-profits that work together to raise funds and build awareness for their agencies. We hold a family friendly festival where we race 15,000 rubber ducks.

Sun, 6/12/2011 - 7:30AM-10:30AM - Silicon Valley Duck Race helpers (Vasona Lake Park/Los Gatos)
  

Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)

Love to knit (or crochet) and like to socialize???  This is the perfect event for you.  Please join One Brick in helping the Sunnyvale Community Services make scarves and hats for their seniors for the holidays. Don't know how to knit or crochet?  This is the perfect opportunity to learn.  We will have a few experienced people who will teach us how to knit and/or crochet. Please feel free to bring your own needles, hooks, yarn etc.  However, these are not required; the Sunnyvale Community Services has given us yarn to use.  We will try to have extra needles and hooks on hand.

Tue, 6/14/2011 - 6:30PM-9PM - Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)
 
 

Luminaria set-up for Relay For Life

One Brick volunteers will assist in setting up over a thousand luminarias. The luminarias are white lunch bags that are decorated, filled with sand, and then either have a glow stick or candle in them - they are either in memory or in honor of someone with cancer.

Sat, 6/18/2011 - 7:30PM-9PM - Luminaria set-up for Relay For Life (Milpitas)

Sat, 6/25/2011 - 6PM-8:30PM - Luminaria set-up for Relay For Life (Fremont)
 
 

 

Event Recaps

Sunset Celebration with CHC (Menlo Park)

Sunday, One Brick volunteers helped out at the Sunset Celebration, which benefits the Children's Health Council.  We worked at various booths including the Sunset Wine Booth, California Travel Council, and numerous beer & wine booths.  Additionally, some volunteers were at the Hawaiian Airlines where attendees made their own leis.  After the event, volunteers walked around to see the exhibits and sample the food.
 

RAFT: Sorting for Schools (Sunnyvale)

One Brickers volunteers were back at RAFT (Resource Area for Teaching) in Sunnyvale on Thursday night.  We worked sorting gold foil, books, CD/DVDs and putting together various kits.  We had a strong turn out for the after event social at St. Johns, where they were showing the NBA Finals. 

12th Annual SVTC Kids Triathlon (Cupertino)

One Brick volunteers ignored the forecast of inclement weather and turned up at 7:30am Sunday morning to run registration at the 12th Annual SVTC Kids Triathlon at De Anza College. Over a thousand kids participated in the event.
