Upcoming Events

11th Annual SVTC Kids Triathlon (Cupertino)

The 12th Annual Silicon Valley Kids Triathlon will be held on Sunday, June 5th at De Anza College in Cupertino. One Brick volunteers are needed to staff the registration area.  Anyone who has worked this event can tell you that it is a BLAST!! You see lots of kids in action, have some good food, and get that great feeling that comes from giving back to our community. All net proceeds go to the Silicon Valley Children's Fund.

Sun, 6/5/2011 - 7:30AM-11:30AM - 11th Annual SVTC Kids Triathlon (Cupertino)
 

Sunset Celebration with CHC (Menlo Park)

Sunset Celebration Weekend is Sunset's annual event where Sunset shares cooking, gardening and home improvement tips with the community. In exchange for our help in staffing volunteers, Children's Health Council receives a generous donation from Sunset. Over 20,000 people are expected to attend the cooking demonstrations, home improvement booths and hands-on activities for children.

Sun, 6/5/2011 - 9AM-1PM - Sunset Celebration with CHC (Menlo Park)
 

Art in Action: Sorting for Schools (Menlo Park)

One Brick will help Art in Action assemble boxes of art supplies for K-8 classroom use. Each ART box holds enough supplies to teach 12 program level-specific lessons to a class of 24 students and includes materials like paper, templates, sequins, feathers, yarn, and beads.

Tue, 6/7/2011 - 6PM-8PM - Art in Action: Sorting for Schools (Menlo Park)
 

Helping Hands at Sacred Heart (San Jose)

One Brick will help Sacred Heart prepare for another week of helping those in need. This may working in Louise's Pantry and Gracie's Clothes Closet. In the pantry, we will primarily make dried food bags of rice, beans, canned goods, cereals, toilet paper, etc. In the clothes closet, we will receive clothing donations, sort through donations, and hang clothes on racks. Some cleaning may also be involved. 

Sat, 6/11/2011 - 9AM-12Noon - Helping Hands at Sacred Heart (San Jose)

Sat, 6/18/2011 - 9AM-12Noon - Helping Hands at Sacred Heart (San Jose)
 
 

 

Silicon Valley Duck Race helpers (Los Gatos)

The Silicon Valley Duck Race is a coalition of local non-profits that work together to raise funds and build awareness for their agencies. We hold a family friendly festival where we race 15,000 rubber ducks .

Sun, 6/12/2011 - 7:30AM-10:30AM - Silicon Valley Duck Race helpers (Vasona Lake Park/Los Gatos)
 

Event Recaps

Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)

One Brickers gathered together at the Panera in Sunnyvale on a previous Tuesday to learn and also to refine their knitting or crocheting skills.  They used yarn provided by Sunnyvale Community Services to make clothing items.  Join us at the next event:  Tue, 6/14/2011 - 6:30PM-9PM 

Full Circle Educational Farm helpers (Sunnyvale)

 One Brick farm helpers spent a glorious Sunday morning  liberating squash plants of bandweed.  We were treated to a tour of the educational farm, and some us got our veg shopping at the farmstand, before grabbing a bite to eat together.
