One Brick Trivia Night (Mt. View)
The holidays are officially over, and the weather is disturbingly nice for this time of year, so we here at One Brick have decided the best way to celebrate is with the first Trivia Night of the year.

We are sure you have spent the last couple of months brushing up on your knowledge of useless fluff, so now's your chance to show it off! Come join some fellow One Brickers for drinks, food, and ferocious competition. The questions will range from the impossible to the absurd, you will meet some lovely people, and you will get to spend the evening with two charming hosts. Did we mention there are prizes...?

Bring yourself, some money for food and drinks, and your best game face. We are also always droolingly appreciative of donations to support our fantastic organization.

Thu, 3/29/2012 - 7:30PM-9PM - One Brick Trivia Night (Mt. View)

Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers (Sunnyvale)
Sustainable agriculture is labor intensive. Full Circle Farm is a fledgling start up non-profit, we will be working to help the farm crew with tasks such as weeding, mulching, thinning and other light labor in the row crops and fruit orchard.

We will be outdoors the whole time on uneven, dirty and possibly muddy ground so please wear sun protection, bring water bottles, and wear clothes that will keep you cool or warm enough and sturdy shoes. This event is rain or shine.  You may get dirty but it is fun!

Sun, 3/18/2012 - 10AM-1PM - Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers (Sunnyvale)

 
 
 

Rebuilding Together (Redwood City)
One Brick will help Rebuilding Together Peninsula prepare for the National Rebuilding Day in April. For this one-day rebuilding event, thousands of volunteers and corporate sponsors unite to rehabilitate the homes and community facilities of our low-income neighbors and revitalize communities across the Peninsula.

We will sort construction supplies into buckets and bags. Materials include paint cans, brushes, goggles, sandpaper, etc... These materials will be distributed and used for construction projects taking place on National Rebuilding Day, on April 28.

Sat, 3/24/2012 - 9AM-1PM - Rebuilding Together: Sorting for National Rebuilding Day
  


 
 

Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen Helpers (San Jose)
Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen, a non-profit organization has been serving the working poor, families, homeless and seniors living in and around the downtown and East Side of San Jose for close to 30 years. What began as a simple first meal of fish and bread for 11 adults and 15 children has grown to nearly 500 meals per day, served with dignity. These no-cost hot meals are offered to our clients in a safe, caring, and friendly environment.

Wed, 3/28/2012 - 3:15PM-6PM - Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen Helpers (San Jose)

Event Recaps

La Mesa Verde Installation day (San Jose)
It was a busy Saturday morning as usual at Sacred Heart with the warehouse full of volunteers sorting food and clothes plus One Brick volunteers starting with the La Mesa Verde installation day. We were provided a briefing on what we were going to do for the day and this involved installing the planting bed boxes, adding planting soil and installing the drip irrigation system.

After the briefing, we proceeded to gather the tools needed and then we formed 2 teams to carpool to 2 locations. These planting bed boxes provide families that sign up with the program a source of inexpensive organic vegetables. Both teams successfully installed a total of 4 planting bed boxes to provide these families healthy vegetables.
