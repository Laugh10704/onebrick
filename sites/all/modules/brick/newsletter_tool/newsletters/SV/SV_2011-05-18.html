Upcoming Events

Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen Helpers (San Jose)

Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen, a non-profit organization has been serving the working poor, families, homeless and seniors living in and around the downtown and East Side of San Jose for close to 30 years. What began as a simple first meal of fish and bread for 11 adults and 15 children has grown to nearly 500 meals per day, served with dignity. These no-cost hot meals are offered to our clients in a safe, caring, and friendly environment. Volunteers at Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen may be asked to help with all aspects of the meals, including setting up our dining room for dinner, preparing the meal, serving and clean up.

Wed, 5/25/2011 - 3:15PM-6PM - Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen Helpers (San Jose)
 
 

Take Steps for Crohn's and Colitis (San Jose)

One in every 200 Americans suffer daily with Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis. Throughout the year, Take Steps enables patient families to join together with their local community to build visibility and awareness about these digestive diseases while raising critical funds. Participants will enjoy a casual 2-3 mile stroll in a festival-like environment with food, music and fun! Our patients often suffer in silence -- Take Steps participants are encouraged to make noise and be heard. One Brick will assist at the welcome/registration tent and the T-shirt tent.

Sat, 5/21/2011 - 12Noon-4PM - Take Steps for Crohn's and Colitis (San Jose)

Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers (Sunnyvale)

Sustainable agriculture is labor intensive. Full Circle Farm is a fledgling start up non-profit, we will be working to help the farm crew with tasks such as weeding, mulching, thinning and other light labor in the row crops and fruit orchard. We will be outdoors the whole time on uneven, dirty and possibly muddy ground so please wear sun protection, bring water bottles, and wear clothes that will keep you cool or warm enough and sturdy shoes. This event is rain or shine.  You will get dirty but it is fun!

Sun, 5/29/2011 - 10AM-1PM - Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers (Sunnyvale)
 
 

Event Recaps

Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)

One Brickers gathered together at the Panera in Sunnyvale on a previous Tuesday to learn and also to refine their knitting or crocheting skills.  They used yarn provided by Sunnyvale Community Services to make clothing items.
 

One Brick SV 2nd Trivia night (Mt. View)

Last Thursday, One Brick volunteers used their vast reserves of knowledge for the OB Silicon Valley 2nd Trivia Night at Kapp's in Mt. View.  The Beer Swillers International emerged victorious in the battle.  Participants also enjoyed beer, pizza, chocolate and candy, while watching the San Jose Sharks win their decisive 7th game with the Detroit Red Wings.
 

Operation Care & Comfort (San Jose)

One Brick volunteers gathered to help assemble these care packages to be sent to "adopted" units of deployed U.S. military service members, serving in Iraq, Afghanistan, and other conflict regions. Operation Care & Comfort are currently supporting up to 200 units on a monthly basis.
