Social Event: Bus Trip to Sonoma Valley

Road Trip!

On Saturday Aug 6th, we are heading out to Sonoma Valley. Space is limited.

Note: To reserve a space please make sure you buy a ticket using Google Checkout on the event link. If you don't pay for a seat we will not be able to hold space for you on the bus.

Details (may change slightly as we pull things together):

Cost: $25 per person.

- We will leave from Mountain View at 9am and return at 6pm
- We'll visit 3-4 wineries and including a stop in Sonoma for lunch
- People are encouraged to bring a picnic (we'll be stopping at Sebastiani picinic grounds).
- Sonoma Town Square is 5 minutes away if you'd prefer to visit a local restaurant.
- $25 covered bus rental only. It does not include tasting fees.
- Tasting wine it totally optional (but a lot of fun).
- Most wineries charge about $10 per person.
- We are talking to wineries to arrange optional tours.
- More details to follow.

Social Event: Bus Trip to Sonoma Valley ($25) - Sat, 8/6/2011
space limited

Upcoming Events

Helping Hands at Sacred Heart (San Jose)

One Brick will help Sacred Heart prepare for another week of helping those in need. This may working in Louise's Pantry and Gracie's Clothes Closet. In the pantry, we will primarily make dried food bags of rice, beans, canned goods, cereals, toilet paper, etc. In the clothes closet, we will receive clothing donations, sort through donations, and hang clothes on racks. Some cleaning may also be involved.

Sat, 7/23/2011 - 9AM-12Noon - Helping Hands at Sacred Heart (San Jose)
 
 

Breathe California's Bike for Breath Fundraiser

Bike for Breath is a fundraising event for Breathe California, serving San Francisco, San Mateo, Marin Counties and the greater Bay Area. This is an annual bike ride that the whole family can enjoy with four "breath-giving" routes, 10, 18, 50 or 62 miles!
One Brick will be Staffing the Kid’s Zone (any face painters would be great!), cheering the returning riders at course monitor locations and at the Finish Line, helping with food distribution, and assisting with general clean-up after the event is finished.

Sat, 7/23/2011 - 10AM-2PM - Breathe California's Bike for Breath Fundraiser (Foster City)
 
 

Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers (Sunnyvale)

Sustainable agriculture is labor intensive. Full Circle Farm is a fledgling start up non-profit, we will be working to help the farm crew with tasks such as weeding, mulching, thinning and other light labor in the row crops and fruit orchard.

Sun, 7/31/2011 - 10AM-1PM - Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers (Sunnyvale)
 
 


 

San Mateo County Health Foundation's Fundraiser

Event Recaps

Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)

One Brick volunteers enjoyed a leisurely evening by enjoying coffee, tea, food and conversing at Panera in Sunnyvale, while making scarves, hat and other items with the yarn donated by Sunnyvale Community Services.  The completed items will be donated to seniors and the volunteers expertise ranged from the inexperienced to the advanced.
 

Helping Hands at Sacred Heart (San Jose)

Saturday morning, One Brick volunteers helped out at Sacred Heart in the downtown San Jose area.  We helped fill hundreds of bags of groceries for the hungry, which will be distributed during the week.  Additionally, we also bagged carrots and lettuce for distribution.  Afterwards, we went to the nearby Panera for food, drink and conversation.
  

Christmas in July (San Jose)

We did a great job of going with the flow, being flexible and efficient, and pitching in wherever and however we were needed at the benefit for Christmas in the Park in the plaza area between the San Jose Museum of Art and the Fairmont Hotel. We loved seeing folks taking out appetizers and entrees, pitching in to run carts out to Safeway, teaming up spontaneousy, clearing tables, registering attendees and answering questions, changing direction on a moments notice, and finding things to do without prompting.  We also did a great job in the silent auction area during the bidding and also for the check out.
