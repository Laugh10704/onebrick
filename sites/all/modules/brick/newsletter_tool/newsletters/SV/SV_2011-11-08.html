One Brick SV Trivia night (Mountain View)
It's been awhile...time for another TRIVIA NIGHT!

Get ready to pick the dustiest corners of your brains, because all of that "useless" information you have been collecting over the years is about to have its time in the sun. The evening promises to be challenging and a whole lot of fun, especially for the people writing the questions (insert evil laugh here)...

We promise fun, laughter, prizes, and the chance to reconnect with some old faces and meet some new ones. What better way to get an early start to your weekend?

A $10 dollar donation to One Brick is highly appreciated.  Don't forget to bring extra money for food and drinks!

Everyone will be randomly assigned to a small group of 2-3 people.  Judges decisions are final! We will start the interrogation at about 7:15pm to give everyone enough time to order food and drinks and settle into teams.

Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)
Love to knit (or crochet) and like to socialize?  This is the perfect event for you.  Please join One Brick in helping the Sunnyvale Community Services make scarves and hats for their seniors for the holidays.

Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers (Sunnyvale)
Sustainable agriculture is labor intensive. Full Circle Farm is a fledgling start up non-profit, we will be working to help the farm crew with tasks such as weeding, mulching, thinning and other light labor in the row crops and fruit orchard.

We will be outdoors the whole time on uneven, dirty and possibly muddy ground so please wear sun protection, bring water bottles, and wear clothes that will keep you cool or warm enough and sturdy shoes. This event is rain or shine.  You will may get dirty but it is fun!

Sun, 11/20/2011 - 10AM-1PM - Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers (Sunnyvale)

 
 
 

Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen Helpers (San Jose)
Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen, a non-profit organization has been serving the working poor, families, homeless and seniors living in and around the downtown and East Side of San Jose for close to 30 years. What began as a simple first meal of fish and bread for 11 adults and 15 children has grown to nearly 500 meals per day, served with dignity. These no-cost hot meals are offered to our clients in a safe, caring, and friendly environment.

Volunteers at Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen may be asked to help with all aspects of the meals, including setting up our dining room for dinner, preparing the meal, serving and clean up.

Wed, 11/30/2011 - 3:15PM-6PM - Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen Helpers (San Jose)

Event Recaps

REI - Ridge Trail Service day (San Jose)
One Brick volunteers joined forces with other volunteers, REI employees, and Parks Conservancy volunteers on a cool morning to help divert rain water from the trails.  After a good hike, we got to our area to build several water bars to help rain water run-off the trails.  Mountain bikers appreciated that we were giving them more jumps, and hikers appreciated that the trail will be preserved.  We hiked back and had burritos donated by Chipotle.


 

JWPOSD Annual Fundraiser (Santa Clara)
One Brickers assisted at the JWPOSD Annual Fundraiser at the Santa Clara Marriott.  The first shift helped them to raise funds by assisting at the registration desk, handing out programs and bidder numbers, monitoring and collecting silent auction bid sheets, moving tables and displays. The second shift came in after the silent auction was closed, and helped moved all of the silent auction items off of the dance floor. Half of the team worked at the payment table, reconciling credit card charges and auction item wins, while the remainder made sure that correct auction items went home with their new owners.  Volunteers had a chance to enjoy pizza, cookies, and chips during their break time.

 

RAFT: Sorting for Schools (Sunnyvale)
One Brick volunteers helped out at the Sunnyvale warehouse on Thursday evening.  We helped check and prepare various types of kits that will distributed for a future RAFT exhibition.  After our shift, volunteers went to St. John's Bar & Grill for some nourishment, drinks and conversation.
