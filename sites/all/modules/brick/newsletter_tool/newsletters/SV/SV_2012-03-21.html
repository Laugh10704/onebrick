One Brick Trivia Night (Mt. View)
The holidays are officially over, and the weather is disturbingly nice for this time of year, so we here at One Brick have decided the best way to celebrate is with the first Trivia Night of the year.

We are sure you have spent the last couple of months brushing up on your knowledge of useless fluff, so now's your chance to show it off! Come join some fellow One Brickers for drinks, food, and ferocious competition. The questions will range from the impossible to the absurd, you will meet some lovely people, and you will get to spend the evening with two charming hosts. Did we mention there are prizes...?

Bring yourself, some money for food and drinks, and your best game face. We are also always droolingly appreciative of donations to support our fantastic organization.

Thu, 3/29/2012 - 7:30PM-9PM - One Brick Trivia Night (Mt. View)

Upcoming Events

Rebuilding Together
One Brick will help Rebuilding Together Peninsula prepare for the National Rebuilding Day in April. For this one-day rebuilding event, thousands of volunteers and corporate sponsors unite to rehabilitate the homes and community facilities of our low-income neighbors and revitalize communities across the Peninsula.

We will sort construction supplies into buckets and bags. Materials include paint cans, brushes, goggles, sandpaper, etc... These materials will be distributed and used for construction projects taking place on National Rebuilding Day, on April 28.

Sat, 3/24/2012 - 9AM-1PM - Rebuilding Together: Sorting for National Rebuilding Day (Redwood City)

Operation Care & Comfort (San Jose)
Since April 2003, Operation: Care and Comfort has shipped over 711,000 pounds of care packages for distribution to Marine, Army, Air Force, Navy and Coast Guard personnel deployed in the conflict regions.  Join One Brick as we help assemble these care packages to be sent to "adopted" units of deployed U.S. military service members, serving in Iraq, Afghanistan, and other conflict regions. Operation Care & Comfort are currently supporting up to 200 units on a monthly basis.

Sun, 4/1/2012 - 10AM-1PM - Operation Care & Comfort (San Jose)

Veggielution Community Farming & Potluck Lunch
On each workday, they do tasks such as planting, weeding, composting, harvesting, and more. You don't have to have any previous gardening experience, they will show you what to do. If you do have gardening experience, they would love to learn from you!

All volunteers are encouraged to take home some food, up to one grocery bag per person. Work In = Food Out. The rest of the produce is distributed through our various programs, or sold at their low cost Farmstand.

Event Recaps

One Brick Knits (Sunnyvale)
One Brick volunteers completed 10 hats and 18 scarves for activity last Tuesday at Panera Bread.  The clothing items will be donated to Sacred Heart Community Services in San Jose.  The volunteers worked, chatted, ate and drank during the event.  Additionally, they also taught each other techniques to be used in the present and future.

 

Second Harvest Food Bank (San Jose)

Despite the cold, cloudy and wet weather, dedicated volunteers braved the elements to help at Second Harvest Food Bank on Saturday morning. The Food Bank will be moving to its new digs in North San Jose on North First Street near 237. This was last time we're working at this facility on Curtner. After the usual briefings, we proceeded to box fresh long beans. This required sorting through the beans to remove the moldy ones while others packed the filled boxes on crates. We cranked through almost 7,000 pounds of green beans and then headed for nearby, delicious Mexican food.
