Schwab Cup
This weekend, One Brick will be walking the green with golfing legends at the Charles Schwab Cup Championship!  The culmination of this season's Champions Tour, the Charles Schwab Cup will determine the Tour's leading player out of a field of 30 golfers including Tom Lehman, John Cook, Mark Calcavecchia, Peter Senior and Russ Cochran.

We'll take in the finale at San Francisco's Harding Park, a beautiful course ranked by Golf Digest as one of the "Best Places to Play," with a majestic treeline and views of the surrounding lake.  Join us this Saturday and Sunday for all the birdies and bogeys!

One Brick will be staffing concession stands, selling snacks, pop and beer, running registers, restocking/icing product and assisting with general clean-up/maintenance in the area. Please wear a clean white polo shirt with khaki pants, capris or shorts (must be to the knee).  Please also wear comfortable closed toed shoes as we will be on our feet for most of the shift.

This event is a fund raiser for One Brick to support its non-profit work. Volunteers will be free to enjoy the event after their shift.

Sat, 11/5/2011 - 9AM-1PM - Schwab Cup-Shift 1 (San Francisco)

Sat, 11/5/2011 - 1PM-5PM - Schwab Cup-Shift 2 (San Francisco)

Sun, 11/6/2011 - 9AM-1PM - Schwab Cup-Shift 1 (San Francisco)

Sun, 11/6/2011 - 1PM-5PM - Schwab Cup-Shift 2 (San Francisco)

 
 
 
 
 
 
 


 

One Brick SV Trivia night (Mountain View)

It's been awhile...time for another TRIVIA NIGHT!

Get ready to pick the dustiest corners of your brains, because all of that "useless" information you have been collecting over the years is about to have its time in the sun. The evening promises to be challenging and a whole lot of fun, especially for the people writing the questions (insert evil laugh here)...

We promise fun, laughter, prizes, and the chance to reconnect with some old faces and meet some new ones. What better way to get an early start to your weekend?

A $10 dollar donation to One Brick is highly appreciated.  Don't forget to bring extra money for food and drinks!

Everyone will be randomly assigned to a small group of 2-3 people.  Judges decisions are final! We will start the interrogation at about 7:15pm to give everyone enough time to order food and drinks and settle into teams.

Thu, 11/17/2011 - 6:30PM-8:30PM - One Brick SV Trivia night (Mountain View)

JWPOSD Annual Fundraiser-Shift 2 (Santa Clara)
The annual fundraising dinner is an event hosted by the parents of the school as a way to say "thank you" to the school. No child should be denied the benefit of the first class education that JWPOSD provides deaf children. It is the annual fund raiser that is hosted by parents and volunteers that generates funds to enable to provide this much needed financial assistance to families in need.

One Brick will help with check-in and registration, man the drinks station and accept vouchers for alcoholic drinks, and assist with the silent and live auctions. Some assignments will require data entry skills. If you would be able to assist with those assignments, please indicate this in your RSVP. Training will be provided at the beginning of he event.

Sat, 11/5/2011 - 8PM-12Mid - JWPOSD Annual Fundraiser-Shift 2 (Santa Clara)

Event Recaps

Day of the Dead Family Art Day (Peninsula)
On Sunday afternoon, One Brick volunteers helped out at the Day of the Dead Family Art Day at the Lucie Stern Community Center in Palo Alto.  We assisted the instructors, kids and their parents with various activities including the calaveras de azucar (sugar skulls), Mexican Tin Art, Minature Food Art, papel picado (perforated paper), paper hat with flowers and face painting.  Afterwards, some of us went to a nearby Peet's for some liquid hydration and conversation.

 

Second Harvest Food Bank (Peninsula)
On a previous Saturday morning at Second Harvest in San Carlos, we labeled bins for the upcoming food drive and sorted canned peaches and mixed fruit.
