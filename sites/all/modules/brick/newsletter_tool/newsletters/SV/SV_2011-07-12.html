Social Event: Bus Trip to Sonoma Valley ($25)

Road Trip!

On Saturday (Aug 6th), we are heading out to Sonoma Valley. Space is limited.

Note: To reserve a space please make sure you buy a ticket using Google Checkout on the event link. If you don't pay for a seat we will not be able to hold space for you on the bus.

Details (may change slightly as we pull things together):

Cost: $25 per person.

- We will leave from Mountain View at 9am and return at 6pm
- We'll visit 3-4 wineries and including a stop in Sonoma for lunch
- People are encouraged to bring a picnic (we'll be stopping at Sebastiani picinic grounds).
- Sonoma Town Square is 5 minutes away if you'd prefer to visit a local restaurant.
- $25 covered bus rental only. It does not include tasting fees.
- Tasting wine it totally optional (but a lot of fun).
- Most wineries charge about $10 per person.
- We are talking to wineries to arrange optional tours.
- More details to follow.

Upcoming Events

Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)

Love to knit (or crochet) and like to socialize???  This is the perfect event for you.  Please join One Brick in helping the Sunnyvale Community Services make scarves and hats for their seniors for the holidays. Don't know how to knit or crochet?  This is the perfect opportunity to learn.  We will have a few experienced people who will teach us how to knit and/or crochet. Please feel free to bring your own needles, hooks, yarn etc.  However, these are not required; the Sunnyvale Community Services has given us yarn to use.  We will try to have extra needles and hooks on hand.

Tue, 7/12/2011 - 6:30PM-9PM - Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)
 
 

Helping Hands at Sacred Heart (San Jose)

One Brick will help Sacred Heart prepare for another week of helping those in need. This may working in Louise's Pantry and Gracie's Clothes Closet. In the pantry, we will primarily make dried food bags of rice, beans, canned goods, cereals, toilet paper, etc. In the clothes closet, we will receive clothing donations, sort through donations, and hang clothes on racks. Some cleaning may also be involved.

Sat, 7/16/2011 - 9AM-12Noon - Helping Hands at Sacred Heart (San Jose)
 

Bike for Breath Fundraiser (Foster City)

Bike for Breath is a fundraising event for Breathe California, serving San Francisco, San Mateo, Marin Counties and the greater Bay Area. This is an annual bike ride that the whole family can enjoy with four "breath-giving" routes, 10, 18, 50 or 62 miles!
One Brick will be Staffing the Kid’s Zone (any face painters would be great!), cheering the returning riders at course monitor locations and at the Finish Line, helping with food distribution, and assisting with general clean-up after the event is finished.

Sat, 7/23/2011 - 10AM-2PM - Breathe California's Bike for Breath Fundraiser (Foster City)
 
 

Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen Helpers (San Jose)

Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen, a non-profit organization has been serving the working poor, families, homeless and seniors living in and around the downtown and East Side of San Jose for close to 30 years. What began as a simple first meal of fish and bread for 11 adults and 15 children has grown to nearly 500 meals per day, served with dignity. These no-cost hot meals are offered to our clients in a safe, caring, and friendly environment.

Wed, 7/27/2011 - 3:15PM-6PM - Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen Helpers (San Jose)
 

Full Circle Educational Farm helpers (Sunnyvale)

Sustainable agriculture is labor intensive. Full Circle Farm is a fledgling start up non-profit, we will be working to help the farm crew with tasks such as weeding, mulching, thinning and other light labor in the row crops and fruit orchard.

Sun, 7/31/2011 - 10AM-1PM - Full Circle Educational Farm helpers (Sunnyvale)
 
 

Event Recaps

Full Circle Educational Farm helpers (Sunnyvale)

On a previous Sunday morning, One Brick volunteers returned to Full Circle farm. This time they battled the dreaded Bind Weed and worked tirelessly to give the emerging squash a fighting chance. Towards the end of the event, the team took a tour of the farm, where they admired the new pizza oven and grazed raspberries straight from the plants.


  

RAFT: Sorting for Schools (San Jose)

On Saturday morning, a team of One Brick volunteers sorted stationery, organized stock and prepared art supplies at the RAFT (Resources Area for Teachers) facility in North San Jose. 

Sorting the reems of stickers brought back memories for Mariza and Judi.  Shilpa and Parul restored order to the book shelves.  Sarah, Celine and Anne organized the stationery and art supplies - items highly valued by the teachers shopping in the store. Tisha entered wonderland upon being assigned the job of arranging the jars of colored glitter.

After the event, some of the volunteers enjoyed a light lunch at the local Panera Bread. Conversation included vacation plans, the next One Brick event at RAFT and colored glitter.
 
 
 

 

Operation Care & Comfort (South Bay)

On Sunday morning in the center of a picturesque Willow Glen, a group of One Brick volunteers assembled at the American Legion #318 on Minnesota Avenue. The One Brick volunteers successfully performed all of the tasks assigned to them.  These tasks included organizing toiletries, assembling bags of food, writing letters, packing boxes and sorting donated supplies for the US troops overseas.  One Brick volunteers were involved in all stages of the process and personally handed over sealed boxes of goods to soldiers who were on site.

 

Luminaria set-up for Relay For Life (Newark)

One Brickers joined in the reminder that "Cancer never sleeps" by helping at the Newark Relay for Life on Saturday. Luminaria bags that had been decorated by participants in honor of loved ones lost were filled with sand and candles and then placed around the track in anticipation of the Luminaria ceremony. We also filled blank bags and spelled out the word "Hope" on the bleachers. As darkness began to fall, we lit all of the candles in the bags, allowing the gentle glow to remind everyone of why they had devoted their precision time to this worthwhile cause.
