One Brick SV 2nd Trivia Night (Mt. View)

The first trivia night was so much fun we thought we'd do it again.
Clive's just bought a couple of new trivia books... Look out world! 

Sample Questions:

- If you book a hotel under "The American Plan" what do you get besides a room?
- What year did Atari introduce Pong?
- Which state is home to the oldest city in the USA? Extra point if you can name the city?
Warning: This time we will introduce the "Lightning Round". 30 questions in 3 minutes..... OMG!!! :)

There will be prizes, a few bum questions and a lot of laughter.

$10 donation requested.  Join us at Kapp's on Castro St. in Mt. View, a block from the Caltrain station.

Thu, 5/12/2011 - 6:30PM-9PM - One Brick SV 2nd Trivia Night (Mt. View)

Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)

Don't know how to knit or crochet?  This is the perfect opportunity to learn.  We will have a few experienced people who will teach us how to knit and/or crochet. Please feel free to bring your own needles, hooks, yarn etc.  However, these are not required; the Sunnyvale Community Services has given us yarn to use.  We will try to have extra needles and hooks on hand.  Join us at the Panera Bread at the corner of El Camino Real and Sunnyvale-Saratoga Rd.

Tue, 5/10/2011 - 6:30PM-9PM - Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale) 
 

Repair a Bike for a Kid in Need (Mt. View)

Here's your chance to learn some new skills or to use your old ones while repairing bikes for deserving children! The Bicycle Exchange meets about once every month to repair and organize its inventory of donated bicycles. New volunteers often learn bicycle repair skills by assisting a team of experienced volunteers. So whether you already know how to repair a bike or not, you are welcome to help!

Sat, 5/14/2011 - 10AM-2PM - Repair a Bike for a Kid in Need (Mt. View)  

Sam's Lobster Fest (Half Moon Bay)

A food and wine family extravaganza!  Get away to the coast and come join the fun at the American Legion Fairgrounds.  Enjoy a spirited oyster shucking and eating competition, taste fabulous seafood, and dance to live music. Activities include: Wine Tasting, Live Music and Dancing, Oyster Shucking and Eating Contest, Arts and Crafts Booths, Delicious Food (Seafood, Burgers and more!), Silent Auction, Kids Train and Slide, Face Painting, and Sam's ChowderMobile.

Sun, 5/15/2011 - 11:30AM-3:30PM - Sam's Lobster Fest (Half Moon Bay)
 
 

National River Clean Up Day (San Jose)

Millions of tons of trash end up in our nation's rivers and streams every year. National River Cleanup is the most popular and successful stream cleanup program in the country that taps into the civic pride of tens of thousands of volunteers across the country. Volunteers are needed to cleanup Coyote Creek at Hellyer County Park.

Sat, 5/21/2011 - 9AM-12Noon - National River Clean Up Day (San Jose) 
 

Take Steps for Crohn's and Colitis (San Jose)

One in every 200 Americans suffer daily with Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis. Throughout the year, Take Steps enables patient families to join together with their local community to build visibility and awareness about these digestive diseases while raising critical funds. Participants will enjoy a casual 2-3 mile stroll in a festival-like environment with food, music and fun! Our patients often suffer in silence -- Take Steps participants are encouraged to make noise and be heard. One Brick will assist at the welcome/registration tent and the T-shirt tent.

Sat, 5/21/2011 - 12Noon-4PM - Take Steps for Crohn's and Colitis (San Jose)
