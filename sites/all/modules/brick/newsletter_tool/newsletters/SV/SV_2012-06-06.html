Upcoming Events

Repair a Bike for a Kid in Need (Mountain View)
Here's your chance to learn some new skills or to use your old ones while repairing bikes for deserving children! Volunteer tasks include cleaning bikes, repairing bikes, and/or tearing down bikes.
 
You'll get to select a project bike that looks like a good match for your skills. If you're a new volunteer or are just looking to learn more about bike repair, you can ask to be paired up with an experienced volunteer - these folks are usually wearing a green name badge. Don't be afraid to ask for help if you get stuck or have any questions! Tools will be provided.
 
Sat, 6/9/2012 - 10AM-2PM - Repair a Bike for a Kid in Need (Mountain View)

 

Operation Care & Comfort (San Jose)
Since April 2003, Operation: Care and Comfort has shipped over 711,000 pounds of care packages for distribution to Marine, Army, Air Force, Navy and Coast Guard personnel deployed in the conflict regions.
 
Join One Brick as we help assemble these care packages to be sent to "adopted" units of deployed U.S. military service members, serving in Iraq, Afghanistan, and other conflict regions. Operation Care & Comfort are currently supporting up to 200 units on a monthly basis.
 
Sun, 6/10/2012 - 10AM-1PM - Operation Care & Comfort (San Jose)

 

Alice in Wonderland @ Full Circle Farm
The Festival Theater Ensemble (FTE: formerly Mid-Peninsula Shakespeare Festival) is returning for their second summer to Full Circle Farm. Come join us for their adaptation of Alice in Wonderland. The set has been newly built, and we've seen some of the props -- this play is going to be all sorts of awesome, and we think you should come watch. Who can pass up hookah-smoking caterpillars and zany tea parties? Not us
 

The performance is on an outdoor set and is a great way to enjoy a pre-summer evening. Donations are requested -- they recommend $10, but any amount is welcome-- however, no one is turned away as long as there is seating available for lack of funds. Seating in the past has filled quickly, so we are going to hang out for a little while before the show starts to ensure we get great seats -- and to enjoy each other's company, of course.

Sat, 6/16/2012 - 7PM-10PM - Alice in Wonderland @ Full Circle Farm (Sunnyvale)

 

 

Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers

Event Recaps

Second Harvest Food Bank New Warehouse (San Jose)

There were a big team of One Brick volunteers at the Second Harvest Food Bank New Warehouse on North First Street in San Jose on Saturday morning.  The One Brick team performed the first ever food salvage at the new facility.  From palettes of donated goods, the One Brick volunteers separated the food from the non-food items.  The food was then further divided, boxed and stacked onto palettes of beverages, canned foods, oversized products.  After the event the One Brick volunteers enjoyed a buffet-style lunch at the nearby Sweet Tomatoes.

 

New Almaden Trail at Almaden Quicksilver
You're on a trail, 3 miles from the trail head. You cross a stream on a heavy wooden bridge. Ever wonder how they got that bridge there? If the trail is in Almaden Quicksliver Park, then wonder no more! One Brick volunteers carried the lumber there on National Trails Day! Go team! Of course, the team retired to Chipotle for a well earned burrito after the activity!
