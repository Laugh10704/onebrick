Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)
Love to knit (or crochet) and like to socialize???  This is the perfect event for you.  Please join One Brick in helping make scarves and hats for seniors.  As this is our second Sip & Knit of the year, we'll decide which group we will support this year.

Veggielution Community Farming & Potluck Lunch

Event Recaps

Second Harvest Food Bank (San Carlos)
President Obama declared the previous Monday as MLK Say of Service so Clive, Taryn and other One Brickers turned out at Second Harvest to create brown bags for needy families. In less than 2 hours, we were able to create 950 bags making sure that each had exactly the right items. Afterwards, we headed out to the local Chavez Market for a well earned burrito.

 

Full Circle Educational Farm helpers (Sunnyvale)
Sunday, a small group of fantastic volunteers braved the stormy weather to help out at Full Circle Farm. We spent the day protected from the rain in the greenhouse, where we had an unofficial bean shelling competition and talked about our favorite Olympic events, New Year's Resolutions, and movies. We took a break to peruse the monthly garden share, where we were rewarded with warm cookies, fresh eggs, and fig tree clippings. We braved the customary tour around the farm and got to see the new farmstand, packing shed, and colorful signage recently provided after a large holiday volunteer effort. After the event, One Brick's newest volunteer explained the rules of cricket over lunch.

 

Second Harvest Food Bank (San Jose)
Despite the stormy weather, we had a good turnout of eager One Brickers looking to do some serious food sorting and packing at the Second Harvest Food Bank in San Jose on Saturday. After the usual briefings, we quickly got into sorting food donated over the last couple of months from Thanksgiving and Christmas food drives. Some sorted, others packed, some sealed and stacked the boxes of sorted food. After sorting and packing thousands of pounds of food, we called it a day and headed to a nearby taquería for good food and company.
