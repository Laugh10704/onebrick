Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)
Love to knit (or crochet) and like to socialize?  This is the perfect event for you.  Please join One Brick in helping the Sunnyvale Community Services make scarves and hats for their seniors for the holidays.
 
Don't know how to knit or crochet?  This is the perfect opportunity to learn.  We will have a few experienced people who will teach us how to knit and/or crochet. Please feel free to bring your own needles, hooks, yarn etc.  However, these are not required; the Sunnyvale Community Service has given us yarn to use.  We will try to have extra needles and hooks on hand.

Tue, 11/8/2011 - 6:30PM-9PM - Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)

Upcoming Events

Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers (Sunnyvale)
Sustainable agriculture is labor intensive. Full Circle Farm is a fledgling start up non-profit, we will be working to help the farm crew with tasks such as weeding, mulching, thinning and other light labor in the row crops and fruit orchard.  We will be outdoors the whole time on uneven, dirty and possibly muddy ground so please wear sun protection, bring water bottles, and wear clothes that will keep you cool or warm enough and sturdy shoes. This event is rain or shine.  You will get dirty but it is fun!

Sun, 10/23/2011 - 10AM-1PM - Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers (Sunnyvale)

 
 

Day of the Dead Family Art Day (Palo Alto)
Palo Alto's Day of the Dead celebration is free to the community! Guests enjoy hands-on art activities, performances, gallery activities, food, music and storytelling for the whole family. Drop by the Children’s Library and the Junior Museum and Zoo for activities throughout the day! One Brick will be helping "behind the scenes" at Community Celebration Family Fun Day at the Palo Alto Art Center. We will be keeping the art stations stocked with supplies and helping families make Day of the Dead themed art projects.

Sun, 10/30/2011 - 1PM-5PM - Day of the Dead Family Art Day (Palo Alto)



 

 

RAFT: Sorting for Schools (Sunnyvale)
One Brick will help Resource Area for Teaching (RAFT) sort community and corporate donations, assemble project packets for classroom use, and get a look at all the cool stuff they do with donations. RAFT is the MacGyver of non-profits! We will also have the opportunity to tour the warehouse. We will see how RAFT is helping our teachers and how our work will directly impact the teaching community.

Thu, 11/3/2011 - 6PM-8PM - RAFT: Sorting for Schools (Sunnyvale)

 
 

REI - Ridge Trail Service Day (San Jose)
Join Santa Clara County Parks and other volunteers from the Trail Center and REI Saratoga as part of the 4th Annual REI Ridge Trail Service Day.  Volunteers will work on part of the Bay Area Ridge Trail at Santa Teresa County Park above the rural Almaden Valley.

Sat, 11/5/2011 - 9AM-1PM - REI - Ridge Trail (BART) Service Day (San Jose)


 

 

JWPOSD Annual Fundraiser (Campbell)
The annual fundraising dinner is an event hosted by the parents of the school as a way to say "thank you" to the school. No child should be denied the benefit of the first class education that JWPOSD provides deaf children. It is the annual fund raiser that is hosted by parents and volunteers that generates funds to enable to provide this much needed financial assistance to families in need.

One Brick will help with check-in and registration, man the drinks station and accept vouchers for alcoholic drinks, and assist with the silent and live auctions. Some assignments will require data entry skills. If you would be able to assist with those assignments, please indicate this in your RSVP. Training will be provided at the beginning of he event.

Sat, 11/5/2011 - 5PM-9PM - JWPOSD Annual Fundraiser-Shift 1 (Campbell)

Sat, 11/5/2011 - 8PM-12Mid - JWPOSD Annual Fundraiser-Shift 2 (Campbell)

 
 

 
 

Event Recaps

Campbell Oktoberfest 5K

Onebrickers arrived early on Saturday morning to assist at the Campbell Oktoberfest 5K in Campbell Park.  We helped with the coffee, water, apples, coffee cake and cutting bananas & bagels.  Additionally, we handed out water bottles, cut off timing chips located on the participant's shoes and cheered for the finishers.  Afterwards, a group of volunteers headed to downtown Campbell to check out the Oktoberfest Festival.
 

 

Help LLS Light the Night! (Peninsula)
Saturday afternoon & evening, volunteers helped at the Light the Night event to benefit the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society              .  The event was held at Palo Alto High School.  We assisted with set-up, staffed the registration desk, handed out T-shirts and balloons, answered questions at the patient tent, and helped hand out goody bags and cheered on walkers at the finish line, all while enjoying to the rockin' Groove Kings.



 

Walk to Defeat ALS (Sunnyvale)
Sunday morning, One Brick volunteers went to Baylands Park to help at the Walk to Defeat ALS.  We helped hundreds of walkers register and cheered the participants on at the finish line, enabling ALS (Lou Gehrig's disease) to run a very successful fundraiser and awareness raiser.
