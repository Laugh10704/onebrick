News and Announcements

Volunteer Holiday Celebration (Sunnyvale)
Volunteers are the foundation of our organization.  We would not exist without you.  You guys all work so hard throughout the year, so why not take a break and hang out with your fellow One Brick volunteers without having to get dirty first?

We would like to invite you to a volunteer social at Faultline.  Come hang out, have a beverage and some food, and chat with other volunteers that you have probably seen once or a gazillion times, but don't really know so well.  If you are new to One Brick, or have been thinking about checking us out, you should definitely come too! We think that you will meet some pretty cool people.

Fri, 12/9/2011 - 7PM-9PM - Volunteer Holiday Celebration (Sunnyvale)
 
 
 

Annual Campaign
Did you know that 2011 is the 10th year One Brick has provided volunteers to community organizations that fight hunger, improve educational outcomes, clean up trails and shorelines, keep arts programs thriving, and so much more? Over the years we've contributed a staggering amount of hours to our community.

It has also been a huge amount of fun!

One Brick is FREE. We refuse to charge a membership fee. We do not charge the non-profits we support. But once a year we do ask our volunteers and supporters to help fund our efforts during the Annual Campaign.

Upcoming Events

Storytime with Santa (Campbell)
Campbell recreation center has invited Santa to read a story to local children.  There will be arts/craft tables out, and cookies/cocoa. After the story, Santa visits with the kids,and we take pictures. Volunteers will assist with arts/craft table, refreshment/beverage area, pictures with Santa and the check-in table.

Sat, 12/10/2011 - 5:30PM-9PM - Storytime with Santa (Campbell)

 
 

Art in Action: Sorting for Schools (Menlo Park)
One Brick will help Art in Action assemble boxes of art supplies for K-8 classroom use. Each ART box holds enough supplies to teach 12 program level-specific lessons to a class of 24 students and includes materials like paper, templates, sequins, feathers, yarn, and beads.

Tue, 12/13/2011 - 6PM-8PM - Art in Action: Sorting for Schools (Menlo Park)

 
 

RAFT: Sorting for Schools (Sunnyvale)
One Brick will help Resource Area for Teaching (RAFT) sort community and corporate donations, assemble project packets for classroom use, and get a look at all the cool stuff they do with donations. RAFT is the MacGyver of non-profits! We will also have the opportunity to tour the warehouse. We will see how RAFT is helping our teachers and how our work will directly impact the teaching community.

Sat, 12/17/2011 - 9AM-12Noon - RAFT: Sorting for Schools (Sunnyvale)

 
 

Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers
Sustainable agriculture is labor intensive. Full Circle Farm is a fledgling start up non-profit, we will be working to help the farm crew with tasks such as weeding, mulching, thinning and other light labor in the row crops and fruit orchard.

We will be outdoors the whole time on uneven, dirty and possibly muddy ground so please wear sun protection, bring water bottles, and wear clothes that will keep you cool or warm enough and sturdy shoes. This event is rain or shine.  You may get dirty but it is fun!

Sun, 12/18/2011 - 10AM-1PM - Full Circle Educational Farm Helpers (Sunnyvale)
 

 
 

Helping Hands at Sacred Heart (San Jose)
One Brick will help Sacred Heart prepare for another week of helping those in need. This may working in Louise's Pantry and Gracie's Clothes Closet. In the pantry, we will primarily make dried food bags of rice, beans, canned goods, cereals, toilet paper, etc.  In the clothes closet, we will receive clothing donations, sort through donations, and hang clothes on racks. Some cleaning may also be involved. 

Mon, 12/19/2011 - 3PM-6PM - Helping Hands at Sacred Heart (San Jose)
 



 

Event Recaps

Tree decoration for Santa's Workshop (Willow Glen)
Friday evening, One Brick volunteers assembled lighted reindeer and Christmas trees was the order of the night for the group working at the Tree decoration for Santa's Workshop event at the Willow Glen Community Center. We got all of the Christmas decorations out of storage and ready for Breakfast with Santa on Saturday, 12/10. Those who battled the parking in downtown Willow Glen were rewarded with really good Mexican food at Aqui.



 

Santa Saturday (San Jose)
Saturday morning, One Brick volunteers assisted the Almaden Parents' Pre-School with their Santa Saturday fundraiser.  We assisted them with arts & crafts and their games, which included Lollipop Lane, Candy Cane Hockey, Reindeer Round Up and others.  Afterwards, several of us went to a nearby taqueria for conversation, tamales, tacos and liquid refreshments.

 

Indulge your Inner Artist (Peninsula)
A group of artistically-inclinded One Brick volunteers helped out at the Cubberly Community Center in Palo Alto on Saturday afternoon.  Volunteers greeted families and helped children create amazing holiday crafts including glass ornaments, dioramas, and even a large bowling ball made out of colorful plastic bags.  This free event was organized by the Palo Alto Art Center.
