National Volunteer Week

National Volunteer Week (NVW) starts this Sunday. At One Brick, we use this week to take a pause, remember all the good work we've done together and make sure we thank all the wonderful people who volunteer with us.

If you haven't volunteered in a little while, this is a great opportunity to re-engage with your community and get out and volunteer. Simply check out the calendar, RSVP and then show up.

This year we have a special thank you for folks that volunteer during NVW. Our friends at bloomspot would like to "take you to the movies." No strings attached. Simply attend a One Brick volunteer event between Sunday April 10th and Saturday April 16th, and we'll send you a movie ticket.

Lastly, if you haven't become a member of bloomspot yet we'd like to encourage you to sign up. They are a great organization wich offers tremendous deals/opportunities. Plus, their "Community Circle" Program supports hundreds of community non-profits, including ONE BRICK.

About bloomspot: bloomspot features daily discounted offers to best merchants in your city. Look for upcoming offers including local bike rentals, full salon services, and delicious Indian cuisine. bloomspot consistently features the best restaurants, hotels, spas and more in your area.

On behalf of the board, our local chapters, teams and, most importantly, all of the 1,200 non-profits we help, thank you!

One Brick Silicon Valley Facebook Page

New contest! "Tell a Friend" about the new One Brick Silicon Valley Facebook page

It's time for the "Tell a Friend" contest to get your amazingly cool One Brick drink coozie.

The rules are easy.

1) Suggest to your friends that they "like" the One Brick Silicon Valley fan page on Facebook (you can use the share function on the bottom left of the page).

2) After they "like" the page, they just have to give you credit by typing this on the One Brick Silicon Valley wall:

I found out about this page thanks to @your_name_here.

(That's your name after the @sign -- taking advantage of the tagging function in Facebook.)

3) When there are five (5) posts on the wall tagging your name -- you've earned your coozie!

4) We'll run the contest until the end of April or the coozies run out, and you must have a Facebook profile to play.

Any questions? Okay, start earning those coozies! :-)

Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)

Love to knit (or crochet) and like to drink???  This is the perfect event for you.  Please join One Brick in helping the Sunnyvale Community Services make scarves and hats for their seniors for the holidays.

Event Recaps

Loaves & Fishes Family Kitchen Helpers (San Jose)

Last Wednesday, we completed one of our first DAYTIME EVENTS. We helped layout and then serve food (Enchilada Casserole, Salad, Chicken Noodle Soup) for 150 people. Afterwards, we helped clear up and do the dishes (all 150 of them). The folks at Catholic Charities were very warm & welcoming and the Chef was a riot.
 

Second Harvest Food Bank (San Jose)

Wednesday evening, One Brick volunteers sorted, bagged and boxed thousands of pounds of oranges at the Second Harvest Food Bank in San Jose.  After the event, some of us went to Five Guys for burgers, drinks and conversation.
