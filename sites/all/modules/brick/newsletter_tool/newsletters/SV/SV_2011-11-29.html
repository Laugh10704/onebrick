Annual Campaign
Did you know that 2011 is the 10th year One Brick has provided volunteers to community organizations that fight hunger, improve educational outcomes, clean up trails and shorelines, keep arts programs thriving, and so much more? Over the years we've contributed a staggering amount of hours to our community.

 It has also been a huge amount of fun!

One Brick is FREE. We refuse to charge a membership fee. We do not charge the non-profits we support. But once a year we do ask our volunteers and supporters to help fund our efforts during the Annual Campaign.

Volunteer Holiday Party (Sunnyvale)
Volunteers are the foundation of our organization.  We would not exist without you.  You guys all work so hard throughout the year, so why not take a break and hang out with your fellow One Brick volunteers without having to get dirty first?

We would like to invite you to a volunteer social at Faultline.  Come hang out, have a beverage and some food, and chat with other volunteers that you have probably seen once or a gazillion times, but don't really know so well.  If you are new to One Brick, or have been thinking about checking us out, you should definitely come too! We think that you will meet some pretty cool people.

Fri, 12/9/2011 - 7PM-9PM - Volunteer Holiday Party (Sunnyvale)

 
 
 

Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)
Love to knit (or crochet) and like to socialize???  This is the perfect event for you.  Please join One Brick in helping the Sunnyvale Community Services make scarves and hats for their seniors for the holidays.

Don't know how to knit or crochet?  This is the perfect opportunity to learn.  We will have a few experienced people who will teach us how to knit and/or crochet. Please feel free to bring your own needles, hooks, yarn etc.  However, these are not required; the Sunnyvale Community Services has given us yarn to use.  We will try to have extra needles and hooks on hand.

Tue, 12/6/2011 - 6:30PM-9PM - Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)

 
 
 

Upcoming Events

Santa Saturday (San Jose)
Santa Saturday at Almaden Parents' Preschool (APPS) serves as the school's largest fundraising event.  Children get to walk into a winter wonderland, play games, make crafts, take a picture with Santa, enjoy a raffle and auction, as well as eat with their friends and family. One brick volunteers will help run game stations that children can play. We will be responsible for giving small prizes and playing games with each contestant.

Sat, 1 2/3/2011 - 9AM-1PM - Santa Saturday (San Jose)
  

Indulge your Inner Artist @ Holiday Family Fun Day
One Brick volunteers will be stationed at the art activity tables, where children and families can make free holiday themed art projects. 

Sat, 12/3/2011 - 1PM-4PM - Indulge your Inner Artist at Holiday Family Fun Day! (Palo Alto) 

Storytime with Santa (Campbell)
Campbell recreation center has invited Santa to read a story to local children.  There will be arts/craft tables out, and cookies/cocoa. After the story, Santa visits with the kids,and we take pictures. Volunteers will assist with arts/craft table, refreshment/beverage area, pictures with Santa and the check-in table.

Sat, 12/10/2011 - 5:30PM-9PM - Storytime with Santa (Campbell)

 
 

Village Harvest (Los Gatos Hills Orchard)
A Los Gatos Hills orchard owner has kindly offered Village Harvest the possibility to harvest their fruit, which will be passed on to local food agencies and food banks to feed those in need in the community.

Sun, 12/11/2011 - 9AM-12:30PM - Village Harvest (Los Gatos Hills Orchard)

 

 

Art in Action: Sorting for Schools (Menlo Park)
One Brick will help Art in Action assemble boxes of art supplies for K-8 classroom use. Each ART box holds enough supplies to teach 12 program level-specific lessons to a class of 24 students and includes materials like paper, templates, sequins, feathers, yarn, and beads.

Tue, 12/13/2011 - 6PM-8PM - Art in Action: Sorting for Schools (Menlo Park)
 
 

Event Recaps

Sip n' Knit (Sunnyvale)
One Brick volunteers have been busy at Sip n' Knit event at Panera and at home.  We dropped off 59 scarves and 18 hats since our last donation.  Our grand total is 88 scarves and 41 hats to be given to seniors from Sunnyvale Community Services.  We look forward to having an additional number of finished items before the holidays!
