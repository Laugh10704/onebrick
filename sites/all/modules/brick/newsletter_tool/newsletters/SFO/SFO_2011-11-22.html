FunCheapSF and One Brick are throwing a volunteer appreciation party to celebrate the holidays, and of course you're invited!  Admission to the party will be two cans of food to be donated to the SF Food Bank.  In between all the Christmas shopping and decorating, party on with fellow volunteers and new friends you have yet to meet.
Holiday Party with FunCheapSF: Sat, Dec 17th 5:30PM to 8PM

What Do You Get For SIX?

Six hours a month to make a big impact within One Brick and within our community.  Here's more favorites from your SF Chapter on what they get being a One Brick event manager:
 
Megan K. - Volunteer since 2005
"Getting to interact w/ the other volunteers & management team--it's a great feeling to come to an event and know folks. I love it when OB folks become not just OB colleagues, but friends too."

Laura D. - Volunteer since 2007
"Being on the One Brick Management Team has really helped build my confidence in speaking to large groups.  It also has allowed me to attend events I may not have attended as a volunteer which has led to many rewarding volunteer experiences as well as exposing me to many other like-minded people."
