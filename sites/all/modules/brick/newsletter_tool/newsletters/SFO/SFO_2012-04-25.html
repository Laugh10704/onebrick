Be More Involved! East Bay Training on 5/14

Want the flashy badge and official-looking clipboard that comes from being an event coordinator?  You know you do.  Learn more about joining our management staff at this training session on Monday, May 14th in the East Bay, and then spend some time hanging out with current managers and coordinators.
RSVP for the Management 'Shadow' Training (East Bay) - Monday, May 14th 6:30PM to 8:30PM

Thanks for Coming!

Thank you to all those who came to our very first social supporting At The Crossroads.  We had a fantastic time toasting this wonderful organization with all of you!  With your help, we raised $2,120 that ATC will use to aid homeless youth.  We hope you enjoyed learning more about one of our awesome non-profit partners.
 
Join us for our next social, a fundraiser for the San Francisco Food Bank.  There will be happy hour pricing with 2 for 1 drinks from 6-7, a raffle, and an opportunity to see some One Brickers bartending!
 
One Brick Social supporting the SF Food Bank - Friday, May 18th 6PM to 9PM

Sort Donated Medical Supplies at MedShare

Let's get medical supplies to those who can use them!  Still usable, but discarded because of production overages and regulations in the US, these supplies will be sent to underserved healthcare facilities in developing countries.
Sun, May 12th 1PM to 4PM: Sort Donated Medical Supplies at MedShare (San Leandro)

HPP's Annual Fundraiser: Our House

The Homeless Prenatal Program works with low-income and homeless families towards ending childhood poverty.  Our House is their annual benefit, a cocktail reception and silent auction, and of course One Brick is there to help out!  Won't you join us?
Sat, May 12th 5:30PM to 10:30PM: HPP's Annual Fundraiser: Our House
