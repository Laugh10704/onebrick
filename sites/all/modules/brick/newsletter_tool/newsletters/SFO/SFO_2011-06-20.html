News and Announcements

Summertime Happy Hour!

Come celebrate the start of summer with us at the Beer Zone @ Lori's Diner.  Time for a cold beer or two!  There will be twelve specialty beers on hand at a price of just $3 for Happy Hour attendees.

It's a chance to learn more about One Brick and hang out with your fellow volunteers.

Wed, June 22nd 6PM to 9PM: Happy Hour

Upcoming Events

SF Free Folk Music Festival 6/26

Experience free live music and dancing on three stages at this festival!  One Brick will help with greeting people, raffle sales, instrument checks and crafts/club table, all while jamming with folk music lovers and fellow volunteers.

Sun, June 26th 12PM to 4PM: SF Free Folk Music Festival

Habitat Restoration at Mt. Sutro 7/2

New trail (re)discovered at Mt. Sutro!  Help the Mt. Sutro Stewards in clearing trails in this 61 acre Reserve.  Afterwards, join everyone for food and beverages, and relax after a good morning's work:

Sat, July 2nd 9AM to 12:30PM: Habitat Restoration at Mt. Sutro

Sea Scavenger Shoreline Cleanup 7/9

Clean up the shoreline with Sea Scavenger - their mission is to rid the oceans of plastic pollution and support the health of the marine ecosystems and people.  And you can help out!  Join them the second Saturday of the month near AT&T ballpark:

Sat, July 9th 10AM to 12:30PM: Sea Scavenger Shoreline Cleanup

Alameda County Community Food Bank 7/9

The Food Bank needs your help to sort and box food items!  With your work as a volunteer, you will help the Food Bank distribute much needed food to the community.

Sat, July 9th 1PM to 4PM: Alameda County Community Food Bank (Oakland)
