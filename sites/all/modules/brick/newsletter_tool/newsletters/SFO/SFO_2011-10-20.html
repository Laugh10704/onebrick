Our Volunteers Rock!
Here's a small bit of data for you number crunchers out there: nationwide, our volunteer hours have already hit 83% of last year's total.  Which means all of you are really getting out there and helping out!  We already knew our volunteers were fantastic, wonderful people, but now there's hard, empirical data to back that up!

Let's add loads more to that total number, shall we?

SF Vintner's Market
Did everyone have fun at the Vintner's Market this past weekend?  Our volunteers always help to make every event better!  A special thank you from Edna to our great volunteers:

"Please extend my most sincere and enthusiastic gratitude to the teams that came out to work the entrance of Vintners Market this past weekend.

Everyone was so gracious and patient as we were thrown a few curve balls at the last minute -- ticket scanners not arriving and the appearance of multiple ticket vendor lists. Entrance was remarkably understaffed (compared to the numbers that were requested), but the OB volunteers who showed up hit it right out of the park! No patron had to wait longer than a few minutes to have their ticket taken! Glass Distribution went so smoothly and efficiently -- the area was organized and set up for the next morning's shift! Oh and we had ZERO product loss in the coat/wine check!

So glad to have heard that the managers were able to break folks so that they could enjoy the event. That made me particularly happy."
