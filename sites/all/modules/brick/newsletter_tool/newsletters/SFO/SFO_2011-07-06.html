Even with living in the Bay Area, how often do you get out on the water?  Well, here's your chance!  Check out the City skyline from a kayak; we'll have fantastic views of the Bay Bridge, Treasure Island, downtown San Francisco, and more... a 360 degree view of the place we call home.  Take advantage of the group rate, and sign up now!
