News and Announcements

What Do You Get For SIX?
What do you get for SIX hours?  That's the question we recently asked the San Francisco Chapter Event Managers.  Six flexible hours per month is all it takes to make a significant impact within One Brick.  Every new EM we train translates to one more volunteer opportunity we can add to the One Brick Volunteer Calendar.  

One Brick events benefit our community by keeping our parks healthy. They benefit our residents by feeding the less fortunate. They benefit great causes by assisting other non-profits.  We do this by offering a wide selection of minimal commitment volunteer opportunities all revolving around volunteer social interaction.  

Are you ready to make your own impact?  Come join our team, and find out what YOU can get for SIX hours.

Our next Management 'Shadow' Training is Sunday, Nov 20th; have dinner and meet some of our current managers, and learn more about becoming a part of the team.

And the Answer Is?
So...  What do you get for SIX hours?  Here are a few outstanding answers from some of your favorite managers:

Michael G. - Volunteer since 2005
"Deep inside, I'm a people person, so being part of the Management Team allows me to get to know most volunteers in whatever event I manage."

Paloma M. - Volunteer since 2011
"I get to meet other volunteers and people attending the event which is interesting and get to learn about the event and why it is taking place. I hone my management skills and public speaking. I learn to prioritize and motivate people (hopefully)."

Bonita S. - Volunteer since 2009
"Having management duties is that bit of 'mortar' that helps me stick to a regular volunteering schedule."

More to come from your fav coordinators & managers...  Next time!

Annual Feedback Survey
Many thanks to everyone for filling out our annual survey!  Out of a thousand people who responded:

97% said they would recommend One Brick to a friend.
80% plan to volunteer with One Brick again.

We love feedback!  By the way, anytime you want to let us know how we're doing, you can fill out the survey on Facebook; just click on "Take the survey!" on the left-hand side of the page.  We look forward to hearing from you!
