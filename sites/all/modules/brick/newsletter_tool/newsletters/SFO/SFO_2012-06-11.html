Highlight Event - Alcatraz Cleanup


We had a fantastic time at our first volunteer event on "The Rock."  The windows of the New Industries Building received a thorough cleaning from our boatload of One Brickers.



And afterwards, the National Parks Service gave us a tour of the island, this former federal prison.



One of the perks of volunteering for events like these is the chance to see a different side of the Bay Area, a little history of this place we call home.

And, yes, we're already planning a return trip!

One Brick Community Dog Walk!
Join our Silicon Valley chapter as they host a Community Dog Walk!

Sunday, June 24th 10am-2pm
Mitchell Park, 600 East Meadow Drive, Palo Alto

Why? Because we like people, we like dogs and we want to raise a little money to support our volunteer work.

It's going to be a total blast.  Doggies, fancy dress, photos booth, food trucks, rescue groups, local pet companies, games, friends and neighbors.  And did we mention doggies (all with waggy tails!)?

So why not join us?
Registration online for $10 ($15 on the day)
 
Everyone gets a bandana and a "doggie bag" of goodies.  The first 100 registrants receive a free collar or lead from Lupine!

No dog?  No worries - Sign up to help run the event.  We need people to help with setup, running the event and teardown.
 
*Sun, 6/24/2012 - 8AM-10AM - One Bark Community Dog Walk - Setup (Palo Alto)
*Sun, 6/24/2012 - 10AM-2PM - One Bark Community Dog Walk (Palo Alto)
*Sun, 6/24/2012 - 2PM-4PM - One Bark Community Dog Walk - Teardown (Palo Alto)

Celebrate Pink Saturday
Join the party!

Every year, on the Saturday night before the Pride Parade downtown, the Sisters of Perpetual Indulgence close the streets to auto traffic and throw the largest underground street party in SF.  With all kinds of music playing on multiple sound stages, this event promises to be a blast.

The Sisters will very kindly make a financial donation to One Brick for every volunteer we bring to the party.  See you there!

Sat, June 23rd 6:15PM to 11PM: Celebrate Pink Saturday
