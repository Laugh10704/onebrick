EggDrop and One Brick

One Brick is teaming up with EggDrop to build a stronger community.  EggDrop is a mobile app that makes it really easy to buy and sell used goods from the convenience of your smartphone.  Get organized, get rid of clutter, and make some money by selling unwanted items on EggDrop, and help raise money for One Brick.  From now until July 31st, EggDrop will donate $1 per item listed for sale by One Brickers.  Click for all the details: EggDrop & One Brick Team Up

Race Around SF with CitySolve

Show off your puzzle solving skills and your keen sense of direction with CitySolve Urban Race!  Here's another chance to sign up for this ultimate urban adventure and experience San Francisco in a new way as you speed around the City solving clues and facing fun challenges.  Use discount code "onebrick" for $10 off, and One Brick will receive a donation from your registration!
For details on the race, the Packet Pickup House Party the evening before the race, and more:
CitySolve Urban Race, August 20th
