Upcoming Events

Habitat Restoration at Mt. Sutro

One Brick will join the Mt. Sutro Stewards to make improvements to the trail network and perform selective habitat restoration in the UCSF Mount Sutro Open Space Reserve. All volunteer days include both habitat and trail work, and there is always something for everyone to do.
 

Sat, 3/5/2011 - 9AM-12:30PM - Habitat Restoration at Mt. Sutro
 

Gardening at Seward Mini Park

The Seward Street Mini-Park is an incredible jewel in San Francisco. Rarely used, yet well-kept by the neighborhood, the mini-park consists of a neighborhood garden, a plaza area with park benches, a crawl-through "tunnel," sand area, and two concrete slides. The slides are screamers if you go down on cardboard! One Brick volunteers will help the San Francisco Rec & Parks Department with weeding, pruning and planting. 

Sun, 3/6/2011 - 9AM-12Noon - Gardening at Seward Mini Park
 

 

Oakland Zoo - Arroyo Viejo Creek

Come to the Arroyo Viejo Creek at the Oakland Zoo and experience the local habitat, while helping it thrive. Grab your gardening gloves and help clean up the creek. We work hard, learn and have fun! One Brick will be pulling non-natives plants, planting native plants, mulching, pulling garbage out of creek, maintaining watershed. 

Sat, 3/19/2011 - 9:45AM-1PM - Oakland Zoo - Arroyo Viejo Creek (Oakland) 

Event Recaps

Lloyd Lake Restoration in GGP

We had a great time helping restore the area surrounding Lloyd Lake in GGP on Saturday morning. Despite the forecast for rain, we were greeted with blue skies and sun as we weeded and cleared an entire area for reclamation.  A great group of first-time volunteers was there to help and afterwards, we enjoyed some Peasant Pies on Irving St.!
