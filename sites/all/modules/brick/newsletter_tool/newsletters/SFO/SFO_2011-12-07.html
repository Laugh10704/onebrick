What Do You Get For SIX?

More from our wonderful event managers on what they get for six hours by managing events with One Brick:
 
Veronica V. - Volunteer since 2010
"I've definitely improved my communication skills, both through emails and in person.  Since English is not my native language I've always found it hard to express myself and get things across the way I wanted (and to feel comfortable talking with people I don't know), and being part of the OneBrick Management Team and dealing with so many different people has been a great challenge and good exercise.  And there is still room for more improvement, so I'll keep managing:)"
 
Elaine E. - Volunteer since 2002
"Making a big difference by giving to the many nonprofits & volunteers with just a few hours of my time."
 
Kristina V. - Volunteer since 2002
"Helping to support events (and an organization - OB), that I believe in.  Also getting to know more people, since I am greeting and speaking to everyone."
 
Are you ready to join the team and make an even bigger impact?
