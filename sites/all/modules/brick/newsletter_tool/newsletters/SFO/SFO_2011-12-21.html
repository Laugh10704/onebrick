Annual Campaign

Over the last ten years, we've connected volunteers with over 1,200 different community organizations at over 10,000 events.  One Brick is a very efficient organization; any donation you can give will have a big impact on the help we can provide to the community.  Please consider making a donation today.
 
And thank you for coming out to every event, ready to volunteer!

Sunday, January 8th: Management 'Shadow' Training
