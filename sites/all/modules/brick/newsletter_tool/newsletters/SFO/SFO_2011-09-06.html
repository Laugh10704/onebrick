SF Management Retreat
Last week, our event managers and coordinators brought their clipboards together for our annual management retreat.  It was our chance to touch bases with everyone and get the lowdown on what's happening with our SF chapter.  We also had the chance to work in some group exercise...  at the House of Air!  Yes, just imagine your managers and coordinators bouncing around everywhere...

After all the discussions and trampoline jumping and dodgeball, we sat down to a fantastic lunch made by the wonderful Shilly S.  Thank you, thank you Shilly for all the yummy!  And many, many thanks to Veronica V. for putting this all together; it was awesome!
