East Bay Happy Hour

This Wednesday, we're headed out to Luka's Taproom & Lounge in Oakland for good times with good friends.  Just a few blocks from the 19th Street BART station, we'll be enjoying happy hour drink specials, pool and video games.  We'll also be holding a raffle; a suggested $5 donation gets you a ticket for a chance to win some great prizes.

See you all there!

When: Wednesday, March 28th 6PM to 9PM
Where: Luka's Taproom & Lounge, 2221 Broadway, Oakland

One Brick Social Presents...

We're launching our newest endeavor!

We'll be throwing social fundraisers that will benefit the local non-profits and community organizations that we support.  At these events, we'll learn more about the non-profits that we volunteer at each month - their goals, the impact to the community, upcoming volunteer events and ways to be more involved.

Our first non-profit partner is At The Crossroads.  Proceeds from this first event on April 17th at Tupelo will be donated to this organization for their future projects.

Watch the event page for more information in the coming weeks, and join us for the start of something new!

One Brick Social: Tue, April 17th 6PM to 9PM, at Tupelo, 1337 Grant Ave, SF
