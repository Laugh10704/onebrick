New Orleans in Autumn
For those of you so disappointed that our May trip is booked solid, we've another trip on the calendar!  Starting September 29th, we'll be spending a week helping the rebuilding efforts in St. Bernard Parish, one of the more devastated communities in the aftermath of hurricanes Katrina and Rita.

Spend the days working construction tasks, side by side with fellow volunteers, and your evenings exploring the city of New Orleans.

For more details, visit the event page on our calendar and our NOLA page.  Tickets available for purchase here.

Wine and Dine for Healthcare
Operation Access brings together a network of medical professionals and hospitals to provide much needed surgical and specialty care for the uninsured and underserved, free of charge.  OA is an official charity of the SF Marathon, and you can join them in celebrating National Healthcare & Nurse Week at their SF Marathon Kick-off.

Wine and Dine for Healthcare will be happening on Thursday, May 10th from 6-9pm, at the Vinyl Wine Bar, 359 Divisadero St at Oak St. in San Francisco.  There is no entrance fee, and there'll be happy hour pricing all evening and a raffle with tickets available for $5 each or 5 for $20.

RSVP is not necessary but appreciated; you can RSVP on the Facebook event page.  If you have questions, you can contact Operations Access at (415) 733-0052 or communications@operationaccess.org
