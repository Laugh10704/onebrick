May 26th, 2012  
News and Announcements

One Brick Social
Many thanks to everyone who supported the SF Food Bank at our last social!  We were able to raise $665 to aid the Food Bank in their fight against hunger.  A special shout out to our volunteer bartenders - most of the money donated came from tips; you guys mixed some great drinks!  Thank you, thank you, thank you!

Watch the calendar for our next social, sometime in late July or early August with the exciting possibility of live music from local bands, benefitting One Brick.

Upcoming Events

Habitat Restoration at Mt. Sutro
Come party with the Mt. Sutro Stewards and work with them to spruce up the trails near the summit.  A great opportunity to spend a Saturday morning in a part of SF just a little removed from the hustle & bustle of the rest of the city, while giving back!
Sat, June 2nd 9AM to 12:30PM: Habitat Restoration at Mt. Sutro

Sort Donated Medical Supplies at VIDA
Donated medical supplies make their way to communities in need with an assist from you!  Help VIDA prepare shipments to be sent to under-served communities in Latin America and the Caribbean.
Mon, June 4th 6PM to 8PM: Sort Donated Medical Supplies at VIDA (Emeryville)

Help Staff SummerTini
This annual benefit for Episcopal Community Services and CHEFS (Conquering Homelessness through Employment in Food Services) will be an evening of live music, great food from local chefs, and live & silent auctions.  Support ECS in their effort to provide a wide range of services to the homeless in San Francisco:
Fri, June 8th 6:30PM to 10:30PM: Help Staff SummerTini

Help La Escuelita Elementary & Met West High Move
Like packing?  We'll be helping the two schools get ready for their move to a brand new building, so let's make sure all their stuff is packed!
Sat, June 16th 9:30AM to 12:30PM: Help La Escuelita Elementary and Met West High Schools Move (Oakland)

Celebrate Pink Saturday
This is SF's largest underground street party, so you know it will be a blast!  (We know how to party around here, don't we?)  We'll be greeting attendees, collecting donations and placing stickers on donating attendees.  The Sisters of Perpetual Indulgence will make a donation to One Brick for every volunteer we bring to this fun event.  And we'll have a great time together!
Sat, June 23rd 6:15PM to 11PM: Celebrate Pink Saturday
