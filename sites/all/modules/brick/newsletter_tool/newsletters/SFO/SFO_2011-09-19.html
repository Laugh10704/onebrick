September Happy Hour!
Start getting ready for Oktoberfest with this month's Happy Hour!

Come out to Thirsty Bear on September 28th, where their Wednesday special is a beer and snifter pairing for $8 (12 oz beer & 2 oz liquid pour).

Before we pull on the lederhosen and start pouring beer at our local Oktoberfest, we'll kick back a few mugs with our great volunteers.  It's an opportunity to get together and relax; and if you want to learn more about One Brick, come on over and say "Hi!"

One Brick Happy Hour - Wed, September 28th 6PM to 9PM

Volunteer fun at this fundraiser!  Help staff this event put on by Rebuilding Together SF, so that they can continue to provide free home repair & renovations for low-income, elderly and disabled San Francisco residents.
Thu, Sept 22nd 5:30PM to 9PM: Changing Neighborhoods, Changing Lives Fundraiser

Eat Real Festival
We enjoy wonderful and diverse food options in our Bay Area; the Eat Real Festival celebrates the fresh and delicious fare available locally.  Come volunteer, and celebrate the good eats with us:
Sat, Sept 24th 1:30PM to 5:30PM: Eat Real Festival (Oakland)

Leland Avenue Street Fair
San Francisco has many terrific street fairs throughout the year; this family-oriented street fair will have live performances by local artists, a children's area, arts & crafts, and more.  Volunteers will assist with the major kids attractions, food and tear down.
Sun, Sept 25th 1PM to 5PM: Leland Avenue Street Fair
