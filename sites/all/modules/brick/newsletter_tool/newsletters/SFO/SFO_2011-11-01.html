November 1st, 2011  
News and Announcements


This weekend One Brick will be walking the green with golfing legends at the Charles Schwab Cup Championship!  The culmination of this season's Champions Tour, the Charles Schwab Cup will determine the Tour's leading player out of a field of 30 golfers including Tom Lehman, John Cook, and Russ Cochran.

We'll take in the finale at San Francisco's Harding Park, a beautiful course ranked by Golf Digest as one of the "Best Places to Play," with a majestic treeline and views of the surrounding lake.  Join us this Saturday and Sunday for all the birdies and bogeys!

Saturday, Shift 1 - 9am to 1pm
Saturday, Shift 2 - 1pm to 5pm

Sunday, Shift 1 - 9am to 1pm
Sunday, Shift 2 - 1pm to 5pm

Vying for the top spot:

  • Tom Lehman leads the pack going into this weekend with three wins on this year's circuit.  He won his debut tournament on the Champions Tour in 2009.
  • Mark Calcavecchia joined the Champions Tour last year.  He won thirteen PGA Tour events during his professional career, and set a Tour record of nine consecutive birdies during his second round at the RBC Canadian Open in 2009.
  • Peter Senior has finished 2nd on the Champions Tour 3 times this year.  The Australian golfer has won more than twenty tournaments around the world.
  • John Cook was a member of the Ohio State University Buckeyes' NCAA Championship team in 1979.  He has three wins on the Tour this year.
  • Russ Cochran won the 2011 Senior British Open and became the first left-hander to win a major since the Senior British became an official event on the Champions Tour.
