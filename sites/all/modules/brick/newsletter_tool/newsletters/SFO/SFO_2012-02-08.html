Celebrating Our Volunteers


Thanks for coming out to our volunteer appreciation celebration; it was great seeing everyone at our last happy hour!  We got to catch up with old friends and meet some new ones.  And we had a blast playing Jenga; some of you are getting really good at that game!
Special shout-out to our two award winners who went home that night with cool, shiny plaques - to Wilhelm Y., our 2011 Volunteer of the Year, and to Richard H., our 2011 Manager of the Year.  Congrats to both of you, and keep being awesome!

Behind the Clipboard and Badge...

Want to be more involved with One Brick?  Ever think about becoming an event coordinator?  You can learn what it takes to be a successful event coordinator at our next training session.  We'll give you the training you need to take that next step, and afterwards you'll mingle with some of our current managers and coordinators who can give you their perspective on being part of One Brick.
 
RSVP at SF OB: Management 'Shadow' Training on Sunday, February 26th, 5:45PM to 8PM

Beer Bus!

If you went on last year's Bus Trip to Sonoma Valley, then you know how much fun it is when we go on tour!  In March we'll be going on another road trip, touring local breweries on a circuit around the bay.  There will be drink specials, discounts on merchandise and, of course, awesome people to share a pitcher with.  Space is limited, so sign up early!
 
There is a $25 cost per person to cover the bus rental.  To sign up, and for more details, please see the event page:
Social Event: Beer Bus Trip on Saturday, March 10th, 11AM to 7PM
