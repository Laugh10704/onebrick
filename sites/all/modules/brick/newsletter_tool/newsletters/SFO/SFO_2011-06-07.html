News and Announcements

Why do we volunteer?  We want to make a difference in our community.  This is the impact our volunteers made one Friday night for the children of Alamo School:

"Hope you all are having a great weekend so far. Pardon the mass email, but every once in awhile I have a "One Brick moment" which reminds me of why I am so glad we have this group doing what we are doing.

Last night Michael G. and I managed the Friends of the Alamo School Silent Auction and Gala, an event that One Brick has helped out with for several years. The volunteer coordinator there, Joe, shared with & expressed his sincere thanks to One Brick. There were two women who performed at the event who were actually the P.E. and music teachers. Their salaries have been slashed completely because of budget cuts in the school district. This event literally pays their salaries and the parents rally together every year to pitch in to help because they value these programs and want to keep the school a strong one. So One Brick was instrumental in making this fundraiser a success. I was really moved by the team of folks we had working hard last night (in cluding two ECs in training)!

Rock on y'all,

Megan"
