Staff Promotions!
Congratulations to our newest Event Managers and Event Coordinators!  Say "Hi" next time you see them at an event.
Krisanne C., Paloma M. and Sean C. have been promoted to Event Coordinators, and will have shiny new clipboards for you to sign in on.  Christine C. is now an Event Manager and Kelly Anne C. has transferred over to be a manager in our SF chapter, so look for them to head up an event near you.  Congrats again to all!

Trek Around the City...
Ever want to be on The Amazing Race, but can’t quit your day job?  We can help!  One Brick is once again partnering with City Solve Urban Race to stage a race through San Francisco!  Join One Brick and navigate through the streets of SF to solve clues and face fun challenges, all while posing for silly pictures and making friends... or weirding out strangers along the way.
City Solve Urban Race, Saturday August 20th

...Or Glide Along the Bay
With all the commuter options in the Bay Area, why not take a kayak?  Guaranteed spectacular views, and you can chat with fellow volunteers - or maybe a few seals and pelicans! - along the way.  Bring your family and friends along, and you can command a fleet!
Kayaking with City Kayak, Sunday August 14th
