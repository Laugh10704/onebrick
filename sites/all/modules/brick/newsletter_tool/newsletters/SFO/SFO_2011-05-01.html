CitySolve Urban Race

Get your sleuth hat on!  CitySolve Urban Race is back in SF for two races this year, the first of which is June 4th.  One Brick race teams get to run on the cheap again this year - just apply the discount code onebrick when you sign up and receive substantial savings for your race team!

Our Management Team

One Brick chapters are 100% volunteer-run; the people you see at every event holding a clipboard and handing out nametags are all volunteers like you!  We currently have 31 active Event Managers and 15 active Event Coordinators, people you've seen at an event in the past few months.

Our goal this year is to grow our numbers to 40 Event Managers and 20 Event Coordinators, because the more people we have managing events, the more events we can schedule.  And more events means more volunteering fun!

If you're interested in joining the management team, come sign up for our new manager orientation Sunday, May 22nd.

Sign Up for Op4G and Support One Brick

One Brick needs your help!  We've partnered with Op4G, an online research community.  By simply SIGNING UP you will help us reach our pool goal of 5000 people by June; this provides cash up front equating to nearly 50% of our annual budget!  After that you are provided the opportunity (i.e. your choice) to fill out online surveys - which you get paid for.  You can elect to defer anywhere from 25% to 100% of your payment directly to One Brick.  But first, you gotta sign up!  Please check them out @ Op4G.com.

Upcoming Events

Beautification at Brooks Park 5/8

Located on the southwestern-most hill in San Francisco, Brooks Park has beautiful panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean, Lake Merced, the Farallones and Mt. Tamalpais.  Come join us in weeding the perennial beds, and help beautify the park.

Sun, May 8th 9AM to 12PM: Beautification at Brooks Park

Ashland Little League Carnival 5/15

Help set up the vendor booths, and work the snack bar, ticket booth, and the silent auction booth at this carnival.  Proceeds go to the Ashland Little League to pay for trophies for all of their players.

Sun, May 15th 11AM to 3PM: Ashland Little League Carnival (Ashland)

HPP's Annual Fundraiser: Our House 5/21

The Homeless Prenatal Program provides services to poor and homeless families including prenatal and parenting support, onsite childcare, housing assistance, and domestic violence and substance abuse services.  This is their annual fundraiser which consists of a cocktail reception and a silent auction.

One Brick will assist with dinner setup and cleanup, and with serving food.  Check out the calendar for more details:

Sat, May 21st 5:30PM to 10:30PM: HPP's Annual Fundraiser: Our House

Painting & Gardening at Children's Fairyland 5/28

A park to delight children and their parents with whimsical storybook sets, gentle rides, friendly animals and more, Children's Fairyland needs volunteers to help with painting, gardening, and general cleanup.

Sat, May 28th 9AM to 1PM: Painting and Gardening at Children's Fairyland (Oakland)
