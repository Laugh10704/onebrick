News and Announcements

St. Bernard Parish (New Orleans)

Once again, volunteers from each of One Brick's nine chapters will be convening in St. Bernard Parish from May 7th-15th. Join volunteers from around the country on another trip to take part in this incredible volunteer effort to rebuild the Gulf Coast.

St. Bernard Parish remains one of the most devastated communities in the aftermath of hurricanes Katrina and Rita. Located to the southeast of New Orleans, St. Bernard suffered significant structural damage to 100% of its residential and commercial units, rendering them completely uninhabitable.

Upcoming Events

St. John's Harvest

Supported by a small church, St. John's Harvest serves about 300 people each week and takes food home to about 500 people. One Brick will be unloading, unboxing, and handing out food. We will also help with crowd control and assist clients choose and take food, and clean up. Any volunteers who would be able to translate for Chinese and Russian clients would be appreciated, but this skill is not required.

Sat, 4/2/2011 - 7:15AM-10AM - St. John's Harvest
 

Habitat Restoration at Mt. Sutro

One Brick will join the Mt. Sutro Stewards to make improvements to the trail network and perform selective habitat restoration in the UCSF Mount Sutro Open Space Reserve. All volunteer days include both habitat and trail work, and there is always something for everyone to do.
 

Sat, 4/2/2011 - 9AM-12:30PM - Habitat Restoration at Mt. Sutro
 

Landscape the 16th Avenue Tiled Steps

The 16th Avenue Tiled Steps project has been a neighborhood effort to create a beautiful mosaic running up the risers of the 163 steps located at 16th and Moraga in San Francisco. One Brick will help weed the hillsides surrounding 16th Avenue Tiled Steps. 

Sat, 4/9/2011 - 10:30AM-12:30PM - Landscape the 16th Avenue Tiled Steps
 

SF Vintner's Market

At the San Francisco Vintners Market, wine buyers can connect with hundreds of wineries and taste and buy wines direct from the source. One Brick will assist with running the event, including set up, registration, assisting the wineries.

Event Recaps

Monday Night PlayGround Series (Berkeley)

We had a group of One Brick volunteers for our last Monday night Playground series of the season. After checking in and ushering in attendees, we all enjoyed a fun variety of plays on a vaudeville theme.
 

San Francisco Food Bank

Rice-a-roni is the San Francisco Food Bank treat! Volunteers bagged, weighed, sealed, stickered, and packed thousands of pounds of rice before heading off to Goat Hill Pizza for more socializing.
  

Painting and Gardening at Children's Fairyland

On a dreary Saturday morning, One Brick volunteers helped to spruce up Children's Fairyland in Oakland.  They made the picnic areas more pleasant for children by removing cobweb surprises.  Besides that, each had the opportunity to get in touch with his or her inner child.  Who could resist sticking one's head in a water fountain that's in the shape of a lion?
