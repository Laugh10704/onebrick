Upcoming Events

Volunteer at the Practice Rounds of the US Open!

From Monday, June 13 through Sunday, June 19, One Brick will be volunteering at the US Open to help raise money for all the great work One Brick does. Practice rounds take place Monday through Wednesday, and the main event will be Thursday through Sunday.

While the Monday through Wednesday shitfs won't be the main event, there are some good reasons to sign up for these early week shifts:

  • More relaxed atmosphere. During the practice rounds, there will be fewer regulations. On Monday through Wednesday, you can bring cameras, and there will be more potential for interaction with golfers.
  • You'll still watch golf for free! The golfers will be getting in lots of practice early in the week.
  • Some employers give time off to volunteer. Check with your employer - you may be able to spend a day at the US Open rather than in the office. And if you have a non-traditional work schedule, here is a One Brick event that doesn't take place on a weekend!

Check out our June calendar here to RSVP for a shift (or two!).

Event of the Month: Explore@NASA Goddard!

Come volunteer this Saturday, May 14, at NASA's Goddard Space Flight Center in Greenbelt, Maryland! For you Marylanders in One Brick, here's an event near you. Even if you don't live near Greenbelt, it's still a lot closer than outer space!

This event will be a day of fun for visitors who will learn about NASA and the space center's research in science, engineering, and technology. You will help with the activiites, demonstrations, and entertainment that will make this an educational and entertaining day for all.

Beyond One Brick

Even more opportunities to volunteer! Be sure to mention that you heard about these events from One Brick.
 
Throughout May - Great Strides is hosting 12 walks on different days and in different locations in the DC area. If you would like to help volunteer at the walk nearest (or farthest from!) you, contact rwerner@cff.org.

Friday, May 13 - If your Fridays are playdays rather than workdays, spend this one building a playground! Laurel's Deerfield Run Elementary School is building a playground, garden, and outdoor classroom. To help with the project, contact jane.hanfman@gmail.com.

Saturday, May 21 - Help Back on My Feet at the DC Capital Striders 8K in Georgetown! Volunteers are needed for tasks such as water stops and registration. Unlike many races, this one does not require getting up early: volunteers are needed at 6 pm. If you are interested, contact gretchen@backonmyfeet.org.

Saturday, June 11 and Sunday, June 12 - Help at a rummage sale at a thrift store in Leesburg, VA. The event will benefit numerous charities, including Jill's House, Joshua's hands, La Voz, Life Line, Habitat for Humanity, MADD, and American Red Cross. Email mah_cardoso@hotmail.com for more information.

Every Saturday - If you need more dogs in your life, come volunteer with HART! One Brick volunteers with the organization regularly at adoption events, but if you can't get your fill of canine goodness from just one day, you can volunteer with the organization at adoption events on other Saturdays as well. Volunteers will sit and walk with dogs in need of homes, showing off the furry bundles of joy to potential human families. If you are interested, contact hartjacob@hotmail.com.

Every Saturday - Food for All delivers food directly to clients in need.  Meet in the morning to pack up the food, then work in teams of two to deliver throughout DC.  A car is not necessary, but if you have one, it is helpful if you are able to bring it.  Contact melatulippe@yahoo.com if you are interested.
