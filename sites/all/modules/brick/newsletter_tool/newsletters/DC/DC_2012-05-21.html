Tiger Woods is back at Congressional!

 
The Washington, D.C. community once again welcomes Tiger Woods as he hosts the 2012 AT&T National at Congressional Country Club in Bethesda, MD. This must-see event features a field of 120 top PGA TOUR professionals competing for a $6.5 million purse on one of the highest-ranked courses in the country.

Proceeds from the AT&T National benefit the Tiger Woods Foundation and various youth programs in the greater D.C. area, while also paying tribute to the many courageous men and women serving in our Armed Forces.

One Brick will be located on the 11th green during the tournament, June 27 through July 1.  Many volunteer shifts are available!  Visit our event calendar here to sign up today!


It is not too late to attend great events at FedEx Field while helping One Brick!


One Brick is teaming up with the Washington Redskins to provide concessions at all home games, as well as a few special events!  A concession stand at FedEx Field will be staffed by One Brick volunteers, with a percentage of all sales going to support One Brick!
 
You MUST attend a general training before being able to volunteer at any FedEx Field event.  Once you complete the training, you will be ready to go for the entire season! 

The next 2 available trainings will be held Saturday, June 9 at 9 a.m. (register here to attend this training) and Tuesday, June 12 at 4 p.m. (register here to attend this training).  You MUST register to attend the training, or you will not be able to get a badge to access the stadium on game day!

Upcoming Events

Safeway BBQ Battle for the Boys & Girls Club
In the shadow of the nation’s capitol, tens of thousands of people witness barbecue teams and restaurants from around the country compete to win over $40,000 in cash and prices, and the title of National Pork Barbecue Champion!  Benefitting the Boys & Girls Clubs of Greater Washington, Safeway’s Barbecue Battle celebrates the art of barbecue in a spirit of community, family, and fun.  Have a finger-licken' good time with One Brick and sign up here!


St. Anthony's Silent Auction & Dance


St. Anthony Catholic Schools Second Annual Auction hopes to raise the funds necessary for classroom supplies, technology and extra-curricular activities.  Founded in 1922, their goal is to prepare its students for lives of service and leadership.  Join us for a fun night running the auction by RSVPing here!

What does it take to become a One Brick manager?


Our volunteer Event Managers are the public face of One Brick and the motor that makes this boat go!  And the best part is, anyone can join our team!  Just sign up and attend the upcoming Event Manager Orientation Brunch on Sunday, June 17 to learn how One Brick works and discuss how you can help out. For more information, email us at dc-team@onebrick.org.
