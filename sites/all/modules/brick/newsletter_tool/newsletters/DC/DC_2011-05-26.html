US Open Private Post Party at Local 16

On Friday, June 24, come spend time with your fellow One Brick Volunteers out on the patio at Local 16.  We'll have a roped off private area on the patio all to ourselves, where you'll get to meet and mingle with your fellow volunteers.

Most importantly, all US Open volunteers will get 2 free happy hour drinks at the event!  That is the least that we can do to thank you for all your hard work at the Open the previous weekend.  We will also be raffling off some special prizes just for US Open volunteers!

Happy hour specials include: $4 Bud/Bud Light, $5 Draft Beer, $5 wine, & $5 well drinks, and we'll have these specials extended til 9:00 for our party.  So, come hang out on the great patio at Local 16, enjoy  the warm weather, and relax after a long week with other volunteers!

Team Member of the Month: NishaP

NishaP began her One Brick journey with two of her favorite kind of events: the Halloween kind! She danced the Thriller to help raise money for a good cause and try to set a world record, and she helped at a haunted house for the Boys and Girls Club. She likes these events best because they "are creative and entertaining, AND you get to work with excited kids!"

Nisha's love of children comes through when she relates the funniest thing that happened to her while volunteering. "A 10 year old girl at a rec center looked at me (age 29, single, no kids) and said 'umph, you look like somebody's MOM' in a way that made me feel 65 years old! But it was funny anyhow.... I guess you had to be there!"

When asked how long Nisha's been volunteering with One Brick, she replied, "I've been working with this group for over 1 1/2 years and have had a great time doing it!" Today, Nisha coordinates manages events and is considering working on the Events Committee as well. In this photo, Nisha is coordinating the Jubilee Support Alliance Gala.

When not volunteering, Nisha enjoys "shopping, eating, working out, maybe sqeezing in a few drinks here and there!" Her favorite aspects of the DC region are "the strength of the city and the diversity of the area," and her theme song is "Don't Stop Believin' ".

Love for One Brick

Remember back when it was cold and it snowed? Even in bad weather, One Brickers showed up to make a difference!

"In January, when we held an Inventory Day for St. Anthony's Bridal, the heavens poured snow. Despite the fact that the blizzard would dump about 3' of snow, One Brick volunteers still showed up! We did what we could, competing with the accumulation of snow. Our organization has a whole collection of photos of our gowns, modeled by One Brick Volunteers. We are so grateful for the help! Thanks!"

~Melanie R. Cameron, St. Anthony's Bridal

Beyond One Brick

Even more opportunities to volunteer! Be sure to mention that you heard about these events from One Brick.
 
Throughout May - Great Strides is hosting 12 walks on different days and in different locations in the DC area. If you would like to help volunteer at the walk nearest (or farthest from!) you, contact rwerner@cff.org.

Saturday, June 11 and Sunday, June 12 - Help at a rummage sale at a thrift store in Leesburg, VA. The event will benefit numerous charities, including Jill's House, Joshua's hands, La Voz, Life Line, Habitat for Humanity, MADD, and American Red Cross. Email mah_cardoso@hotmail.com for more information.

Every Saturday - If you need more dogs in your life, come volunteer with HART! One Brick volunteers with the organization regularly at adoption events, but if you can't get your fill of canine goodness from just one day, you can volunteer with the organization at adoption events on other Saturdays as well. Volunteers will sit and walk with dogs in need of homes, showing off the furry bundles of joy to potential human families. If you are interested, contact hartjacob@hotmail.com.

Every Saturday - Food for All delivers food directly to clients in need.  Meet in the morning to pack up the food, then work in teams of two to deliver throughout DC.  A car is not necessary, but if you have one, it is helpful if you are able to bring it.  Contact melatulippe@yahoo.com if you are interested.

Become a One Brick Event Manager!

Our volunteer Event Managers are the staff who make One Brick events happen! Anyone can join our team - all we ask is that you attend an Orientation Brunch to learn how One Brick works and then we'll discuss how you can help out. For more information, email us at dc-team@onebrick.org.
