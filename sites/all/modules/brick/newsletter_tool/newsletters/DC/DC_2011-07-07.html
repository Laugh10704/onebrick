Are you ready, willing, and able to lead an organization that provides thousands of volunteer hours to the DC community each year?

Are you looking for a professional development opportunity and a way to refine key skill sets?

Do you want to work with an amazing team of event managers and coordinators?

We announced a few weeks ago that we are beginning to search for a new Chapter Director here in DC. Ideally, we would like to recruit someone who has volunteered with us, since you understand who we are and what we do.

The Chapter Director works with the committees to achieve our day-to-day operation goals as well as provide longer term strategic guidance for the chapter, establishes the agenda and the goals of the monthly meetings, and keeps the chapter moving forward in a positive direction.

If you are interested in learning more about the position or would like a more detailed position description, send me, Kevin, an email (kevinf2@onebrick.org). Please respond no later than July 28th.

Event Recaps

June Recap

The biggest highlight of June was the US Open, where 147 One Brick volunteers stocked sodas, poured beer, and sold concessions to golf-goers. All of these volunteers were eligible to win an iPad, and the lucky winner was KimB. Congratulations Kim!

We raised $13,000 thanks to your hard work! This money will go toward continuing One Brick's volunteer service with non-profits throughout the DC area.

One of these non-profits is ALIVE, which provides food for people in need. This photo from our June ALIVE event shows a One Brick volunteer distributing food to clients.

Beyond One Brick

Even more opportunities to volunteer! Be sure to mention that you heard about these events from One Brick.

Wednesday, July 13 - Learn more about volunteer opportunities with the Children's Inn, where children can stay while their parents are being treated at NIH. An open house for volunteers interested in helping with fundraising and outreach will take place on July 13; contact contele@mail.nih.gov for more information.
 
Saturday, July 16 - Dreams for Kids' Extreme Recess is back again this year! Help kids with disabilities enjoy adaptive water skiing at Pohick Bay in Lorton, Virginia. Please note that volunteers will need to bring their own equipment; however, food and beverage will be provided. To sign up, visit http://extremerecessh20skiing.eventbrite.com/.

Saturday, September 3 - Calling all music lovers! The 23rd Annual DC Blues Festival needs volunteers to distribute flyers and sell merchandise throughout the day. Contact fest-volunteer@dcblues.org if you are interested.

Every Saturday - If you need more dogs in your life, come volunteer with HART! One Brick volunteers with the organization regularly at adoption events, but if you can't get your fill of canine goodness from just one day, you can volunteer with the organization at adoption events on other Saturdays as well. Volunteers will sit and walk with dogs in need of homes, showing off the furry bundles of joy to potential human families. If you are interested, contact hartjacob@hotmail.com.

Every Saturday - Food for All delivers food directly to clients in need.  Meet in the morning to pack up the food, then work in teams of two to deliver throughout DC.  A car is not necessary, but if you have one, it is helpful if you are able to bring it.  Contact melatulippe@yahoo.com if you are interested.
