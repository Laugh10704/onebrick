What is your New Year's Resolution? In 2012, resolve to...
 

  • Donate to One Brick! Give today to our 2011 Annual Campaign, or resolve to give in 2012.
  • Volunteer! Check out our calendar for opportunities.
  • Become an Event Coordinator or Event Manager! Give just 6 hours a month toward a leadership role in One Brick. Email dc-team@onebrick.org for more information.
  • Spread the word about One Brick! Follow us on Twitter, subscribe to our blog, like us on Facebook, forward this newsletter to a friend, or tell your friends and colleagues about us!
  • Make new friends! Come to one of our monthly happy hours. Stick around after volunteer events for the post-event social and get to know your fellow volunteers.
  • Let us know about new volunteer opportunities! If you know of a non-profit that needs volunteers for one-time events, contact dc-events@onebrick.org.

Beyond One Brick

Even more opportunities to volunteer! Be sure to mention that you heard about these events from One Brick.

Every Saturday

  • If you need more dogs in your life, come volunteer with HART! Brick volunteers regularly assist at adoption by sitting and walking with dogs in need of homes; as well, showing off these furry friends to potential human families. Email hartjacob@hotmail.com for details.
  • Food for All delivers food directly to clients in need.  Meet in the morning to pack up the food, then work in teams of two to deliver throughout DC.  A car is not necessary, but if you have one, it is helpful if you are able to bring it.  Contact melatulippe@yahoo.com if you are interested.
Every Other Saturday - The Solomon Dixon Foundation's Cleaning for Chemo program needs volunteers to help clean the homes of those undergoing chemotherapy, as house cleaning is more challenging as well as more vital for chemo patients. If you are interested, please contact  maketadixon@cleaningforchemo.org. 

Become a One Brick Event Manager!

Our volunteer Event Managers are the staff who make One Brick events happen! Anyone can join our team - all we ask is that you attend an Orientation Brunch to learn how One Brick works and then we'll discuss how you can help out.  For more information, email us at dc-team@onebrick.org.

Donate to One Brick!

One Brick has only one paid employee, our Executive Director. At the chapter level, One Brick is 100% volunteer-run. To help us keep making a difference in our community, you may may make a tax-deductible donation here.
