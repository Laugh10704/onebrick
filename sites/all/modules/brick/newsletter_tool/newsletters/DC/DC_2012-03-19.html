News and Announcements

State of One Brick DC
Thank you all for your kind words of support over the last couple of months regarding One Brick's financial emergency. It's been tough but our new strategy is starting to pay off.

As promised, we've been hard working to find innovative ways to support One Brick.  The first two opportunities are now on the calendar.  They will help get the word out about the work we do, raise some much needed funds, and most importantly of all THEY WILL BE FUN!

First, next Saturday we've been invited to attend the Reason Rally to promote community engagement and volunteering. So we will be out on The Mall with likes of Adam Savage (Myth Busters) and Eddie Izzard (you know, that odd Brit comedian) handing out flyers about One Brick.  Best of all the rally organizers have designated One Brick as their local charity for the event. They'll be giving shout outs from the stage, encouraging the crowd to get out and volunteer. They'll also be encouraging people to support our work by making a donation on a special page. So, if you're not busy on the 24th why not sign up to help get the word out?!

Second, in May we'll help run an event called Warrior Dash. This event is a difficult, muddy 5k race through obstacles such as a crawl through mud pit under barbed wire and a leap over a fire pit.  In exchange for our assistance, the organizers will donate $4k to One Brick DC!!  We'll be sending out more details in the next few weeks, so mark your calendars for this event on May 19 and 20!

I hope to see you at one of these upcoming events, as I will be in attendance at both.  If you ever have any concerns or questions about One Brick, I am only an e-mail away.

-Liz Wigandt

Chapter Director, One Brick DC
