The New CD of One Brick - DC!

As we've announced in previous newsletters, our current Chapter Director (CD) KevinF will soon step down after a two-year term. We are pleased to introduce our new CD, LizW, an active Event Manager in our chapter. In the immediate future, Kevin and Liz will work together to ensure a smooth transition.

From Outgoing CD KevinF:

So long! Farewell! Auf weidersehen! Goodbye!
 
OK, well, we know that’s not completely true. I can’t shake One Brick that easily. But…after two great years at the helm, it is time for me to step down as Chapter Director here in DC. It’s been an amazing ride, and we’ve accomplished so much in the past couple years. DC has grown into one of the strongest One Brick chapters in the country, and every month we continue to do amazing things in the DC region!
 
As a One Brick volunteer, YOU make One Brick what it is – a community of volunteers who know how to have a good time while giving back!
 
See you at an event soon!

From Incoming CD LizW:

When I initially received Kevin’s ominous email back in July stating that One Brick DC was looking for a new Chapter Director, I was less than enthusiastic.  I joined One Brick back in 2007 never expecting to be involved with the organization beyond completing a few events here and there.  Somewhere along the way, probably after the lure of a free brunch, I became an Event Manager and Coordinator.  While I had grown to enjoy commitment-free volunteering and leading a few events whenever time was available, I could not envision taking on the enormous commitment of being the Chapter Director.

Yet that email made me realize how important One Brick is not only to the community we serve, but to me as an individual.  The possibility of this organization being led by a person who had never volunteered with us did not sit well with me.  The more I talked about it with close friends and family, the more they told me I was nuts to take on such a huge challenge sans pay.  Yet the more I thought about it, my doubts diminished and the reservations subsided, I knew I was the ideal candidate One Brick needed.

When Kevin told me I was his personal choice to succeed him, I knew immediately that One Brick had found its new Chapter Director.  Kevin has been an exemplary leader and has left me with big shoes to fill.  His continued guidance will enable me to take the proverbial reins to steer our chapter in the right direction.  Since this decision was announced, all of the support from our committees, Event Managers/Coordinators, and volunteers has been incredible - and for that, I am truly grateful.

There may be a few changes instituted within One Brick over the next few years, but the heart of this organization you have come to know will never change.  I truly believe that our chapter’s future is a bright one with the continued ability to positively impact our community.  One Brick is the best volunteer organization in Washington, DC.

I am looking forward to the amazing opportunity of being the Chapter Director and serving not only the community, but you, our volunteers.  I want to hear directly from you. Good ideas come from everyone, so please send me any comments, questions, suggestions, or even complaints.  My email inbox is always open.

I am confident that One Brick DC’s best years are yet to come.

Liz Wigandt
lizw@onebrick.org

Beyond One Brick

Even more opportunities to volunteer! Be sure to mention that you heard about these events from One Brick.

Every Saturday - If you need more dogs in your life, come volunteer with HART! One Brick volunteers with the organization regularly at adoption events, but if you can't get your fill of canine goodness from just one day, you can volunteer with the organization at adoption events on other Saturdays as well. Volunteers will sit and walk with dogs in need of homes, showing off the furry bundles of joy to potential human families. If you are interested, contact hartjacob@hotmail.com.

Every Saturday - Food for All delivers food directly to clients in need.  Meet in the morning to pack up the food, then work in teams of two to deliver throughout DC.  A car is not necessary, but if you have one, it is helpful if you are able to bring it.  Contact melatulippe@yahoo.com if you are interested.
