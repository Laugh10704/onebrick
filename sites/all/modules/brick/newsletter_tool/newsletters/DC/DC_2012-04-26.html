One Brick needs your help this Saturday!

Are looking for a fun, exciting event that promises to entertain, as well as help One Brick raise money this Saturday?

 
 
One Brick will be assisting at CitySolve this Saturday during 2 shifts: 9:15am-1:30pm, and 12:45pm-5pm.  We would love to have you volunteer with us at this awesome event!

What is CitySolve?  Think scavenger hunt plus Double Dare physical challenge!  We will be helping CitySolve with organization and logistics the day of the event.  We will be focused on helping run the race, including keeping times and ensuring clue completion.  CitySolve expects 150-200 teams of racers (with 2-6 racers per team) to solve clues and face fun challenges all while navigating Washington DC by foot and public transit! 

Join your fellow One Brick volunteers--RSVP today for the morning shift or afternoon shift!

 

Are you interested in attending CitySolve, but would rather compete for awesome CASH prizes instead of volunteering with One Brick along the course?

You can still help One Brick!  As a part of the partnership, CitySolve will provide a 60% discount on your ticket plus donate $5 for every race participant who signs up with the coupon code OneBrickDC.

Cost: $25 per ticket (after One Brick discount of over 50%!)
Tickets must be prepaid.  You can register to become a participant here: Register for the 2012 Washington DC CitySolve! 


 

Mark your calendars for May 19 and 20!



One Brick will be partnering with the Warrior Dash on May 19 (Saturday) and 20 (Sunday) in Mechanicsville, Maryland.  With your assistance, we will be able to raise over $4,000 for One Brick in just one weekend.  Join us for mud, beer, live music and turkey legs!

Carpool arrangements will be made from METRO stations to the event.  Parking is free for volunteers at Budd's Creek!
