One Brick Needs YOUR Help This Weekend!


One Brick needs your assistance at the Warrior Dash!  One Brick will be at the 2012 Maryland Warrior Dash on May 19 and 20 from 1pm to 6:30pm. We will be helping to run the event; for example, you may be a super excitable volunteer who will pump up the participants, give high fives, hugs, and hand out medals.

We need 50 volunteers each day.  In exchange for our help, Warrior Dash will donate $4,250 to One Brick.


As a thank you to our volunteers, we will be raffling off a brand new Kindle Fire!  For each Warrior Dash shift you attend, you will receive an entry into our raffle.  If you sign up to assist on both days, you'll have 2 chances to win!  The raffle will be held at our June Happy Hour (you do not need to be present at the raffle to win).

So what are you waiting for?!
Sign up to help on May 19 or May 20!

Have questions about helping at the Warrior Dash?  Contact Liz at lizw@onebrick.org for more information, including carpooling and gas reimbursement.


What is the Warrior Dash?

The Warrior Dash is a mud-crawling, fire-leaping, extreme 5k run from hell.  Warriors conquer extreme obstacles, push their limits and celebrate with music, beer and Warrior helmets.

 

In addition to supporting One Brick, the Warrior Dash is a partner with St. Jude Children's Research Hospital.
