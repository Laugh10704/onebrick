Thank You US Open Volunteers!

Think of your favorite soda, beer, or sandwich. Now think of how many sodas, beers, and sandwiches we sold at the US Open - all those golf spectators we made happy, all that hard work we put in, and all that money we raised to continue One Brick's efforts to make a difference in our community!

While you're still thinking of sodas, beer, and sandwiches, make sure your calendar is marked for tomorrow's private party at Local 16 in the U Street corridor. Anyone and everyone is welcome to come. As a special thank you to US Open volunteers, everyone who spent a shift at the US Open will receive two free Happy Hour drinks. And don't forget, all US Open volunteers will be put in a random drawing; one lucky winner will take home a brand new iPad! US Open volunteers are also eligible to win free One Brick swag. (You have to attend the party to win the swag; you don't have to attend to win the iPad.)

RSVPs are most appreciated so that Local 16 knows just how big a party to expect! RSVP here.

Team Member of the Month: SharvariC

SharvariC's One Brick journey began three years ago, when she got lost on her way to her first event. (We've all been there!) Her favorite event was Sweet Charity, the chocolate fashion show, because "it was a big event where you got dressed up and got to sample chocolate!"

In addition to coordinating events, Sharvari works hard as a member of the Training and Recruitment Committee, "so you may see me at one of the Recruitment Brunches!"

When not volunteering, Sharvari reads, cooks, and runs. She enjoys the DC area because there's so much to do and "never an opportunity to be bored." Her theme song is "Twist and Shout."

Beyond One Brick

Even more opportunities to volunteer! Be sure to mention that you heard about these events from One Brick.
 
Saturday, July 16 - Dreams for Kids' Extreme Recess is back again this year! Help kids with disabilities enjoy adaptive water skiing at Pohick Bay in Lorton, Virginia. Please note that volunteers will need to bring their own equipment; however, food and beverage will be provided. To sign up, visit http://extremerecessh20skiing.eventbrite.com/.

Every Saturday - If you need more dogs in your life, come volunteer with HART! One Brick volunteers with the organization regularly at adoption events, but if you can't get your fill of canine goodness from just one day, you can volunteer with the organization at adoption events on other Saturdays as well. Volunteers will sit and walk with dogs in need of homes, showing off the furry bundles of joy to potential human families. If you are interested, contact hartjacob@hotmail.com.

Every Saturday - Food for All delivers food directly to clients in need.  Meet in the morning to pack up the food, then work in teams of two to deliver throughout DC.  A car is not necessary, but if you have one, it is helpful if you are able to bring it.  Contact melatulippe@yahoo.com if you are interested.

Become a One Brick Event Manager!

Our volunteer Event Managers are the staff who make One Brick events happen! Anyone can join our team - all we ask is that you attend an Orientation Brunch to learn how One Brick works and then we'll discuss how you can help out. For more information, email us at dc-team@onebrick.org.
