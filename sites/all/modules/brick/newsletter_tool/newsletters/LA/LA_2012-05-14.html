News and Announcements

Presidential Awards
Congratulations to Meng, Mary and Bill for receiving the President's Volunteer Service Award. The three founders of One Brick's Los Angeles chapter were sent letters from the White House recognizing their service to the community. The President’s Volunteer Service Award recognizes individuals, families, and groups that have achieved a certain standard – measured by the number of hours of service.

Event Recaps

Humanity, Gratitude and Celebrities!

One BrickLA had a busy spring with several events, including the Miles for Melanoma 5k walk, which drew celebrities who joined us!

One BrickLA made history, sort of, by joining Operation Gratitude on the march to one million individual packages to the U.S. troops based all over the world. The group expects to send package one million sometime later this year or early 2013.


We also helped a local family make a little history of their own, by preparing to move into their own home for the very first time.
