Last chance to RSVP!


Summer’s over…but that doesn't mean you have to bid your swimsuit body goodbye! Welcome Fall with confidence by taking on a healthy challenge: The Phelps Conditioning Class in Prospect Park.  Fight the urge to hibernate! Join other socials in NY to burn calories and push your body to the limit, maintaining your warm weather physique and feeling great doing it with new-found friends. This hour-long class will keep you invigorated and ready to take on any task.

Dillen’s back - and he’s ready to whip you into shape. 
 
As a Marine Corps Martial Arts Instructor, Dillen has led marines through many combat conditioning courses. Dillen is offering a class using the same principles that he has taught in the Marine Corps to anyone who wants to get in better shape and improve their overall fitness level.
 
Event Cost: (a portion of the proceeds will go to supporting our One Brick volunteer organization. )
·         $10 per person if you RSVP by 11:59pm on Friday, October 14th.  
·         $15 per person on the day of the event.

RSVP here: http://newyork.onebrick.org/event/?eventid=10133
