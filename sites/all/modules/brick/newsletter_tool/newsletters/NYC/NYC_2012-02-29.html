News and Announcements

We need your help.... Can you spare $2?

No, we're not stranded overseas. No, you don't have to send it Western Union.  We can give you the long drawn-out explanation, but hey...your time's valuable, and we're only talking about $2.  If you can spare it without worry, you can save time by just donating it here. 

And hey, we’ll even add some entertainment value for your contribution.  When you visit the donation page we've added a video of a bird feeding noodles to a dog. Nothing to do with One Brick. But really sweet.

Ok, a bit more detail about why we’re asking for this help.  One Brick is a very unique organization.  We deliver a ton of value to the communities we serve (we're in 12 chapters now!). At the same time, we deliver huge value to the volunteers who participate in our events.  Countless people have found friends, roommates and spouses through the organization.  And we’ve completely lost count of the ‘little bricks’ running around as a result of all these introductions. 

While we do this on a shoestring budget, we do have expenses and a single full-time employee.  Unfortunately, a foundation that’s been a significant long-term supporter went belly up at the end of 2011 and defaulted on a pledged donation, leaving us with a significant hole in our budget as we entered 2012. So here we are in a significant deficit and need your help. 

We're emailing our 40,000 volunteers.  If everyone responds and can spare a couple of dollars, we're in the clear. We know response rates won't be 100%, so if you can spare an extra dollar or two, or share this with your friends, family and social network, that'll help compensate for non-responders. 

One last thing. While we are in a short-term bind I want to reassure you we are working hard to find new and innovative ways to sew up the hole in our finances. Each chapter is working to find for-profit community partners, the board is reviewing ways to produce larger fundraising events (anybody got experience that can help here?) , and we are trimming the budget even further to make sure we can keep One Brick going.

Thank you in advance

Dave Shefferman,

One Brick Founder.

The Return of Martha's Kitchen!
Those of you that have been volunteering with One Brick for a while know that the Village Church has been one of our most beloved partner organizations.  For the past couple years, they've gone through a major kitchen renovation and are now ready to have us back helping with their Saturday feeding program!

We're excited to see what the new space looks like and excited to see some old friends/patrons of the soup kitchen.  

This event fills up fast so please visit our calendar and sign up for this Saturday!  We hope to be adding more dates for you to volunteer at Martha's Kitchen.
