Help make volunteering easy!

Are you interested in being more involved with One Brick?

Do you want to develop your skills, build your resume, and contribute your abilities to One Brick? Would you like to get to know other One Brickers who are working to make all our volunteer opportunities happen?

One Brick - NY invites you to join our wonderful leadership team!

We are looking for dedicated volunteers to become Event Managers/Coordinators and to help with the various committes (Events, Fundraising/Socials, Newsletter, Marketing).
