Help out ESS

Episcopal Social Services (ESS) is a child and family social services agency that positively impacts the lives of over 5,000 New York City’s most vulnerable residents.  Next Friday, June 22, ESS/Sheltering Arms is hosting an indoor Carnival for over 200 Foster Care children where they can enjoy an evening of fun, games, music, and dancing. They are looking for volunteers who can help decorate the event space to create a festive environment on Friday, June 22nd from 3:00 – 5:00PM in Midtown Manhattan at 210 West 31st Street, Ground Floor (entrance is on 30th between 7th & 8th Avenue). Although One Brick will not be "managing" this event, it would be great if you could help out. If you are available and interested, please contact Daniela Ramos, Special Projects Coordinator, at ramosd@e-s-s.org.
