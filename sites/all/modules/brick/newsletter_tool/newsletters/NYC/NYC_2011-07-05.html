Calling for volunteers in Brooklyn!

Brooklyn Bridge Park needs our help!

To better plan for programs in the future, they need to get an idea of how many people are using the park on a given weekend.

Here's where we come in... We will be helping them count the people who use the park this weekend. We are covering 3 shifts over Saturday and Sunday so we need all the people we can get.
