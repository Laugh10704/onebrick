Coney Island Spring Gala

Come support our partner, Coney Island USA, by volunteering for their
Spring Fundraising Gala in preparation for the 30th anniversary of the
Mermaid Parade.  Not only will we be there to make it the most
memorable gala they've ever had, there will be tons of entertainment
during the event executed by the cast of Sideshows by the Seashore,
some of New York’s most talented and titillating burlesque performers,
a glorious gamut of go go gals and guys, as well as special musical
guests for your auditory pleasure.

RSVP now by clicking here http://newyork.onebrick.org/event/?eventid=11110

Please note that you must be 21 and over to volunteer at this event.
