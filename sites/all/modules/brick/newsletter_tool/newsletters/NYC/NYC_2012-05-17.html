Upcoming Events

June is heating up!

The warmer weather is finally here and One Brick has plenty of outdoor events coming up to help you enjoy it! For our Bronx volunteers (and the rest of you too, of course), we have the Summer Walks on June 9th & 16th. For our friends in Brooklyn, we have several events including the Puppetry Arts Festival on the 2nd, the Renegade Craft Fair on trhe 23rd & 24th, and, of course, the ever popular (and always outrageous) Coney Island Mermaid Parade. If you've never experienced this unique event, you owe it to yourself to go! Just click on the links to the right to check out these and the other great events on our calendar.

For those of you who signed up for the Men in Aprons event next week, please be advised that, unfortunately, it has been postponed til the fall, so please keep an eye on the calendar for the reschedule.

Event Recaps

One Brick is totally STOKED!

Last night, a festive group of One Brick volunteers were at the Stoked on Spring fundraiser at the amazing Scholastic Building Greenhouse in Soho, greeting attendees, gathering sponsorship info, helping with the art auction and enjoying the incredible views! This was our first time working with Stoked, a non-profit youth development organization that provides enrichment programs based in skateboarding, snowboarding, and surfing culture. Their people were great to us and we hope to be able to work with them again in the future.
