One Brick Date Auction
Who says February is the only “Month of Love?”

Whether you’re looking for “The One”, a fleeting romance, or if you’d just like to meet NEW FRIENDS…come out and join us on November 9th. 

It’s a Date Auction + Happy Hour + Fundraiser + One Brick Social = All in One! 

HAPPY HOUR drink specials are from 6:30 - 9:00pm.
DATE AUCTION starts at 7:30pm.

Cover Fee: $1, or bring a canned food item to donate to the food bank.

How it Works: Each volunteer date comes with an actual date (i.e. movie tickets, dinner for two, theater tickets, etc). Biddings will start at $10, depending on the date activity.

Proceeds to benefit One Brick & Food Bank For New York City.

www.onebrick.org
www.foodbanknyc.org
www.jackdempseys.com


Please note: One Brick does not claim to be a dating service. The auction is all in good fun and in support of raising funds for One Brick and The Food Bank For New York City.

The One Brick NY chapter holds monthly socials to encourage a sense of community and friendship amongst our volunteers. Social events are NOT volunteer events, but it’s a great time to catch up with old friends and meet new people. If you haven’t volunteered with One Brick yet, but would like to know more about us – this is a great opportunity for you! Check us out atwww.onebrick.org.

Volunteer Dates Needed!
Think you would make a great date and want to contribute to a great cause?

Support One Brick and the Food Bank For New York City by being a volunteer date for our upcoming auction!

What does that entail?

Step 1: Plan an amazing date (movies, dinner, salsa lessions, walk in the park, etc).

Step 2: Attend the date auction in your most dashing attire.

Step 3: Put on a smile and wait for the bids to come.

If you're interested in being auctioned off, please email kayl@onebrick.org with a photo, a few sentences about yourself, and a short description of the date you have planned by 5pm on Tuesday Oct 25th.

Please note: All components of the date will be provided and paid for by the volunteer date.  We can provide you a tax deduction receipt for your donation.
