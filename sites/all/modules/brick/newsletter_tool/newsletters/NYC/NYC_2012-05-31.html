Upcoming Events

If April showers bring May flowers and May flowers bring Pilgrims, what does June bring? Lots of great events on the One Brick calendar, of course!

Great Strides Walk

This Sunday, 6/3, we are helping out at the Great Strides: Taking Steps to Cure Cystic Fibrosis Walk in Brooklyn. This is the largest fundraising event for the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation so we need lots of volunteers to run the event. So please come on out and ask all your friends, family, co-workers, etc. We really need a big turnout for this one!



Fun in the Bronx!

One Brick is teaming up with WHEDco to run the Summer Walks events. This program is an attempt to give the underprivileged to coonect with the retailers and services in their community. We will be there on Saturday 6/9 and Saturday 6/16.

Other News

Help out LLS
One of our longtime non-profit partners, the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society, has asked us to send a note about a couple of events they have coming up in June. Through their Team in Training Program, they fund research and raise awareness for blood cancers.
On Saturday, 6/9, several of thier runners/walkers will be participating in the NYRRC Women's Mini 10K and they are looking for volunteers to man (or woman) their Cheer Zones and Water Station. The race starts at 8:00 AM so volunteers would need to arrive before then. The shift should end around 11:00 AM. And it includes a TNT t-shirt!
And then on Tuesday, 6/12, they are having their Fall Season Kick Off to introduce the program to new recruits at the NYC Museum School - 333 West 17th Street between 8th and 9th Ave. The event will run from around 5:30-8:00 PM.
Although One Brick will not be "running" these events, LLS is a great organization to work with and you're sure to have a worthwhile experience. For full details or to sign up for either (or both!) event, please contact TNT Recruitment Coordinator Abby Baram at abigail.baram@lls.org and give her your name and contact info.
