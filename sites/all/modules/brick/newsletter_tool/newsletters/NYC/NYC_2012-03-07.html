March Madness
Thank you to everyone who has supported One Brick by donating funds to our cause!  As of today, volunteers from One Brick all over the nation have raised over $15,000!!!  Please consider contributing $2 and help us reach our goal of $40,000 here.

We've got a jam-packed month of March for you with lots of exciting events!  The team in NYC has been working hard to increase the number of events available on our calendar and the work has paid off!  Join us as we help out some old favorites like AIDSwalk, Earth Celebrations, Coney Island USA, Meals-on-Heels, Dress for Success and Fourth Arts Blcok this month!

Upcoming Events

Coney Island Spring Gala
Coney Island USA is having their annual Spring Gala.  This year is extra special as it is the 30th Anniversary of the Mermaid Parade.  The main focus of this event is raising funds so that Coney Island USA can have the most memorable parade ever.  All funds raised will help out with all the other events throughout the year too.

One Brick will be supplying all of the volunteers for the gala on March 24th.  Please RSVP here for a night of fun and celebration for Coney Island!
