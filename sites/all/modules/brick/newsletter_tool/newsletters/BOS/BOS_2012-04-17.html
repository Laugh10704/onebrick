News and Announcements

Happy Volunteer Appreciation Week!

Please join us tomorrow night, April 18th at Julep Bar for our volunteer appreciation social!  As a way of showing our gratitude to you, One Brick Boston is thanking you for volunteering with us with free appetizers!  Everything starts at 6pm. We can't wait to see you there!   

Click here for more details on tomorrow's social and to RSVP!  

This is a special occasion to celebrate the most important part of our unique organization, our volunteers!  Don't miss out!  

Upcoming Volunteer Events - Help out with great causes!

Please check out our calendar of upcoming events here. We have two big outdoor events coming up soon!  There's no better way to get outside, help make a difference, and have fun! 

Help out this coming Sunday, April 22nd at the Boston Area Rape Crisis Center's (BARCC) 7th Annual Walk For Change!  Play a role in this energetic and inspirational event to raise funds and awareness to prevent sexual violence and support survivors of sexual abuse. Click here to RSVP!

Plus, join us on April 28th at Strides Against Stigma on April 28th. This, unique, one-of-a-kind "stride-a-thon"  seeks to assist in raising awareness of depressive disorders, and promotes erasing the stigma around depression. Help out with various tasks at the event which will include activities, performances, and games.  Volunteer for the morning, afternoon, or both shifts! (click here for more details)

Details on all our open volunteer events with can be found on the right.. Please RSVP as our events are filling up fast!

Want to get more involved?

Do you feel like getting more involved with One Brick Boston?  Been to events and like what you've seen? One Brick Boston is recruiting new event managers.

Our event managers are at the heart of One Brick Boston's unique approach to making volunteering exciting and fun. If you are interested in finding out more about becoming a One Brick Boston event manager, please contact Yanina, our chapter director, today at yanina@onebrick.org!
 
See you all tomorrow night! 

Until next time,
 
Yanina, Graham, Melissa, Lori, Nikki, and Roy
