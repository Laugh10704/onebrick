News and Announcements

June Volunteering fun from One Brick Boston!

Hi One Brick Boston Volunteers and Supporters!

Hope you all had a wonderful and fun long weekend!  Make next weekend even better by volunteering with us and helping with great causes! 

Check out our calendar of upcoming events here. Now is the perfect time to get involved with us! 

Join us this Sunday, June 3rd at AIDS Walk Boston!  Don't pass on helping out with New England's largest community fundraiser to support the fight against HIV/AIDS. One Brick Boston volunteers will be course marshals to guide thousands of dedicated volunteers committed to the fight of HIV/AIDS. (Click here to RSVP!)

Also, this Saturday, assist the Cambridge Arts Council with putting on the Cambridge River Festival!  This annual festival is a free one-day celebration of the arts along the banks of the Charles River.  One Brick Boston volunteers will be there to help ensure the festival runs smoothly.  The festival features jazz, folk, Latin and world music performances, dance, art demonstrations, family art-making activities, and speciality food vendors!  Sign up today! (Click here to RSVP)

All the details on all our open volunteer events can be found on the right.. Make sure you RSVP as our events are filling up quickly!  And we're not done yet, we're hard at work putting together even more awesome volunteering opportunites for you this summer! Stay tuned!  

Join the One Brick Boston Team as an Event Manager! 

One Brick Boston is looking for new event managers to join our fun team!  Our event managers are at the center of our unique approach to making volunteering fun and easy. The more event managers we have, the more volunteer events we can put on for you!

If you are interested in finding out more about becoming a One Brick Boston event manager, please contact Yanina, our chapter director at yanina@onebrick.org!

Stay tuned for event recaps, and exciting news about upcoming volunteer and social events! 

Our volunteers are the best!   

Until next time,
 
Yanina, Graham, Melissa, Nikki, Lori, and Roy
