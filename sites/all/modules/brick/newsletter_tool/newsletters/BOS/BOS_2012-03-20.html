Event Recaps

The Taste of the South End

On March 6th, One Brick Volunteers and Executive Team Members joined force with other volunteers to assist the AIDS Action Committee of Massachusetts at the 17th Annual Taste of the South End at the BCA Cyclorama in Boston's South End. 


 
The sold-out event brought together delicious offerings from over 40 of the South End's finest restaraunts in support of New England's largest HIV/AIDS service organization. The event was a blast and all of us had a ton of fun while helping out with a great cause!  One Brick Boston Volunteers assisted with a variety of tasks including setup, registration, busing and cleanup, and other important logistics which helped to make the event a rousing success! 
This year's event raised more funds for the AIDS Action Committee than ever before!  And we all got to sample some of the amazing food during our breaks!  One Brick Boston looks forward to working with the AIDS Action Committee again in the future!
