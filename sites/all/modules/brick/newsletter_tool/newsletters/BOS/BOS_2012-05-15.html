Upcoming Volunteer Events from One Brick Boston!

Hi One Brick Boston Volunteers and Supporters!

Have a blast and help make a difference by volunteering with One Brick Boston! 

Check out our calendar of upcoming events here. This is the perfect time to get involved!

Head up to Peabody this Saturday, May 19th and spend a day helping out Habitat for Humanity of the North Shore in rehabilitating a house.  Your contribution will help create an affordable, inviting home for an area family in need! Make sure to wear comfortable clothes and shoes!  (Click here to RSVP!)

Plus, join us on June 3rd at AIDS Walk Boston! Help out at New England's largest community fundraiser to support the fight against HIV/AIDS. One Brick Boston volunteers will be course marshals to guide thousands of volunteers doing their part to fight HIV/AIDS.  Don't miss out! (Click here to RSVP!)

All the details on all our open volunteer events can be found on the right.. Make sure you RSVP today as our  events are filling up fast!  We're hard at work putting together even more awesome volunteering opportunites for this summer! Stay tuned!  

Become an Event Manager and Join the One Brick Boston Team!

One Brick Boston continues to look for new event managers to join our dedicated team!  Our event managers are the key of our unique approach to making volunteering fun and easy. The more event managers we have, the more volunteer events we can put on for you!

If you are interested in finding out more about becoming a One Brick Boston event manager, please contact Yanina, our chapter director at yanina@onebrick.org!

Event Recap: Boston Medical Center Gala

On May 5th, One Brick Boston volunteers helped out at the Boston Medical Center Gala at the Seaport World Trade Center.  This annual event is a important fundraiser for Boston's most important community medical center.  The BMC plays a significant role in ensuring a safety net for Boston residents.
