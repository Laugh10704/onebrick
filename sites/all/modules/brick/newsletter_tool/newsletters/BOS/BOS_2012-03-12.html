Thanks so much for those who attended our official launch party on February 29th! We had a blast, gave away some amazing prizes (bonus points for people to send in photos with their prizes!) and it was a special moment for all of us at One Brick Boston.
 
Want to get involved with the One Brick Team?

Have you been to a few of our events? Like what you have seen?  Want to lead them? We are actively looking for new event managers. Our event managers are a critical part of One Brick Boston's unique approach to making volunteering fun and exciting. Plus, the more fun, outgoing event managers we have, the more events we can put on every month.

If you are interested in finding out more about becoming a One Brick Boston event manager, email our chapter director, Yanina, at yanina@onebrick.org with “Event Manager” in the subject. Let her know why you’d make a great event manager, and which One Brick event is your favorite!
