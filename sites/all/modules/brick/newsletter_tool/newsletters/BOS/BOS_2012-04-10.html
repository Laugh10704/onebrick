Event Recaps

More Than Words

On March 30th, One Brick Boston volunteers kicked off their weekend at More Than Words. After a tour of the store, located on busy and fun Moody Street in Waltham, One Brickers began sorting and scanning hundreds of donated books, searching for the elusive titles that would fetch a profit either online or in the store. 
 
More Than Words is a non-profit social enterprise that empowers youth who are in the foster care system, court involved, homeless or high school dropouts to take charge of their lives. The More Than Words model uses a two-fold approach to help youth team members create positive personal change. Through a business job, teens learn valuable business and leadership skills such as professionalism, customer service, inventory management, and teamwork. Through the experience, the youth address personal barriers that will bring them closer to achieving their education and employment goals.


 
If you are ever passing through Waltham, be sure to check out the More than Words store. In addition to supporting an awesome social entperprise that helps empower at-risk teens, you can enjoy a cup of Starbucks coffee and free wi-fi! Support More Than Words by donating your books, purchasing used books from the store and more!  One Brick Boston looks forward to working with More Than Words in the future.
