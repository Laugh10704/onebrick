April's Full of Volunteering with One Brick Boston

Hi One Brick Boston Volunteers and Supporters!

We're pumped because we have a ton of volunteering fun available for our volunteers as we jump our way into April!  This is your opportunity to volunteer for great causes and get more involved with us!

Check out our event calendar here!.  Some of our upcoming highlights include: 

Work side-by-side with and help empower at-risk youth to take charge of their lives at a nonprofit used bookstore and coffee shop at More Than Words (click here for more details)

And Make sure to volunteer at Strides Against Stigma on April 28th at Boston University! It is a unique "stride-a-thon" to raise awareness of depressive disorders, and promote erasing the stigma around depression. Help out with various tasks to operate this exciting event which will include activities, performances, and games. (click here for more details)

 Details on all our events can be found on the right.. Don't miss out! RSVP today! 
 
Become an Event Manager and Get More Involved with One Brick Boston! 

Do you wish you could be more involved with One Brick Boston?  Been to our events and like what you've seen? We are actively looking for new event managers. Our event managers are at the heart of One Brick Boston's unique approach to making volunteering fun and exciting. The more fun, outgoing event managers we have, the more events we can put on every month for our dedicated volunteers.

If you are interested in potentially becoming a One Brick Boston event manager, please contact our chapter director, Yanina, at yanina@onebrick.org with “Event Manager” in the subject. Please let Yanina know why you will be a great event manager, and which One Brick event is your favorite!
