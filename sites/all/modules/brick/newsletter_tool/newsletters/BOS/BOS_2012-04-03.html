Event Recaps

Event Recap: Men Take A Stand

On March 21st, One Brick Boston volunteers had a chance to assist the Boston Women's Fund in a special event to honor three community leaders who have worked tirelessly to promote social justice, gender equality, and leadership amongst women and girls.  The event took place at Space With a Soul in the Fort Point area of the South Boston Waterfront.


The Boston Women's fund is an organization with the sole purpose of supporting grassroots organizations run by and for women and girls with the goal of creating a society based on racial, economic, and social justice. 
 
One Brick Boston volunteers assisted with preparation and setup, greeting of guests, registration, and other tasks which helped make the event a rousing success. Awards were presented to Jarrett Barrios, CEO of the Red Cross of Eastern Massachusetts and former State Senator, Paul Marcus, Executive Director of Community Change, Inc, and David Moy, Program Officer at the Hyams Foundation.  
 
Volunteers had the opportunity to enjoy the event and network with social-justice minded people, and enjoy compelling artistic performances from Project Hip Hop, Acting Out and the Batucada Belles, amongst others.  One Brick Boston looks forward to working with the Boston Women's Fund in the future.
