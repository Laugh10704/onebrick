Event Recaps

Event Recap: AIDS Walk Boston

On June 3rd, One Brick Boston Volunteers had the opportunity to assist the AIDS Action Commitee of Massachusetts at the annual Boston AIDS Walk and 5K Run on the Esplanade along the Charles River.  AIDS Walk Boston is New England's largest community fundraiser to help support the fight against HIV/AIDS. 


Despite a gloomy start, the event went off without a hitch and was a blast for all. One Brick Boston volunteers helped out as course marshals and made sure to keep all the dedicated AIDS Walk walkers & runners safe and heading in the right direction. One Brick Boston looks forward to the opportunity to help out the AIDS Action Committee of Massachusetts again in the future.
